## Sinopse

In Teisei Accademy, club activities are just about the most important thing. The principal specifically said at their orientation, "We are all "youth loving idiots" and that all teachers and students must participate in a club academy". The main charater, a NEET and a Netoge lover, decides to join only becasue he got a free scholorship. After realizing his mistakes, he bands together with other odities to form the "go home club", whose primary goal is to excape from school grounds before getting caught by the other clubs.
