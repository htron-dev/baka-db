## Sinopse

After being heartbroken by his crush from college, Hozumi ventures into the world of homosexuality. After frequenting a certain gay bar, he becomes friends with the master there. Tired of living in his apartment with the noisy children above him, Hozumi has some luck and the Master of the bar offers to let him live in his second home. However, Hozumi doesn't find out until after he moves that he will have a roommate named Asahi. Can these two new roommates overcome their differences and become friends? Maybe even something more?

(Source: Exiled Rebels Scans)
