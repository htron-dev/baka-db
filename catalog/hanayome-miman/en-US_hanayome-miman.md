## Sinopse

Ako is an office lady who works in Tokyo and she's soon to be married. Her fiance's father passes away and she decides to attend the funeral by herself, so off she goes to his hometown, which is on the in-land island of Shiojima. What she doesn't realize though is that behind her back her fiance's family is trying to prevent her from going back to Tokyo for some reason...

(Source: Clairparfait)
