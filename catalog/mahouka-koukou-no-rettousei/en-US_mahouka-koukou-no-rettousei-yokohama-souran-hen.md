## Sinopse

Manga adaptation of the 3rd arc of the Mahouka Koukou no Rettousei series.

The Thesis Competition, an event with as much importance as the Nine School Competition. This competition has the particularity of having its competitors compete to see which one has more mastery on magic theory. However, things may not go smoothly... And "disturbance" may unfold...
