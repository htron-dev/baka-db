## Sinopse

Promotional manga based on the hit Phoenix Wright videogame series by Capcom.

This fifty page manga was released along with a limited edition version of GS:YG (PW:AA to English-speaking countries) in Japan. It features two sections, one focusing on Phoenix and one on Edgeworth, as they meet at a television studio.

(Source: MU)
