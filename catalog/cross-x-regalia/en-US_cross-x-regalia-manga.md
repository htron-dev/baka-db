## Sinopse

On the corner of Chinatown, the two of them crossed, The boy name is Inumi Haserou, high-school bodyguard who takes 1000 yen for each trouble; the girl name is Nata, self-styled Sage who mysteriously collapsed in the back alley. Deciding to protect Nata, Haserou would have to face the sudden change in his daily peaceful lives, as a group of Vampiric being who consumed people Ki (energy), called "Demonic Sage" starts to attack them. Their purpose: to hunt Nata, who holds the key as the powerful weapons capable of turning them powerless!
