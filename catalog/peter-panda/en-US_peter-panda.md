## Sinopse

Rui, despite being an orphan, lives life to the fullest. One day, when her neighbors leave a stuffed panda behind, she takes the opportunity to wash him and adopt him as her own. The next day, however, the toy panda turns into a human who looks exactly like Rui's ideal Prince Charming!
