## Sinopse

Shinobu Satou babysits during the day and works at an izakaya at night. Everything was normal until he found a child, Shion, abandoned in the rain, near his home. Unable to leave him in that place Satou decides to take him to his home... but suddenly Shion's eyes turn red?!
