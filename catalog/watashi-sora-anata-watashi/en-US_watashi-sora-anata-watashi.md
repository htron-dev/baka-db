## Sinopse

The manga's story centers on the Adachi family: the mother Kurumi, who runs a beauty salon at her home with her two daughters Ringo and Anzu. The family welcomes two new people to their family. The first is Remon Suzuki, the daughter of Kurumi's ex-husband by a different woman. Left with no other relatives, Remon is welcomed by the Adachi family. The second is Izumi, a former salaryman who seeks employment at the salon.

(Source: ANN)
