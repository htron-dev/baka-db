## Sinopse

It has been years since the end of bio-war, and the society is still struggling in the aftermath. While the upper class is attempting to maintain its humanity with genetic manipulation, the rest of the people are starting to be affected by their non-human genes.

This is a tale, about people living in this post-apocalyptic society.
