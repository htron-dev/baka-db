## Sinopse

An "episode 0" streamed before the main series' release. The protagonist Eiji assembles "cunninglasses" so he can read his classmate's note about the boy she likes. In the end, he finds the last piece needed to make the glasses work, and reads her note that says "Eiji is pretty weird."

(Source: ANN)
