## Sinopse

Ichito, a high school teacher, is supposed to be living together with his lover... but he walks in on his boyfriend having an affair! Suddenly, the mysterious man who helped him to his apartment is furious. In fact, the man is Ryuugasaki, the other guy's boyfriend! The stunned Ichito is pushed down by Ryuugasaki who says he must "take responsibility" and then he takes his virginity!!
