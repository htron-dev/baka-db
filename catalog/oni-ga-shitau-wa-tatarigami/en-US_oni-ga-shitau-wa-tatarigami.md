## Sinopse

Tsubakimaru is a young half-oni that has been captured by bandits and taken through a strange mountain where no plants or animals live. Soon a vengeful God named Shii saves Tsubakimaru from his captors. Tsubakimaru finds that his saver is very kind and lonely is determined to stay by Shii's side forever.
