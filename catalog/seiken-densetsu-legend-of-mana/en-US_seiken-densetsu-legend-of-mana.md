## Sinopse

Retelling the story of the game, Legend of Mana centers around Toto, who has to save the mana tree from withering completely. But is the chaotic and egoistic swordsman able to find his way to the tree? Or will he be sidetracked by a his figurine collection or a princess? Luckily his friends can bring him back on course...
