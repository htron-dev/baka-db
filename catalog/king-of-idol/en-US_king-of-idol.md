## Sinopse

Mahoro Harukana moves to Tokyo to achieve the dream of becoming an idol. In order to do this, Mahoro will attend to the National Entertainment and Music School in Kamisaka. Everything goes surprisingly well, being great at singing and dancing, and even her teammates are astonished by her talent.

The only problem is that... Mahoro is actually a boy, and a boy who wants to become The King of the Idols!
