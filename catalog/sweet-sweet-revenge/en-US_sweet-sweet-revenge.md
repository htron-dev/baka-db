## Sinopse

After five years of leave, Hikari Minamoto returns to a prestigious school that lasts from kindergarten to university she used to attend. Why? To get revenge on the five boys who used to bully her because she wasn't pretty. In order to do so, Hikari made a total makeover and mastered ninja skills to set traps.
