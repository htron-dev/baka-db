## Sinopse

A young man, Yuusuke Sakanoi, who is making a living as a freelancer, is helped by a mysterious beautiful woman when he's about to faint due to heat stroke one day. Yuusuke looks for her and managed to find her, but she's in a soapland!? Her name is Ginka, she is a novice yuki-onna who came to the city in search of spirit and her husband, and the soapland was the feeding ground for the yuki-onna!! What's more, Ginka and Yuusuke will become a temporary couple...!!? What will be the fate of Yuusuke!? Frozen with a little ecchi, newlywed love comedy ♪

(Source: Takeshobo, translated)
