## Sinopse

Komatsu Akina is a passionate photographer for her school's newspaper club. When Aoi-kun of the Student Council confesses to her, all of a sudden she has this huge decision to make. Since she has never dated before, Komatsu is confused as to whether or not she should date the extremely popular Aoi-kun, who is constantly surrounded by pretty girls. She continues to struggle with her own lack of confidence and her budding feelings for Aoi-kun, but decides that sometimes she just has to take a leap of faith and go for it!

(Source: Dazzling Scans)
