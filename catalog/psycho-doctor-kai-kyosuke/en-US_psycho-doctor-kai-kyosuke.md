## Sinopse

Kai Kyousuke is a talented psychologist and former assistant professor. His office may be a mess, but he is both skilled and dedicated to helping people who are in psychological pain. He and his assistant, Kanno Azusa, get to the bottom of each patient's case. His first case in this series is a woman whose physical pain may be a manifestation of her distrust for her lover...
