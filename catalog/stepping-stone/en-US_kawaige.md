## Sinopse

This volume is a spin-off from Stepping Stone.

Story 01 - Continuation of the relationship between Hirosue Kenji and Mogi Kousei who makes his first appearance in the fourth story of Stepping Stone.

Story 02 & Story 03 - Nakano Shunji finds himself getting involved with a younger fellow co-worker, Mejiro "Yasu" Yasushi, who harbours a dark secret. Hirosue and Mogi make appearances.

Story 04 - A love triangle of two high school boys and their teacher.

Story 05 - About Delicacy

Story 06 - The Best!

Story 07 - Daylight - A love triangle of two high school boys and their teacher.

Plus there is an Omake at the end. Please also note, Play Boy Blues, Gaten na aitsu, Kawaige, Stepping Stone, Punch Up! and Otonage. are loosely connected.
