## Sinopse

All three of Mao's highschool girlfriends have been taken away by the gorgeous-looking Yuu. But in retaliation to the slap that he gave this popular guy, a confused Mao gets a kiss..!? Soon he's dragged into the world of the school's "celebrities," who all have special talents, which range from "entertainment" to cosplay, and even pleasure!

What has Mao (unwillingly) gotten himself into?
