## Sinopse

Hotta couldn't find a proper job even after graduating college two years ago, so he's still working part time at the same convenience store, he also has no friends. One day, a new part timer joins, Ryouta Kashiwagi, a young and handsome college freshman. Ryouta is popular and promising, he's on a different level, that's why Hotta is surprised to hear his sudden confession.
