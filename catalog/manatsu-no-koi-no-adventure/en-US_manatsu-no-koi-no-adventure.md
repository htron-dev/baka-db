A furoku (free) comic volume included in an issue of Petit Comic magazine. Contains 6 short stories, including:

"Manatsu no Yoru wa Yume wo Miyou" by ANISAKI Yuna
"Nettai Suisou Shounen" by HASEBE Yuri
"Tengoku ni Ichiban Icchaisouna Paradise" by AYUKAWA Mio
"Lady Killer" by TOKIYAMA Hajime
"Otona no Ensoku ZZ" by KOIDE Mami
"Yuuwaku no Rakuen" by TODA Megumi
