In order to save the world, the female hero, Hizumi is about to engage in the final fight against the Demon Lord. However, he asks her to talk things out. Not trusting the words of the Demon Lord, Hizumi draws out her sword, but...? This is a forbidden romantic comedy between a hero and demon!
