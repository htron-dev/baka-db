## Sinopse

Photographer assistant Kurejima Eiji has just been dumped by his girlfriend when he meets high school student Hiroto Suginoi, who is cold and is trying to warm himself on the roof of Eiji's car. Eiji brings Hiroto back to his apartment, where he discovers that Hiroto is gay and also broken hearted. The two become friends, even though Hiroto wishes there could be more. After Hiroto realizes it is useless to continue to pursue Eiji, he decides to leave. Can Eiji convince Hiroto to stay?
