## Sinopse

The game centers around players traveling to different train stations in Japan and collecting the anthropomorphized representations of different stations and railway lines. The anime is set in Yokote City in Akita Prefecture, and will center around the characters Miroku, Mero, and Runa.

(Source: ANN)
