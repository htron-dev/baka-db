## Sinopse

North Korean leader Kim Jong-il is dead! A coup occurs within North Korea. USA has been waiting for this moment... It's time to get rid of little menace. South Korea gets entangled in this affair of course. Will Korea be united? Or is there more than what meets the eyes?

(Note : This webtoon was drawn when Kim Jong-Il was still alive.)
