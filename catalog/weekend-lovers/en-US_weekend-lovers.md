## Sinopse

Koutaro is a carefree young temp worker, while Asahi is seven years older and an elite salaryman. Due to Asahi's busy schedule, the two men can only be together for two weekends every month. One day, Koutarou accidentally breaks Asahi's glasses. For the first time, he gets the see Asahi looking vulnerable, and he realizes that the glasses are something his lover needs "so much that he can't live on normally without them"; now Koutarou wishes he could be that important to Asahi...
