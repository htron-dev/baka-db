## Sinopse

A story about two high school friends, Ohara and Kawai, who were also in the same astronomy club. They failed to convey their love properly during the teenage years and grew apart in spite of being neighbors due to busy daily lives, but now when Ohara is about to move away from the same neighborhood, what will Kawai do...?
