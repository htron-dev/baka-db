## Sinopse

Naruyuki Touya, young student, arrives at Amagiri Gakuen, his new high school. He finds himself on the quay of the station faced to a robot out of control and is attacked by a girl and then a classmate.

Who are they? How come the girl knows his father?

And what is the connection with him and this high school?
