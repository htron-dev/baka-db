## Sinopse

"One day, Sakuraki Ruu picks up a mysterious cat that can talk, use magic and hides many secrets. When asked, the cat seems to have lost its memories..."

The cat - and its twin - have come from another world, where their graduation exam is to go to the human world and grant a wish for a human being. And so, their fun-filled adventure with Ruu and her friends begins.
