## Sinopse

A story about a girl from Paris who went to Basel, Switzerland in order to reunite with her father, brother, and the guy she loves, Zerman. Waiting for Zerman to pick her up from the train station, she accidently hugs San, a handsome, wealthy, and ally to some of the few powerful men.
