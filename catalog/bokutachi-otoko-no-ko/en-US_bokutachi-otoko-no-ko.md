## Sinopse

Based on the drama CD series of the same name, Bokutachi Otoko no Ko follows three boys named Seri, Ren, and Tama who dream of becoming top idols. Little did they know they would come together under Barney's agency to form the cross-dressing idol unit, UNBALANCE. With the help of the Barney's agency president, Fujishiro Ryuichi, and their manager, Yuji Takigawa, they will become Japan's top cross-dressing idols!
