## Sinopse

An official Inazuma Eleven spin-off series—Ryouhei Haizaki's story starts here! Read about his life, from his first encounter with the genius playmaker Yuuto Kidou, to his daily shenanigans with his teammates—the other members of the Seishou Eleven (not to mention, guest appearances from popular characters of the Inazuma Eleven series). Chock full of scenes that are not shown in the anime, the magic of Inapen starts here...!
