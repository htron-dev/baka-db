## Sinopse

Two youths, Ray and Saki, are leaving the majestic european St.Hainheratsu Theological School for Japan - their task? To subdue the vampires that have spread across the world.

It has been a long time since Saki left his hometown, and he plans to enjoy seeing his family again. Rei, however, senses that danger is near. Saki has been too naive and now his family is a target. The vampires have set a cruel and terrifying trap...
