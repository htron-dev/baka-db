## Sinopse

Unattractive 40-year-old NEET Tagami on a certain night on Metropolitan expressway meets beautiful woman, Aiko-san. That unexpected meeting changes Tagami's life completely... By the request of Aiko's, Tagami transforms himself into "Suppleman," a lovely technician that heals women with just love.
