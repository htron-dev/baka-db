## Sinopse

Rei runs away from his uncle's home. During the night, he meets a mysterious man that promises him that they will meet again the next time there will be a full moon. However, the kid is taken by the police and sent to an orphanage asylum. Ten years later, he meets the mysterious man again.

(Source: Yaoi Otaku Scanlations)
