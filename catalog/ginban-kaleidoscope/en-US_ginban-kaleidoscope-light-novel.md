## Sinopse

Meet figure skater Sakurano Tazusa, 16 years old. Although blessed with beauty and talent, she is hated by those around her because of her high-handed nature. Unable to show her true strength during competition, her dreams of representing Japan at the Olympics are slipping away. Then one day, of all things, she is possessed by a ghost?!
