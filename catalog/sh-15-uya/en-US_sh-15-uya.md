## Sinopse

Tsuyoshi wakes up with no memories in an alley in a town called Shibuya - a world somewhat different from reality. There he meets Ema, the only person who knows him, and who possesses a mysterious power to oppose the world of Shibuya. The two 15-year-olds fight a desperate battle for survival in this virtual world!
