## Sinopse

Collection of one-shots:

-   Otoko Hiroimashita
    The owner of Boy's Club Garcon is closing up the store one night when he finds an injured young man on the street! His name is Kouta, and when he catches a glimpse of his handsome rescuer, it's love at first sight!

-   Ano Koro no Mama de Ite (Stay the Way You Were)
    A guy name Hikaru working in a gay club suddenly by accident met his senpai, Takano from high school. But it seems like Takano doesn't remember him. Meeting each other again in such a odd situation, although Takano doesnt remember Hikaru? What will become of them?!
