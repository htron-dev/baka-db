## Sinopse

Tetsushi (a 5th grader delinquent who rules over the students of Jouin elementary), Ryouji(Tetsushi's right hand man) and Yuusuke (their sometimes-ally), are known to everyone in town as the "three evil prankster kings."

In a creepy pharmacy located out-of-town, the boys meet an old man who bestows a strange and other-worldly power upon them. Soon after, our 3 young pranksters find themselves battling a variety of ghosts and demons...!?
