## Sinopse

One morning, Ryousuke, the new salesperson in a clothing store, wakes up in the bed of the store manager he admires, Tsuge! Tsuge, a grown man with excellent taste, is Ryousuke's ideal. "You were so adorable last night," Tsuge tells him with a kiss, but Ryousuke was so drunk, he can't remember anything! Will chaos ensue?

Plus, childhood friends face a dilemma when love at first sight goes straight to sex!

A debut comic full of cute love stories.

(Source: Bushido Angel Adventures)
