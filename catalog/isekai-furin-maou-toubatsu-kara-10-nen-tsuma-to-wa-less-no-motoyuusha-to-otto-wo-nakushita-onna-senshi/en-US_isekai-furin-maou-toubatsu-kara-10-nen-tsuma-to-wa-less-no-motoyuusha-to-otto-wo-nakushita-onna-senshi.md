## Sinopse

Yuta Shimabara, who was run over by the truck, reincarnated in a different world as a hero and subdued the Demon King with unrivaled power! Ten years later, the only thing he has to show for is a mediocre and hard-working daily life. However, the forbidden love with the female warrior Lucas, a member of the Demon King Subjugation Party, heats up!?
