## Sinopse

In the near future, an advanced AR technology known as Mixed Reality System has become widespread and introduced to the world a new sport. Circlet Bout is particularly popular with high school girls with successful players achieving nationwide fame. Sasaki Yuuka has just transferred to Saint Union Academy, but can she revive its ailing Circlet Bout team?
