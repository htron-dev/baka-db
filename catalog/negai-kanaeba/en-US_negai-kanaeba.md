## Sinopse

Pupil of the lesson of dramatic art in the faculty and writer at his hours, Kubota is put at feet of the wall. His best friend, Yaginuma, young miracle of the stage setting, suggests him writing a play.
But for the young author, eaten away by a profound inferiority complex, the piece of news has everything of the poisoned gift! Without counting that Yaginuma seems to have many other projects in head …
And their friendship is going to take quite other form …

Four short stories, six couples and some more of funny and charming characters.

A manga by Romuko Miike.
