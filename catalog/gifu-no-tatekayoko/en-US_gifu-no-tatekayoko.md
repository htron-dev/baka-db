## Sinopse

The series revolves around life in Gifu Prefecture in central Japan — as seen through the eyes of Tatekayoko, a female inhabitant who speaks in the dialect of Gifu's Tounou region. The anime will air within the Animal Gekijou program on Tokyo MX.

(Source: ANN)
