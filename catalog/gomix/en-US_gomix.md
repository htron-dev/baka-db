## Sinopse

Tao Warazuto is a high school girl that isn't remembered by anyone. This is because her name is difficult to pronounce; however, perhaps one of the main reasons is her hopelessness in interacting with other people. On the first day of her second year, she finds out that her new class is the infamous Trash Class—a class full of troublemakers…
