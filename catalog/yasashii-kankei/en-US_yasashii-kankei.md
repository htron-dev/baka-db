## Sinopse

Naoya is a car salesman and when he goes to drop off the keys for a new client, he realizes that it's his childhood friend Tsukasaki who he hasn't seen since junior high. After Naoya's co-worker sexually harasses him, he begins to live with Tsukasaki, but will things be just like they were before?
