## Sinopse

After the mercenary group he had been part of was utterly destroyed, Loren, who survived the disaster, decided to walk the adventurer's path as a way to make a living. However, for Loren, who had no acquaintances and nothing but an empty pocket and his great sword on him, receiving a single quest seemed like a faraway goal. So, as he was contemplating about what he should do next, another adventurer called out to him.
