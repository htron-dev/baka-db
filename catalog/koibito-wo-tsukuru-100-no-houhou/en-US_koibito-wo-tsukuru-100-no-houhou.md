## Sinopse

Written by Kobayashi Miyuki and is made into manga by Kawachi Yukari. The story is about Mia, a 16 years old girl. Mia never has a boyfriend and her daily activity are doing house works and buying grocer at the shops along the station. Contrary to her mom, who is a beautiful novel writer who changes her dates regularly and going out to have some fun all the time.

100 ways to get yourself a lover is her mom's best selling book. Seeing this book, make Mia thinks about her own love life. Will she be able to find her lover according to this book?
