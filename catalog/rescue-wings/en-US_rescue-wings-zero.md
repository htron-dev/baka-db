## Sinopse

The first full-scale rescue comic depicting the activities of the Air Self Defense Force search and rescue unit.
The female hero is a rescue helicopter pilot whose father was a pilot before he died.
Collects manga stories originally serialised in the military technical journal 「コンバットマガジン」 (Combat Magazine).
