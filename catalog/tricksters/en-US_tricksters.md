## Sinopse

After making an evil grin, the magician then said "A game? Seems quite interesting——"

The game was boldly announced.

"I hereby declare a student amongst us is going to be chosen as a sacrifice."

After the confusing announcement becomes reality, Emerald University, the place where this tale takes place is brought into chaos. Suspicion, horror, frenzy is rapidly spreading. However a beautiful female magician with her ingenious magical tricks is here to unravel the mysteries. The game is also heading towards an unanticipated ending.

A reasoning fiction imitative magical tale——tricksters.

(Source: Back cover of the novel)
