## Sinopse

Tiny!! Impotent!! Boring!! That's what Tomoya's girlfriend said when she dumped him. Since he's worried that he's limp because of exhaustion, Tomoya consults his big brother, who introduces Tomoya to his massage therapist friend, Hayato (who's said to possess "golden fingers"). As Tomoya's letting himself enjoy a nice massage, Hayato's fingers go in an unexpected place... This is something right out of an adult story!!! Will Tomoya's little friend be able to regain its strength thanks to the golden fingers!? This is a hot and ditzy yaoi story with a lot of sweetness and absurdity!
