## Sinopse

A bunko-bon collection of Tsuda Masami's one-shots that all share the common theme of different types of love and relationships. The first four are related. A few have already been published in another volume titled "Tenshi no Sumu Heya".

1. Busu to Himegimi
2. Koi wa Ichibyou
3. Suki ni Natta Hito
4. 18-Sai
5. Aete Yokatta
6. Ima wa, Dokoka no Sora no Shita de
7. Kimi ga Iru Kara
8. Kimazui Kankei
9. 3-Bitter Pain
