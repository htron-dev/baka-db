## Sinopse

Hinano, an ordinary girl, is in love with Katsu who dreams of becoming a scriptwriter despite the difficulties of the trade. They enjoy a perfect love until love rivals come to threaten their relationship! Sei, a beautiful boy and compulsive flirt, attempts to seduce Hinano and Juri, an intelligent woman, tries to her hand at getting close to Katsu!
What will become of the couple's love?
