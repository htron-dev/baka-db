## Sinopse

A land without magic, dou-qi or martial arts, but having essence spirits. This is the combat continent 10 000 years after the formation of Tang Sect. A new hero and his friends walk the land. There are new "Seven Monsters of Shrek." Will the Tang Sect be able to struggle through? Or will it crumble due to a new essence system?
