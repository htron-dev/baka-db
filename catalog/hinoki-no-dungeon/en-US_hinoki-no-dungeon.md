In a world dotted with dungeons, hunters brave these places in search of mystery, thrill, and reward. One of these hunters has come to try the "Hinoki Tower" dungeon. He enters, and... eh? What? In lots of ways, this dungeon is kinda terrible? An everyday comedy unfolds in this crapsack dungeon.
