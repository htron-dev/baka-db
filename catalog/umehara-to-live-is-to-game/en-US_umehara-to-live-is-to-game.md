## Sinopse

The decade is the '90s, and fighting games are taking Japan by storm. Against the backdrop of this fighting game frenzy, gamers gather day and night at the arcades to test their mettle. And amidst the fervor, one boy meets his destiny...

An exciting dramatization about the real life fighting game legend Daigo Umehara.
