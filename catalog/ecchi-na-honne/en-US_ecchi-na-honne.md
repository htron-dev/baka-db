## Sinopse

From Shoujomagic:
Sex is a love barometer. Everyone wants to be lovey-dovey with the person they love!! But there are things that we don't know.... Do guys like it better without a condom after all? Do girls also want to have an affiar? What does it mean to have an orgasm? But, the most important thing is how the two feel about each other. This is a hilarious comedy, entailing many true feelings of these lustful lovers.
