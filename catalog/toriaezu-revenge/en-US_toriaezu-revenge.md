## Sinopse

Tomoya hates his tutor, Hirasawa. Obsessed with his destruction, Tomo creates a doppleganger of himself to do his dirty work for him. But instead of being the evil minion Tomoya had hoped for, Chibi!Tomo loves Hirosawa! But is hatred really the only thing big Tomoya feels for his tutor? Or is hatred only a symptom to the true illness...?
