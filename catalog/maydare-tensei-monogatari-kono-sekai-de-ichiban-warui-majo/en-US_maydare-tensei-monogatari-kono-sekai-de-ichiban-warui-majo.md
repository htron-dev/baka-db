## Sinopse

Maydare is a place where great magicians control the flow of the world. Makia, the daughter of a notorious witch, saves a young slave boy named Thor. They grow up and learn magic together. However, Makia's previous life is that of a high school student in Japan, who was killed alongside two of her classmates.

(Source: Square Enix, translated)
