## Sinopse

Tokai Hayato moves deep into the mountains of Shikoku, and accidentally transfers to a school for youkai. There, he meets Amemiya Hatsuka, who has had trouble making human friends due to her identity as a nameonna, an obscure youkai that licks men's bodies.
