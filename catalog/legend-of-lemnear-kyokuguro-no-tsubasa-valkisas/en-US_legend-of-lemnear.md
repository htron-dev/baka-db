## Sinopse

Join the lovely Lemnear on her quest as she journeys far from her homeland in search of adventure and companionship. The evil god Varohl is intent on capturing her and stealing her latent powers but finds she is not as helpless as he thought. With the help of her new found allies, Lian, Mesh and Lunagu, Lemnear battles the trials and tribulations that await her.
