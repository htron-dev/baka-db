## Sinopse

Concurrent with an ADV game by HuneX/NEC-HE, the title is a pun about its detective and idol protagonist. It was released on issues 1 to 3 of NEC's Anime Freak FX, a CD-ROM magazine resembling a game for the PC-FX platform.

The hacker Idol King is targeting idols, exposing awkward scenes of their private lives. Now, the next is the popular May Star...
