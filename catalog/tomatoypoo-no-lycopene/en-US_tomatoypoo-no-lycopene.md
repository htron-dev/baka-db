## Sinopse

Meet Lycopene. A tomatoy poodle. Wait! Hold up. A...what?! Apparently a tomatoy poodle is a poodle born from a tomato plant. Meet Meh-Meh, a normal schoolgirl, who finds herself lost in Cuteopia, a strange world inhabited by strange creatures like Lycopene.
