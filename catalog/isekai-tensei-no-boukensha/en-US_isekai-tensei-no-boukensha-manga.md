## Sinopse

Ootori Tenma, who lost his parents and was raised by his grandfather and grandmother's friends who are living in the countryside, loses his life at the age of 25. A god of a different world called out to the Tenma, who became a ghost. This is a story about Tenma, who is liked by multiple Gods of reincarnation, receives multiple cheats, and lives a second life in another world.

(Source: Shousetsuka ni Narou, translated)
