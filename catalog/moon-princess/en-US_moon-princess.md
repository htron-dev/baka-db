## Sinopse

Mana, Kai and Kei are three siblings who get along well, in a country where a queen with mysterious powers inherits the throne. The story begins when the door connecting the Blue Moon and Yellow Moon opens. Mana's journey to become an adult, to the Blue Moon, starts on her 13th birthday.
