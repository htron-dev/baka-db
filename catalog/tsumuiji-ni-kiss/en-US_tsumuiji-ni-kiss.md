## Sinopse

1. Any Form of Love
   Shou Fujii’s life of torture by his brother, Kou, started when they were children. His brother would deliberately do spiteful things to cause him pain. Now, that Shou and his brother are older his torment continues.
   Kou’s torture tactics have evolved from killing Shou’s treasured animals to repeatedly raping Shou. Can Shou’s long time childhood friend, Haba, save Shou from his brother’s twisted love?
