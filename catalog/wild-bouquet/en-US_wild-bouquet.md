## Sinopse

In a country where the sum and total of a woman's existence is to be married, a Princess is brought to the kingdom to marry the King. She will live in the palace for six months until her 16th birthday, when she will become King Leonidas' consort. Instead, Princess Deriana falls in love with one of her maids, Josette.
