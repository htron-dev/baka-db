## Sinopse

Remake of the original Transfer Student Storm Bringer series, by the same author.

This time Ju Ingong is no longer a lucky wimp with a front of being strong. Instead Ju Ingong is now a famous fighter renowned for becoming Jjang of a school and transferring to another to crush it which earned him the title of 'Transfer Student Storm Bringer' Ju Ingong now has his sights on a new school!
