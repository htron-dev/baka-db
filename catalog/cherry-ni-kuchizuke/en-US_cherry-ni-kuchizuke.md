## Sinopse

Do you believe in Soulmate? Himeki Sakura does. Sakura has a heart shaped birthmark on the back of her left shoulder and a psychic told her that her destined lover will have the same birthmark on the same position as her. Sakura's parent have to work abroad so she moved in with her aunt in another town and Sakura intended to find her destined lover here.
