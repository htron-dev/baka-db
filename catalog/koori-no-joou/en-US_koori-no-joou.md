## Sinopse

The very shy Nika uncovers the hidden life of her average-looking classmate Nanao when she follows him to a skating rink. After seeing him skate, Nika tries her hand at skating and discovers two new loves; skating and her classmate Nanao. What will Nika do when it's revealed that Nanao is a famous ice skater?
