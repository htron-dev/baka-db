## Sinopse

On a star with a thoroughly managed society, mankind was dominated by fungi and deprived of all freedom. An "Irregular," the strongest soldier, Dante, decided to overturn the reason of this world after his chance meeting with a girl. Thus, the boy rises against the star. In order to meet you again.
