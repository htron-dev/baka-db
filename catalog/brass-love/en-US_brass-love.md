## Sinopse

Konhara Sui was touched by the National Music competition performances; and this prompted her to apply to study at the music school which Sou Tsukasa attends. Though the school music club rejected her proposal, after much recommendation from Tsukasa, she was admitted into the Brass group… Will they be able to beat the Music Club…?!

Includes two other stories
