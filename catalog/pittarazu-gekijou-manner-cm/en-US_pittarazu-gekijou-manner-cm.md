## Sinopse

Pittarazu-themed manner movies shown at T-JOY owned theaters around Japan. The first four were shown from August 7, 2013 to November 1, 2013 and were included as specials on the Pittarazu DVDs. Beginning August 23, 2014, five more manner movies were shown at theaters. All nine manner movies were later posted on DLE's official YouTube channel.
