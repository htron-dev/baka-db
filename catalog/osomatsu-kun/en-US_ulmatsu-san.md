## Sinopse

The Ultraman franchise and Osomatsu-san both share a common theme of six brothers, naturally leading them to unite. Set in the world of Osomatsu-san's fictional “Akatsuka Ward” in the city of Tokyo, what lengths will the Ultra-Matsu brothers go to protect their peaceful lives as NEETs?
