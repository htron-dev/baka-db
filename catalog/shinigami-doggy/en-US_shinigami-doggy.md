## Sinopse

Ken was a normal high school student until he was killed...chasing after a riceball. In the afterlife, he meets a Shinigami named Shin, who grants last minute wishes to females who pass on. Shin agrees to give Ken's life back if he is able to grant ONE girl's wish. But will he complete this job successfully? And what if something goes wrong?
