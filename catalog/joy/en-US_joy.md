## Sinopse

Go Okazaki, an author of shoujo manga, spends his days aiming to create a work that will move all the girls, alongside his assistant Yusuke. One day, Okazaki accidentally discovers that Yusuke is homosexual, shortly before receiving a proposal to write a BL manga. To make it more realistic, the young author has the idea of spending the days pretending to have a loving relationship with his assistant, until the border between the staging and the reality start mingling...
