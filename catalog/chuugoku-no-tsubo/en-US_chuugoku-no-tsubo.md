## Sinopse

This vase is a magical vase from China...It's a destiny or it might be faith. Shiki's mom is married with another man. Her new dad is rich but aside from studying, she still have to do house chores and gardening for her new house. Lucky for her, she has someone that will take care of her. His name is Fei Long and he came out of the vase!?!
