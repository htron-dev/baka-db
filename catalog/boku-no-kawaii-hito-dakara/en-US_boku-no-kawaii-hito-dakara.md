## Sinopse

Yuuri, Elaine, and Roy have been taken by their old classmate Hugo. With revenge in his heart against those three he has decided to make Roy watch as either his lover or friend tortured in front of him. In order to spare Roy, Yuuri offered himself to be humiliated in front of Roy. How long will he be able to last before he's broken and Hugo moves onto Elaine?
