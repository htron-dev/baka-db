## Sinopse

The story takes place in 1924 in Tokyo. A handsome young trader named Kinnosuke Minoura learns that his fiancé is murdered in a locked room. The elite doctor Michio Moroto, who's been in love with Minoura for a long time, asks to help solve the case.
