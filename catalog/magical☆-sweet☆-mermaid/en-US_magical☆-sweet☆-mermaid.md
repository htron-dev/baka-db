## Sinopse

One day the mermaid Mei hurt herself and a human boy saw her and bandaged her sore. She forgot to thank him and realized she had to make up for it. She wanted to go to the human world, so she went to the mermaid queen and asked her to make her a human. The mermaid queen reminded her that the existence of mermaids must remain a secret, and Mei agreed. Thus, the queen made her a human. Mei went to the human world to find him...
