## Sinopse

Hiroki, who was just dumped by his girlfriend, suddenly encounters a tiny floating spaceship containing a large blue diamond. The little ship starts talking to him, and then (to make it easier to communicate) it borrows the form of a popular male idol singer it sees on a nearby tv screen! Hiroki's life is about to get very interesting...
