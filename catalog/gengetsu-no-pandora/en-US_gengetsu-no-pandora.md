## Sinopse

One day, Fumiki joins the game research club. When he goes to the club room, hot water suddenly comes out from the ground! Also, a girl appears in front of him and plugs a cable into his chest. When he regains his consciousness, he finds himself being in a digital world. A girl appears and says to him, "Collect some data of human's desire..." When he comes back to his world, his portable game console changes into something different... The console is called "Pandora" that makes human's desire something like a game. With this strange game console, what kind of games will be created?
