## Sinopse

Sanda Shinichi has just got a new job, and the company that hired him has an unusual system: the new hires choose their own boss. To his surprise, all four of his potential bosses are women, and they're all really cute! Unable to decide which one to pick, he ends up working for all of them until he can make his choice.
