## Sinopse

A girl whose dream is to be a world renowned pianist. A guy who is a popular pop star. Would it be love at first sight? Rin just moved back to Japan, and Yuya, a popular artist lives next to her. Unhappy incidents between them happen and Rin's crush, Ryochi falls for another artist, Akaya! She is upset but her relationship with Yuya seems to improve. Will she fall for Yuya in the end? And will she become a world renowned pianist?
