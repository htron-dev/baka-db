## Sinopse

Proclaimed handsome elite businessman, Yuuto Shinonome, falls in love at first sight with Tamaki, a beauty who's exactly his type. It hits him like a ton of bricks when he finds out Tamaki is actually a guy!! Nevertheless, Yuuto still acts like a kid with a crush and teases Tamaki like there's no tomorrow...

How can perverse Yuuto handle love!?
