## Sinopse

Brought up carefully by her parents, Hikari grew up with the idea that her parents would choose the person she will marry in the future. However, her fate changes when she meets a boy and falls in love... He is a "deaf person," someone who has lost the ability to hear.

(Source: Shogakukan, translated)
