## Sinopse

Depressed about a love for a straight coworker that would never be fulfilled, a salary man wanes through his greyed daily life. One day, he comes home only to be confronted by a man sitting on a floating cloud demanding to make him human. And thus started the daily life of a thunder god and a salary man.
