## Sinopse

Ichi is a club host working in a family restaurant during the daytime, and that's where he meets his ideal man. The handsome stranger introduces himself as Kyouichi Satou and seems to be the embodiment of Ichi's dreams. Ichi tries to look perfect in his eyes, but Kyouichi keeps witnessing Ichi's most compromising situations. In an unexpected turn of events, Kyouichi becomes his customer and Ichi is about to break quite a few of his professional and personal rules!
