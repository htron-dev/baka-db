## Sinopse

Rika Sawaguchi is a campus idol known as "Madonna of Sugiura High." With dozens of "boyfriends-in-reserve," she is confident to bring every men on their knees in front of her. When her best friend Mako was dumped by the school's playboy Kazuki Hirota, Rika volunteered to avenge her by attracting Kazuki then dump him once he is hooked, yet Kazuki is much more than an average opponent.

(Source: ANN)
