## Sinopse

From: Rebellious Love
Dumped after her high society boyfriend uses her, Chidori swears off rich boyfriends. One day, she was robbed, but the guys turned out to be none other than Yuu and Kikuya practicing their acting skills. Chidori is attracted to Kikuya, but her friend who likes Kikuya tells her that Kikuya is very rich! Afraid of being hurt again, Chidori keeps a 'safe' distance from him...
