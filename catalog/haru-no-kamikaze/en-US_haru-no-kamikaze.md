## Sinopse

Join Haru, Kamiki, and their lively classmates in their over-the-top adorable and heartwarming daily lives. Everyone in the town and school adores Kamiki, but no one is a bigger fan than Haru. While Haru tries to find every reason to hug Kamiki, Kamiki continues to bring happiness and comfort to everyone she meets.
