## Sinopse

Koharu is a shoujo manga editor on a mission to get the manga artist she's in charge of to write a big love story, and to that end, she begins a pseudo-romance with a pharmacist, Sawaki! She struggles to make any progress using her skills as a shoujo manga editor, but for the sake of gathering material, she has an "adult kiss" with Sawaki.
