## Sinopse

Mio Mizumori's parents die, and she goes to live with her evil cousin, Tadaomi Shijou. Her only salvation is Rei Narumiya, the Golden Girl of her school. Simultaneously, Mio discovers that they have powers over the elements of nature... and a destiny to fulfill.
