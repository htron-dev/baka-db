## Sinopse

The story takes place in Japan, in the society that no one believes the presence of God. However, the evil still stays deep inside the society and it is active behind the scenes connected to some people. One day a girl is sent to Japan. She is an official exorcist and her name is Maria. As a matter of fact, she is cursed and will lose her life if she doesn't remove the curse and save humans by the 1st of November. She just hunts devils in front of her to get rid of the curse. What will happen to Maria and boys around her...?
