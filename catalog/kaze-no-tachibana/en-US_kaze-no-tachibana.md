## Sinopse

Arriving by hitching a ride on a big truck, Tachibana is ready to face his very first Kendo match. Despite his small figure which makes him the laughing-stock of everyone, he is very talented at Kendo. He comes from Ehime with only one purpose: to rule the world with his Kendo ability!
