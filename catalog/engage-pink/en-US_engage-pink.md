## Sinopse

A steady love story begins in Engage Pink with a woman in her 30s hoping to find the one, a man in his 50s, and a young 20-something man with a cruel side! Shiori is a 31 year old working at a publisher and, with no luck in dating, goes to eat at a Chinese place where she meets an older man named Takeshita and a younger man named Sakurai in this love triangle romance by Nao Shikita.
