## Sinopse

Tenth volume in the Kemono series. With a hidden agenda, college student Saiki Jun moves in with his late sister's crush, Jin Kyoutaro, who is the contracted agent to the Toudou Group's Intelligence. On the surface, Jun works for Jin, but underneath, they're both struggling to suppress their feelings for each other.

Also included, 2 chapters of the most popular "Beast Couple" Chiharu x Kazuki.
