## Sinopse

Mari Amachi is a very devout Christen who transfers to Perfect Religion Academy. A school devoted to all religions of the world in effort to make it's students the next world leaders in religion. Her friend Saori is kidnapped by the Black Budda cult which is a group who wishes to take away religious freedom though force and brainwashing. Mari prays to God for help but Buddha answers the call! Mari accepts only to help her friend and she is transformed into Butt Attack Punisher Girl Gotaman. Between fighting bad guys, making a deal with Buddha, and wearing scandalous costume is it more then Mari can take? (MAL)
