## Sinopse

A few years ago, Kirishima Juri's parents and younger brother were murdered right in front of her eyes. Ever since then, she has been unable to laugh. Running desperately away from her family's killers, she meets a video journalist named J through her boyfriend Kengo, who unravels her fate towards a new direction...
