## Sinopse

When gentle giant police officer Ippei Hyodo is forced to witness the brutal murder of both his younger sister and police partner, the gentle "Elephant" becomes a "Mammoth" overnight and goes above the law to slowly pick off each man standing between him and his loved one's assassin.
