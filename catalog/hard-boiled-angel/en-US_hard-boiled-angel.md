## Sinopse

The series is about Jiran Ha, a tough, hard-hitting police officer who is Korea's first female detective. She and her partner, Detective Park, are members of the Hard Crimes Unit, codenamed "Blue Angel." They regularly deal with violent and occasionally sexual crimes.
