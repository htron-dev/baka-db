## Sinopse

Star Ocean: Blue Sphere is the sequel to Star Ocean: The Second Story.
Taking place 2 years after SO2, the Star Ocean characters find themselves on a new adventure. All the characters return (with Chisato and Noel making their first manga appearances), with a new spin, and a new story.
