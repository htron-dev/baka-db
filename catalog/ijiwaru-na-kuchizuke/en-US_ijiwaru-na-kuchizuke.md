## Sinopse

A collection of Kobato Mebaru's short stories originally published in Daria.

1. Kinou yori mo Sukoshi dake
2. Sono Te wo Hiraite
   3-4. Ijiwaru na Kuchizuke
   5-6. Matataki wo Kurikaeshite
3. Eine Kleine
4. Matataki wo Kurikaeshite: Sono Ato

Also comes with atogaki, and some copies come with an extra 4-page booklet for the story "Ijiwaru na Kichizuke."
