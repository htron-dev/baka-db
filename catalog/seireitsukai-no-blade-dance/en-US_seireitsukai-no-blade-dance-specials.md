## Sinopse

Mini specials bundled with each Blu-ray/DVD volume of Seireitsukai no Blade Dance.

Each special is tied to the new 32-page short story that original light novel author Shimizu Yuu wrote for the limited first pressing of every Blu-ray/DVD volume.
