## Sinopse

A collaboration between Mameshiba and the Japanese tokusatsu television series Idol × Warrior Miracle Tunes!. The televised commercial starts with Edamame Shiba trying to do his trivia skit when he's interrupted by the shiba-ified cast of Idol × Warrior Miracle Tunes!. They promote their show and ask viewers to watch it every Sunday at 10:30AM. Collaborative goods were also sold for a limited time.
