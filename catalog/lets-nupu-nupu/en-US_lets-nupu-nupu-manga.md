## Sinopse

Ms. Shitara is no ordinary school nurse: while directing her lustful affections at the young and resistant Takagi, she spends her days plotting new and increasingly perverted ways to bed him. Despite kinky exercising, suggestive pole stroking and even bribery, she just can't get the innocent boy to satisfy her urges!

From this shameless woman, to the hopelessly inept Sushi Cat and busty Mari-chan, there's more sexual innuendo than you can shake a stick at!
