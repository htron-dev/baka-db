## Sinopse

"I will create a human."

In 19th Century, The British Empire is full of prosperity as a result of the "Third Industrial Revolution" by Golem technique. Golems changed everything associated with "automatic" life and people's daily life turned rich. But who could expect that the existence of "Automatic Doll" would bring a "finale" to the world?

When a girl who wants to be a Golem engineer, Ashley, meets Gibson who's a genius engineer, the gears of fate start to revolve...!
