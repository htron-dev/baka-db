## Sinopse

After the great battle between the Demon King and Hero, justice prevailed and the devils scattered. The Demon King was killed...

A few years later, the Demon King's daughter went on a journey to find her father s heritage, and met the granddaughter of the Hero. Could this journey signal a new start for the two?
