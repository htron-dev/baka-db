## Sinopse

The Zero-Sum Original Anthology Series features themed oneshots from various mangaka that publish in Ichijinsha's Zero-Sum and Ward magazines. Volume 11 theme: Time Travel and Time Travelers.

2. The Classroom Where He Is by Kumeta Natsuo
