## Sinopse

A novice model sees an erotic model nicknamed "the demon" after filming for a propaganda. It feels like as if he is receiving an astounding education as he watches the erotic model's filming style. But just as he becomes morose as he watches, he finds that the erotic model is staring at him with a scorchingly hot gaze for no reason.
