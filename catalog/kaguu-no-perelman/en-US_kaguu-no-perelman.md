## Sinopse

Utsumi Touma has lived with relatives since his parents died in a car crash a year ago. Everyone is concerned that he is too much of a loner, but he seems to prefer living that way. One day, a previous classmate named Yura appears and asks to take refuge at Touma's house from the police...
