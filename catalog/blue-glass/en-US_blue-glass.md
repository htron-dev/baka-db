## Sinopse

You may know the story of the Princess of Nakrang, but behind the scenes of the ancient Nakrang Kingdom's royal palace unfolds the story of a different princess. The youngest princess of the Nakrang Kingdom, Seol is determined to pursue a different life from her sister Ran, who by unlucky fate, was persecuted by her father and brothers. Miru, the slave boy she rescued, the odd-eyed Jamalta, and the one we know very well... The three men are in her fate.

(Source: Seoul Cultural Publishers, translated)
