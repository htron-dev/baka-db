## Sinopse

While walking down the school halls with his friends, Yuito was nabbed into a room by another student. The student is a new 2nd year transfer student called Mitsu who wants Yuito to keep him busy. Yuito on the other hand sees him as a creep who is a year below him and does not want to associate with the potential sexual harasser.
