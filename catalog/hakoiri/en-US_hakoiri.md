## Sinopse

The protagonist of this story, Tanaka Kouichi, is a poor college student that is the son of a servant of a rich family, the Ayase household. He and his childhood friend Ayase Shion fell in love. However, since he is poor, her family doesn't think he is eligible to be her partner, and they try to separate them. Will they be able to fulfill their love?

(Source: MU)

Included one-shots: Ookami Shounen no Koi, Abnormal
