## Sinopse

Enjouji Takumi is a rickshaw driver from the city that has been hired to work in a small town. Meeting the exquisitely beautiful (but rather clueless) Hana is a stroke of luck, and when he ends up working with Hana, Takumi's in heaven. But the "flower of the town" has many admirers...
