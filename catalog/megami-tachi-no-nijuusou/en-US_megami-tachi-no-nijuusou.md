## Sinopse

Akane is driven to despair in her life of poverty, and Reika is sick of being confined by her family. These two look-a-likes decide to switch places! Akane finds herself in a huge mansion surrounded by Reika's doting husband who has a mother complex, and his younger brothers who have their own agendas. Reika befriends a photographer called Kumada, and ends up approaching him with a need...?! This is a story of these two beauties, love and desire, and their lust for the impossible!

(Source: Media Do International/Jitsugyo no Nihon Sha)
