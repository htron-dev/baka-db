## Sinopse

Somebody wants the Earth! After being invaded for 6 times by monsters and aliens, Tokyo was damaged and almost destroyed. When Runna Minami meets an honor student, Mika Kujiraoka, the bishojo fighters of justice, Mikarun X, is born.

They transform themselves into a giant, without wearing anything, the two heroines fight against the various enemies.
