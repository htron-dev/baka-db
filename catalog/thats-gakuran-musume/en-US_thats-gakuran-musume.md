## Sinopse

Takahara Shiori just moved back from Moscow and she wants to have her normal life, however her friend and cousin stay by her side constantly. The two of them are in a gang, and their presence scares people away.
When Shiori and two of her friends are attacked by a mysterious gang, things get complicated. Who are they? And what exactly do they want?
