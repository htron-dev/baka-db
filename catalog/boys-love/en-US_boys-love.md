## Sinopse

Nagi is an average high school student making his classmates jealous because he is friends with Gou, a top student, and Satoru, an amateur model. But, Gou and Satoru want to be more than just Nagi's friend. When Satoru finally makes a move on Nagi and Gou confesses his feelings, Nagi has to decide between the two...or does he?
