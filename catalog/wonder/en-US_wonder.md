## Sinopse

A young boy and dog suddenly appear in the life of a carefree couple. The boy is Kouta, taken in by his uncle and aunt after the sudden death of his mother. The dog is a mysterious creature he picked up named Wonder. The four of them grow as they awkwardly overcome various incidents and troubles.
