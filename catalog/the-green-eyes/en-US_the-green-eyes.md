## Sinopse

A plane crashes in the Amazon. Onboard are the boss of the Ranmyo Company with his wife and child, Hiroki. Then, 18 years later, a search party led by Hiroki's grandfather in the Amazon finds his daughter and her husband have changed... as well as Hiroki who's become a warrior for revenge?!
