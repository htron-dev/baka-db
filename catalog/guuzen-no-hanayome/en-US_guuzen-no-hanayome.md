## Sinopse

Clare's target is the heartless Mark King, a playboy broker who toyed with her sister and dumped her. Clare plans to seduce Mark and bring him back home to see her sister. Before she can do that, Mark's good looks and charm begin to reel her in. Clare soon begins to stray from her goal, but she manages to pull through and bring Mark back home. After everything is set and done with Clare's sister, Mark and Clare's paths cross one more time.
