## Sinopse

Mariko Tokujo is the 19-year-old daughter of the owner of a large conglomerate of enterprises who has always lived a sheltered and luxurious life. After her mother remarries again, she decides to run away from home searching for freedom, but finds herself lost in the turmoil of the city. A photographer named Nitta offers her a place to stay, but what will happen to her after she is caught up in the papparazzi scandal Nitta is inmersed in...?
