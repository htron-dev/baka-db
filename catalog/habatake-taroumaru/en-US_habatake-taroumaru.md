## Sinopse

Enjoy this moving masterpiece full of vivid imagery that depicts everything from the basics of falconry down to the minutiae of the lives of hawks. Find out if Gentarou, a young boy, will be able to forge a bond of trust with his Hodgson's hawk-eagle, a stubborn bird who refuses to yield easily to humans.

(Source: Manga Reborn)
