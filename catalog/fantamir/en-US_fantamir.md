## Sinopse

When a mysterious incident causes Mir to lose her magical abilities, she decides to retire and become a student in a normal school—or so she thought! To her surprise, she enrolls in a school with a special connection to her family. Will Mir's boring new life keep her from her magical past...and her divine future?
