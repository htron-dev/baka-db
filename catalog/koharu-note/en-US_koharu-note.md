## Sinopse

Koharu is strong, kind and hard-working, but she has terrible luck! After quitting her previous job due to the trouble her bad luck was causing, she finds work under a fortune teller, but he's a person full of secrets.

Will Koharu's positivity find a way to fight against her bad luck?
