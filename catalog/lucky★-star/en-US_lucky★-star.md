## Sinopse

Lucky Star is a collection of contemporary gay romance comics from Japan. From the urban jungle to the barbaric wilderness, the pursuit of love and acceptance are universal themes. The five stories included in the first volume explore family taboos, nomadic marauder lust, yuppie drama, and the meaning of love. Recommended for mature readers.
