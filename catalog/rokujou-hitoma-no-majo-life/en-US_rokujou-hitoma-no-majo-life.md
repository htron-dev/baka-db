## Sinopse

Novice witches Madge and Lilica live together in a six tatami apartment. As the two are both low-ranked witches, the witches society paired them together to take on commissions. However, because of their rank the jobs they can receive are equivalent to odd jobs and reward little. The story of two witches with contrasting personalities as they struggle together to rank up.
