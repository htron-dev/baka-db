## Sinopse

Lee Han-Ah used to be bullied in the 2nd grade for having a ''wide and flat'' butt by a boy in her primary school and has hated him ever since. Now, in high school she is the celebrated, legendary captain of Il-Hwa Girls High School and she is seeking out a pretty boy boyfriend. What happens when she meets the mellow and seemingly perfect Yoo-Min? (Source: Esthétique)
