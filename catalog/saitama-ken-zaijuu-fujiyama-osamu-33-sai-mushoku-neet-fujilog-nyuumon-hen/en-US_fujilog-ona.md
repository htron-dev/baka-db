## Sinopse

The first episode is a motion capture of the Fujilog characters with Perfume's song with the raw data provided by the Perfume website.

The second episode is smooth/realistic CG animation on top of real life footage; it shows Fujiyama in his OSAM 1-gou that we see in the TV series.
