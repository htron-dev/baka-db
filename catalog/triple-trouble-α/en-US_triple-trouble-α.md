## Sinopse

Follow the story of the Osaki siblings, Yuu and her twin brother, Jun, and Takeru. The twin are constantly fighting with each other. Jun and Takeru dislike Yuu's boyfriend, Igarashi very much. His aloof behavior gets on their nerves. Will the twins ever get along? How will Yuu deal with her brothers over these issues?
