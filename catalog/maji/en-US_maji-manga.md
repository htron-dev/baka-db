## Sinopse

Experience the life of a young member of the Japanese mafia. Our main character, Maji, lives his daily life as part of the Nagisa-gumi, protecting the locals and running their turf. He may not be the smartest man in the world, but he has the guts and attitude to become a great Japanese mafia member.
