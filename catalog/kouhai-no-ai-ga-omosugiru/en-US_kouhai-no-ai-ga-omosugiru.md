## Sinopse

Fujiwara hates the school's hero, Ichijou. In an attempt to get revenge on him, he tries to get a girlfriend first, but it's all to no avail. One day, Ichijou confesses his love to him. Hoping to take advantage of his weakness, Fujiwara agrees to date him, but that's when the unexpected happens, and Ichijou boldly pushes him down in the classroom...
