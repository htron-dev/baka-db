## Sinopse

Zero experience points, shy, and pure patissier Erio and Kai, a writer with a mysterious past. Kai is a regular guest at Erio's shop and always messes with Erio, but they are soon drawn to each other.

As well as the title cut depicting the sweet and wet love of the two, this book contains other sweet and sexy short stories.
