## Sinopse

Fantastic PWP series of a stray werewolf and two idols of the school : Nanato Shirogane, a stray boy of Werewolfs, "Garouzoku", hides his identity and goes to high school. The idols En Isaki and Mikado Isaki, twin sons of the owner of the school suddenly confess their love to Nanato because they love Nanato's real face under his eye glasses. But the full moon makes Nanato transform into a wolf and the Isaki know his secret. Enter then Kuro, a fox, enemy of Werewolfs, who tries to kill Nanato.

Additional oneshot : an idol meets again his elder friend he has known since their orphans' home. But his friend pretends not to know him... (Source: M-U)
