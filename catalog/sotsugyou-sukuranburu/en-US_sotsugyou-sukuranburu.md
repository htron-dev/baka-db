## Sinopse

Sotsugyou Scramble consists of five one-shots.

Story 1 - Magical Scramble: Ninomiya Sora and her boyfriend, Hinase Hoshi, go to a fortune teller, and the fortune teller tells Sora that she will lose the one she loves. Soon after, a mysterious stranger snatches Sora away in a midst of playing cards!

Story 3 - High School Scramble + Bonus: Sora and her friends are finally graduating, but the day before the graduation ceremony her grandfather and Hoshi's mother tell her she has to pass a special test. If she fails, she will repeat junior high school.

Story 4 - Sassy Boy: China and her friends go to the beach for vacation. While there, they attempt to hook up with boys. Will they succeed?
