## Sinopse

Takahashi, Sango, Izmi, Horinochi, and Shinji. The five members to a school group called "Movie Research Club". When the club is to close down, they decide to create a movie as the end-of-the-club ceremony. However, with Izmi not wanting to do it the whole group collides?
