## Sinopse

A love story between an athletic boy and a serious girl who is inexperienced in love.

Makino Himeko is a second year girl who belongs to the lower level of the social pyramid, is not interested in love or being the center of attention. That is, until popular student Kohai Haruki asks if he can fall in love with her.
