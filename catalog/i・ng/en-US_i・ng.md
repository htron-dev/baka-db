## Sinopse

Miyashita Ai is a first-year student at Kyoto's Aoi Art High School. A year before when she had traveled to Kyoto on her school trip, a boy had helped her out when she had gotten lost in front of Aoi High. Since then, she hasn't been able to forget him, so she applied to Aoi High in hopes of meeting him once again. To her great surprise, it turns out that Miyuki, a first-year boy she just recently met, is that very same guy. She screws up her courage and confesses her love to him, but....

This manga volume also contains a one shot titled "20Okubun no Koi"
