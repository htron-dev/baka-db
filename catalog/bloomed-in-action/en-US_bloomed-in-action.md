## Sinopse

His name is Terawaki Kyou, a mercenary. Originally from the National Guard, he traveled across the world, hopping around different mercenary squads, until one day he finally screwed up. He was caught in the explosion of a suicidal enemy. And when he woke up...

He was in the body of a girl.
