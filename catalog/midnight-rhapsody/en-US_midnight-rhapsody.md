## Sinopse

Wony is a wolf that worries about his TOEFL score and likes to hang out with his friend, GOM (bear), just like other Korean guys in 20's. However, he has TOEFL score which is less than the average, and even he served his mandatory military service, he does not have a girlfriend. Now, here is the story that can be happened to you tomorrow!
