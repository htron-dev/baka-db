## Sinopse

Yaya is a manhwa by the same author of Hissing (Trapnest). The main character of this story is a girl with obsessive compulsive disorder. On a visit to her new school, she meets a fellow student, "bad boy," and a handsome teacher. Things start to heat up later on, who will she end up with?!
