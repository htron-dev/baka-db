## Sinopse

The beautiful illustrator, Tatsuki, has a problem. No one can see past his good looks. That is until freelance writer, Mikami, steps into the picture! Of course, Tatsuki thinks Mikami is just like the others, but soon finds out nothing could be further from the truth. It turns out Mikami doesn’t even like men. As they spend more time together, Tatsuki starts developing certain feelings… Will he be able to show Mikami how he feels? And, how will Mikami respond…? Find out in this manga by Mui Hatori!
