## Sinopse

Nene, Hajime, and Ryou are childhood friends. After watching a marching band in their town, all three practice and join their school's marching band. Unfortunately, with the seniors graduating, the club now lacks members and and is going to be merged with the Wind Band. Amidst the dejection, feelings between the three intensifies, and Nene finds her emotions in a turmoil.
