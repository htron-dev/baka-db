## Sinopse

Tetsuo Iriya was the nice, quiet kid that everybody knows. Until well-meant prodding on the part of Satoshi made him chose the 'easy' way out. He purposely failed the entrance exam to a good school, and picked fights so that he wouldn't have to be around Hanada anymore.

The last thing he expected or wanted was for strong-willed Ryouji to show any interest in him. The sparks fly between the two, while Iriya's best friend Ryouta watches jealously....
