## Sinopse

Asakura Hime is a girl who loves magic and is good at nothing else. One day at her part-time-job-place, she meets a very strange guy who can perfome a magic that amazes her. She goes to meet him and asks him how he can performe such magic. He thinks magic sucks so she wants to prove him it's not, she decides to co-operate. Now the start of her high school life with a panda-lover mad scientist begins!
