## Sinopse

Shy Mii-kun and lively Kei-chan have been friends ever since their first meeting in the snowy countryside. The two are inseparable—that is until Kei-chan gets a girlfriend. Mii-kun, who is secretly carrying a torch for his best friend, is devastated. How will their relationship change?
