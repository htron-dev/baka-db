## Sinopse

Can memories ever be repaired...?

Tired of her work and love, Akari has moved to the town where she spent a little time of her life there. At the corner of the desolate shopping district, she meets the owner of a watch store that has a strange plate on the display window. Thus, Akari starts her new life...
