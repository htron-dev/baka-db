## Sinopse

Ibuki Noa is an eighteen-year-old girl who has never had any experience with love. In order to maintain the family’s inn, her mother then made her go to Oofujiya Inn to carry out a secret plan to find a spouse! To only just experience her first love, how would the spouse scout go…?
