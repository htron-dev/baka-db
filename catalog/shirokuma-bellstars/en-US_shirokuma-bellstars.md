## Sinopse

This story features the Santa Claus team that takes care of Shirokuma City. Toma is in charge of keeping the reindeer and sleigh going, and he works with a team of cute female 'Santas'. It's up to them to make sure Christmas goes on without a hitch!

Based on PULLTOP's visual novel under the same name.
