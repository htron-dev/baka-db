## Sinopse

A collection of 3 one-shots by Suetsugu Yuki all sharing the common theme of summer love between high school students.

July:
Sumitomo Ayumi has had a secret crush on Narazaki-kun for 2 years and has finally mustered up the courage to tell him, but just as she is about to confess her feelings, she finds out that he is suddenly going to move away...

August:
Mizuki Chihaya chose to concentrate in the sciences at her high school, which made her 1 of only 4 girls in her homeroom class. Teased and harassed by the boys, she is miserable. If it weren't for Kuonji-kun, her classmate from middle school, it would be unbearable...

September:
Fujio Chitose has been shamelessly going after her big time crush Takasuga-senpai of the soccer team, when suddenly, he assents to being her boyfriend. Her long-time dream has finally come true, right?
