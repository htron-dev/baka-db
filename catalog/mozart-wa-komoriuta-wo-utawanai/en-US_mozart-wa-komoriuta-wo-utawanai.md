## Sinopse

Set in 1809, this story involves Carl Czerny, Franz Peter Schubert and Ludwig van Beethoven as they attempt to investigate the mystery surrounding Mozart's piece Mozart's Lullabies, driven by the death of a store owner. Want to know more? Want to unravel the mystery?
