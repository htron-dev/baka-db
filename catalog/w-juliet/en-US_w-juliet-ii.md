## Sinopse

Ito and Makoto have finally gotten married - but W Juliet is FAR from done!

Ito must learn to accept Makoto's dream to become an actor as well as cope with all the disasters and stress her friends and family have!

Continuing from the hit and well loved manga W Juliet, W Juliet II is a whole new story with old and beloved characters!
