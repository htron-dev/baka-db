## Sinopse

Regular high school girl Aoba Wakana's crush is the good looking, tall, athletic, and clever Asahina-kun. But what that guy loves more than anything else are Japan's national male idols, "Jokers!" He's a "J-Ota Guy!" Wakana summons the courage to confess her love, but Asahina mistakes her for just a "J-Ota friend"...!?
