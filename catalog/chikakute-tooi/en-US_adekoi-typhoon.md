## Sinopse

"I want to tie a bond with Chika-chan". The Ukon General Masanori-sama, and the Sakon General Kanechika-sama had been friends since they were little. Masanori-sama, who's unorthodox yet has a pretty face, always follows Kanechika-sama around in the imperial court. One night, just having had talks about his marriage come up, Masanori-sama sneaks into Kanechika-sama's room... for a night visit!?
