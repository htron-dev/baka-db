## Sinopse

Since her birth, Chika has got a mark in form of a flower on her décolleté. She is a student of a noble college, but she lost her parents. The only thing left, is her cat, which disappeared. While searching for her friend, she meets a demon who offers help, but getting her chastity as an exchange.
