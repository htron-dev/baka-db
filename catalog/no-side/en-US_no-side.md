## Sinopse

It's about a guy named Yuuji, who love to play rugby. One day he just up and dies, in the middle of a Rugby match. His spirit is unable to rest, because his team has never gotten a try. So what does he do? Take over someone else's body to accomplish this goal. But...the body turns out to be a girl's
