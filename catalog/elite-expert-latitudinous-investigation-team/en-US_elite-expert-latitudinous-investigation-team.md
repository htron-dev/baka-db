## Sinopse

Imafuku Sachi is a policeman working in a countryside. He is a sole survivor of a plane crash twenty years ago and has escaped unhurt from three accidents. One day, Imafuku is appointed to the criminal division of Tokyo Metropolitan Police. There he meets two young leaders of Special Wide Area Investigation Section. (Source: Mainichi)
