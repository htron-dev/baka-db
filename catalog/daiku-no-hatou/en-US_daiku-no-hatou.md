## Sinopse

The story follows Minato Ebihara who's mom is a famous fashion magazine editor. Minato himself is a pretty big deal on social media and often models things for his mom.

One day his mom asks him for a favor, which is to head all the way out to Kyushu island to meet his dad who he doesn't even remember.
