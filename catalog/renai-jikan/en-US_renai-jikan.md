## Sinopse

"I love you."

Arita hears the confession from his junior, Hirose. Arita is shocked to know that the younger junior has been in love, and he is the love interest. As you begin to comprehend, everything is just hot and painful words. Non-pained facial expressions show occasionally in contact with a cold attitude, always calm Hirose, Arita starts to open your heart, little by little...
