## Sinopse

A collection of short stories. Each story ranges from unrequited love to curious relationships to love fulfilled. Two one-shots are interrelated (Winter-tinged Thoughts and A Lunch Made With Love) though they can also stand alone.

1. Maple Love
2. Love Letter
3. Cycling Under a Starlit Sky
4. Kawaii Anata
5. Winter-tinged Thoughts
6. A Lunch Made with Love

(Afterword by Otsu Hiyori)
