## Sinopse

Saki Asami works in the emergency room at the Suginami Hospital. Patients needing emergency attention come to her workplace, night and day.Here, Saki comes into contact with people from different backgrounds and it is here that we see her in full action as the enthusiastic nurse wanting to help everyone.
