## Sinopse

Chiba, Motoyoshi and Fujimoto are three students from an art school located in the mountains in the northeast of Japan where everyone can sign up, even dummies! These inseparable friends will have to face troubles linked to artistic creation, uncertainties as to their future, and the cold of winter, but will enjoy the pleasure of a life in a school composed mainly of young girls.

(Source: naBan Editions, translated)
