## Sinopse

In a major bank, Junko Shirakawa, working in the overseas investor business department, is an elite prospect who is admired by her peers. One day, Kayo Kuroda, who entered the company at the same time, is transferred to Shirakawa's department. Shirakawa is put in charge of Kuroda's department training, causing their work lives to be turned upside down. How will their bizarre and violent relationship play out within the pit of conspiracy and evil called a bank?!

(Source: Jitsugyo no Nihon Sha, translated)
