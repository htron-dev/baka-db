## Sinopse

The story is based on the Monster Farm 4 game for the Playstation 2.

Phayne's monsters Antlan, Golem, and Garu must battle the three evil gods known as "Chaos Durahan," "Chaos Ripper," and "Chaos Dragon." Can they destroy the Chaos?
