## Sinopse

The cool and pushy astronomy club member, Takayanagi, always begs the hardworking student council officer, Kamijou, to "give them more funds for the cultural festival" but always receives the answer, "absolutely impossible!" Like cats and dogs, Takayanagi goes to pick a fight with him every day because the adorable sight of Kamijou turning red with anger makes his heart race… A cultural festival romance featuring the pushy astronomy club member vs. the tsundere student council officer!
