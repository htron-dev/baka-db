## Sinopse

"Ohenro" refers to a pilgrim completing the Shikoku pilgrimage traditionally done by foot to eighty-eight Buddhist temples. The manga's story will follows three high school girls as they complete the pilgrimage in commemoration of its 1200th anniversary.

(Source: ANN)
