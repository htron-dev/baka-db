## Sinopse

Fantasy series of short stories that involves animals:

Pet Doctor Atsushi Kirishima meets a cat-guy with tail, cat-ear and injured eye when he is searching for his runaway patient animal. Atsushi can not leave the cat-guy and takes the guy to his animal hospital with him.

Hattori, an apprentice of Atsushi's father, Kenzou finds a dog in the mountains and brings it back in a car. On the way home the dog changes into a girl..

(from B-U)
