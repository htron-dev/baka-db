## Sinopse

Hino is a bit of a plain girl, whose friend Yukari wants to be a fashion model. What she doesn’t know is that one of the boys in her class has been secretly taking photos of all the girls in class to build up a portfolio and become a fashion photographer. And when he presents his work, Yukari gets scouted, but so does Hino, which now makes them rivals!
