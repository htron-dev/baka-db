## Sinopse

Attention Girls! Is loving another worth it? As our play unfolds at Saint Theresa Girls Academy, this anthology is filled to the brim with young women's passion. She-wolf, Natsuki, and the transfer student, Fuyuka's, slapstick days are portrayed in "She-Wolf", Haruka who loves grabbing other girls' chests, and the best friend who is interested in her, Akiho, star in "Expressions of Love," Reiko and Ayano, two teachers who find adult love in "First Kiss" and more, these stories are full of breast-grabbing, kinda sexy fun! Three female couples' stories interweave in this Girl's Love Comic collection!
