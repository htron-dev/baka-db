## Sinopse

1. Ushiro no Shoumen Darling
   Jundai inherits a very special guitar.

2. Touch Me Baby
   Heita tries to find out the identity of his mysterious phone sex partner.

3. Tonari no Otaku
   The story is about this otaku name Noguchi, and his animal loving neighbor Takatsu. Noguchi wakes up with a snake in his bed! The snake belongs to his neighbor Takatsu, it's about the third time his snake, Benji, escaped from his fish tank...

4. Anoko ga Hoshii
   Pero the ugly Labrador gets pawn off to the reluctant Eigo by the very persistant Katsura.

5. Release
   The easygoing truck driver agrees to a high schooler's pleas but the hitch-hiker turns out to be a knife wielder...

6. The After Series
   A series of short extras on what happened after the stories.
