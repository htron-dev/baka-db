## Sinopse

At the "Mikazuchi Dormitories" where the male high school students reside, there is a bright and popular Dormitory Captain, Amano and his supportive helper, the Vice Dormitary Captain Hanyuu. Amano always joked and called Hanyuu his "Wife" and even continued to deepen their relationship. But, for Hanyuu, that only continued to hurt him as he held onto his unrequited love for the past 3 years...

(Source: Houbunsha, translated)
