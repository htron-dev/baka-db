The craft's club president at Seishun Boy's High is nothing but an exceptional beauty, Kurone Kanae is known throughout the school body. A mid year enrollment student, Matsuda Mirayoshi makes a sudden decision in joining the club upon setting his eyes on the Kurone, after his application is in, Matsuda has several challenges to face!
