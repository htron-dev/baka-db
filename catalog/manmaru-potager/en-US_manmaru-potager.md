## Sinopse

Today marks the start of a new life...

Touko was hospitalized due to overwork and her husband left her when they moved to the countryside. However, her neighbor Hiro, who works as a chef, claims he'll open his own café and take care of her daughter Hana! How will Touko's life be from now on?
