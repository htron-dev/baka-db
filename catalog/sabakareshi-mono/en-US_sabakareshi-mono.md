## Sinopse

Public prosecutor Kyou Sugiura pursues a case of government corruption against young politician Tatsuki Toudou. Their first face-to-face meeting, however, opens the floodgates of the past and asks the question: What is just?

Before this case is through, he that judgeth will himself be judged.

This volume includes the light-hearted, hospital romance, Like a White Phantom.
