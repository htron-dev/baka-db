## Sinopse

A year and a half at his first job, Akira Shoji runs into a student teacher from his high school days, Ryoko. But she, who once had a relationship with him, now has a ring sparkling on her left ring finger... Set in the world of advertising, this love story faithfully depicts the trembling hearts of men and women.
