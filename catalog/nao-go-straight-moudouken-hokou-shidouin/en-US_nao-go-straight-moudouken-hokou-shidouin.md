## Sinopse

The coming-of-age story of Nao's dream to become a guide dog trainer! Dumped, unemployed and generally unsuccessful office lady Konno Nao.

The story depicts Nao's development from a woman sick of life and people to someone who dreams of becoming a guide dog trainer after a chance meeting. "Memorize the names of 60 dogs?" "Work from 6:30AM to 10:00PM for five years?" All kinds of surprising facts come to light!
