## Sinopse

A long time ago, in a certain world, the evil magician Aval was thoroughly defeated by a brave man Ruldo, who carried "Sword Breaker". Unfortunately, Ruldo was banished by Aval's curse into a different world. Now, will the call for a hero bring Ruldo back into his original world once more?
