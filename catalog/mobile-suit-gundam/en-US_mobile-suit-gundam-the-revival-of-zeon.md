## Sinopse

Taking place in the interlude between Gundam ZZ and Char’s Counterattack, Kazuhisa Kondoh’s dynamic manga of continuing Zeon rebellion against the Federation showcases the continuing struggle of former Zeon OYW cadet Frederick Brown. Currently a Lieutenant in the Zeon Forces, he leads several ragtag supporters on a seemingly impossible counterattack against the Earth Federation Forces. [synopsis by Zeonic Corps]
