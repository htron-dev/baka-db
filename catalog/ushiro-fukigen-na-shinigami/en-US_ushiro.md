## Sinopse

This horror spirit fantasy series follows the strange master-servant relationship between high-school girl Nanako Nishizaki and death god Ushiro Reiichirou, who captures the souls of the dying using mysterious methods and abilities. But the bright and positive Nanako doesn't want anyone to die.
