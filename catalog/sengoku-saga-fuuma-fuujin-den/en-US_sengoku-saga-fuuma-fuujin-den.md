## Sinopse

At the Age of Civil Wars, there was a man challenging the men of power, Hideyoshi Toyotomi and Ieyasu Tokugawa, who is called "a child of wind god." His name is Koutarou Fuuma. He has a huge body that is higher than 2m; with his tough muscle, he stands against his enemies with his bare hands!
