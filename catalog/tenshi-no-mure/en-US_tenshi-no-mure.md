## Sinopse

The members of the number one pop band Angelaid wear wings on their backs. This inspires their multitudes of fans to wear wings in public. But among the people with costume wings, people with real wings secretly exist! Typical teenager Shea suddenly grows wings because of the mysterious disease. Can Shea adjust to being a new kind of human?
