## Sinopse

Contains one longer story and two short stories. The first, longer one is about two high school guys. One is on the swim team and the other is a very famous model/actor. When the model disappears during a photo shoot in the mountains, he suddenly reappears and attacks the swimmer. The second story is about a guy who is rescued by a doctor. The third story is about a baseball player who is taken advantage of by a teammate.
