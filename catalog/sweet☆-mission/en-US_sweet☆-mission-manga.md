## Sinopse

Yoshida Akari, a very stupid, easy going girl hates Morishita Natsu, the smartest guy at school. He is also head of student council, and can control anyone with the power and authority he has. But what do they say? Hate is love. Akari starts to think about Morishita everyday. What would happen to those two opposites?!
