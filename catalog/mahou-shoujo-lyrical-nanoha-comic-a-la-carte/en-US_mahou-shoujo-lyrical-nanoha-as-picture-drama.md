## Sinopse

A picture drama released on the BD Box Set of Mahou Shoujo Lyrical Nanoha A's. Subaru, Teana, Vivio host a radio show promoting their own BD Box Sets. This episode is the continuation of the Iryou Shoujo Medical Shamal picture drama episode that was included on the Mahou Shoujo Lyrical Nanoha: The Movie 2nd A's BD release.
