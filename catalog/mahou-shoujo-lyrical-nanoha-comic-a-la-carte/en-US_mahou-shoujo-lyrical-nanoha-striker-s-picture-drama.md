## Sinopse

A picture drama released on the BD Box Set of Mahou Shoujo Lyrical Nanoha StrikerS. Subaru, Teana, Vivio host a radio show promoting their own BD Box Sets. They look at a hypothetical Midchilda Academy with all the core cast is present be it as students or as teachers.
