## Sinopse

"You Can Enjoy!" is a surreal and easy-going comedy cartoon created to introduce Fukushima prefecture’s wonderful agricultural and marine products to the whole world. The story is set in Farm Academy on Happy Island where the products are studying hard preparing for shipment.

(Source: Official Website)
