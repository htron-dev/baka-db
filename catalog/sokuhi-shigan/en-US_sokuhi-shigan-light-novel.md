## Sinopse

Aida Kiyoka is a so called internet cafe refugee, living her days at an internet cafe and earning a living from her part-time cleaning job. However, one day she is suddenly hurled off to another world. She is fortunately picked up by a kind couple who provides a comfortable life with food and shelter. Her sole complaint is that there is no bath...
