## Sinopse

This story follows the tale of teenager Tamada Q-taro, who is the reincarnated Saint Andrews. 400 years ago, Saint Andrews created 13 miracle golf clubs with 13 holy maidens embedded in each them. Can Q-taro rescue all 13 golf clubs (or maidens) and prevent them from falling to the wrong hands? Come and read this happy-dappy fantasy manga full of hope and dreams.
