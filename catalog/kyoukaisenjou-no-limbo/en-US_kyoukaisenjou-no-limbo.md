## Sinopse

Fuu is a girl who was born between elf and human, and because of that she was treated as unwanted. Fuu leaves her hometown, and arrives at a place called "Limbo" - a world where a hybrids like Fuu gather. Day by day, interaction with the other residents let Fuu's heart and feelings grow.
