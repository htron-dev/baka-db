## Sinopse

After dying in an accident, 30-year-old Satou Taro gets transferred into another world by god. Blessed with a cheat like ability to heal injures and create curatives, Satou Taro adopts the moniker "Tarou" and begins his new life as a merchant. This time, he's going to live as he likes.
