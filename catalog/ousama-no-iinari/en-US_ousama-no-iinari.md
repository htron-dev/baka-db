## Sinopse

There are four office ladies working at Toaru Computer Parts Maker. They met “The Wolf”, “The Irresponsible Man”, “The Devil” and “The Prince”!! This is a love story about the office ladies and their stimulating men!! In this work is “The Reference Room’s Daughter”, “The Irresponsible Game” and “The Devil’s Invitation”.
