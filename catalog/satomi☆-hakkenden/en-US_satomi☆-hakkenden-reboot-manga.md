## Sinopse

A remake of the original Satomi☆Hakkenden series.

500 years ago the people of the Kingdom of Awa lived in fear of Youkai whom are attempting to take over the kingdom. In the midst of this Inuzuka Shino is entrusted with a sword from her father and goes out to find companions.
