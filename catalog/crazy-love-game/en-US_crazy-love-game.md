## Sinopse

Not every girl has the misfortune to see the boy she likes confess to the most beautiful girl on the tennis team--or the luck to see him get rejected! Unfortunately for Kazusa, her beloved Kurosaki has absolutely no interest in dating her even after he's been turned down, and a confession from another upperclassmen only serves to make this "love game" even crazier.

Includes the one-shots Tsukinami Rose and Princess Ship.
