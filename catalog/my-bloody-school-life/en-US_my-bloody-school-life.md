## Sinopse

When a girls high school merges with a neighboring boys high school, everyone is excited except Bada. Bada is the boss of the school grounds and girls say she hates boys, but they don't know her secret life on MSN! This bully and filthy game nerd turns into a shy darling before her online boyfriend. However, her double life is in danger of discovery because boys from the neighboring high school are joining her class and the boy sitting next to her is her boyfriend's friend!
