## Sinopse

The manga follows the adventures of young Ryu and Ken, as they venture forth to improve their martial arts skills after they experience brutal defeat at the hands of Guile. During the series, Ryu and Ken become acquainted with other Street Fighter characters such as Chun-Li, Fei Long, Sagat and Dhalsim. Eventually, the duo find themselves involved with the criminal syndicate Shadowlaw, led by M. Bison, after defeating one of their subordinate organizations (Ashura). Among the agents of Shadowlaw include the Spanish nobleman Vega, hired muscle Zangief, the assassin Cammy and Interpol double agent Balrog.
