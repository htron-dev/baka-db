## Sinopse

A penis wanders alone through the countryside. Kind and gentle, all it longs for is warmth and companionship, be it from a frog, bird, or cat; it extends a helping hand to those who need it, only to be continually shunned by the beneficiaries of his generosity. Nonetheless, it appreciates the small things in life and is aware that true happiness lies in the heart. Seasons may change, and the world may pass by, but optimistic about the future, the penis will continue to wander on...
