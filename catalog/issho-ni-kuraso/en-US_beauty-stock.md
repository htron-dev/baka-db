## Sinopse

High school teacher Keigo is reaching the end of a major decision... being held by Eita, who he just met. The following morning after waking up, Keigo’s hair is cut short and he’s in a major panic!!

“I cut it! <3” says Eita, an optimistic and charismatic hair artist from the famous hair salon, Beauty Stock.

In the end, it turns that he has a fetish for Keigo’s hair and says that he loves Keigo’s hair type. Is he being too forceful!? The ultimate fetish LOVE series!!
