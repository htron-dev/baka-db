## Sinopse

At times hilarious, at others heartwarming, this collection of short stories penned by one of Japan's most talented artists is a perfect addition to any library! Fans of Mori will enjoy seeing concept designs and historical notes from her award-winning series, Emma and Otoyomegatari, as well as Mori's own brand of enthusiastic commentary throughout.
