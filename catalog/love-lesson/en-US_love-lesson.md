## Sinopse

A younger model and a beautiful actor: a forbidden corporate love! Actor Mineyuki gives Ayami, his kouhai in the agency, tips on getting into character for a main role in a movie. "Shall I teach you?" Telling this to Ayami, who had never been in a relationship before, a temporary pseudo-romance begins...
