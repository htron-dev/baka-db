## Sinopse

The high school girl Mint finds a strange little boy called Lime in the temple in which she lives with her grandfather. Suddenly, Mint eot some magic powers which bring her into weird situations and help her meet a lot of strange people. At least she's got a magic dress... which vanishes every time it gets wet or she insults it!
