## Sinopse

Nishiori style ninjutsu user Nishiori Koto unexpectedly became the bodyguard for the boarders of a specialty boy's dorm for performers, popularly named "Prince's house". Her first duty is to be the bodyguard of the popular movie actor Kanae, but....!? A kunoichi and a top model's ninjutsu action and a little love comedy takes the stage!
