## Sinopse

Tamaki Mishina is an orphan girl who left her village to study in the city. On a hot afternoon, while she was looking for a place to stay, she fainted. Upon waking up, she realized she was picked up by a playful rock musician. The story of their cohabitation that could (probably) turn into love, starts!
