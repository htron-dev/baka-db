## Sinopse

Satoshi is a popular boy thanks to his success in soccer. Kaori becomes jealous and spreads rumors that Satoshi was of low class birth. The school begins to shun Satoshi to the point that he stops playing soccer. When Kaori's house is damaged by a landslide Satoshi comes to help.
