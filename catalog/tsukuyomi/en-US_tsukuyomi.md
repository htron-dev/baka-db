## Sinopse

When people pass away, they leave behind something called a "tsukishirube", or a footprint of their death, but not everyone is able to see these footprints or believe that they exist. Only a small number of people, called "tsukuyomi", possess the special ability to read these "tsukishirube". Sakuya Isshin is one of them...
