## Sinopse

Tooru Suzuki gets an unexpectedly good deal for a house. Although skeptical, he accepts the offer and moves in, where he realizes that it has an actual dungeon. How will he cope with the unexpected turn of events, coupled with the appearance of new characters from another world?
