## Sinopse

Mizuki Ikuta goes back to her hometown after struggling to find a job and place to live. She runs into her childhood friend Asahi, and is then taken in as an apprentice at his fathers studio and begins her new life as a gargoyle maker while living in the apartment next to Asahi.
