## Sinopse

Victoria is the young daughter of a wealthy family. Her parents adopt Billy, an orphan about the same age as her. The two children start to love each other deeply, but after the death of Victoria’s father her family rapidly becomes poorer and Victoria and Billy can no longer live together. Will they meet each other again someday?
