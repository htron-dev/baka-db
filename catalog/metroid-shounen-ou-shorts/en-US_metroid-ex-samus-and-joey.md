## Sinopse

Metroid EX: Samus and Joey centers around Samus Aran, who lost her Power Suit upgrades to Greed, a creature of the same species as her nemesis Ridley. With the help of Joey Apronika and Diesel, Samus works to recover them in perilous adventures across the Extreme Galaxy.
