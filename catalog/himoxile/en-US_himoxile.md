## Sinopse

"No money, no job, unpopular. Because I'm useless at best, I'll become a himo!"

Himo is a term for a man who doesn't work and is financially dependent on the women he goes out with. The curtain rises on Akiko Higashimura's himo-man training dojo!

(Source: ANN, edited)

This series was put on hiatus due to online criticism of the subject matter.
