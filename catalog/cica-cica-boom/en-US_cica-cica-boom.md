## Sinopse

Momo, a high school freshman, has her dreams of being fashionable ruined when she is forced/guilted by her father into wearing what she thinks is a hideous pair of glasses. However these glasses are just the thing that get the attention of a very unusual guy at her school, and lead her to join a very unusual club!
