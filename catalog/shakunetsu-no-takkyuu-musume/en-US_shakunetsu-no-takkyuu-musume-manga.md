## Sinopse

The world of middle school girls' ping pong enters an age of heated rivalry as the throne at its apex is surrendered...

Tsumujikaze Koyori, a girl who loves ping pong, has just transferred to Suzumegahara Middle School. Along with her friends who strive to be the national champions, can Koyori bring the winds of change to the school?!

The curtain is about to rise on a scorching hot tale of girls who love ping pong!
