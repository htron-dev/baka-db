## Sinopse

A collection of few stories.
Main one is about Michihiro who grieves after his dog's death. But then one day a guy who claims to be his dead dog appears. Who is he and why does he know every little thing about Michihiro? Funny and heart warming story revolving around school life.
(from B-U)
