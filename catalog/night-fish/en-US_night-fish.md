## Sinopse

The story is about a middle-aged woman, who cares mostly just about her job in the office and not about the real life. One day, she finds out that her boyfriend is cheating on her. At first she is not sure what to do, but with the help of her friends, colleagues, and a handsome chief she can overcome everything.
