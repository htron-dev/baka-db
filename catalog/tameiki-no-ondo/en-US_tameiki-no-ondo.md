## Sinopse

Jun is always being compared to his older brother Ryou, and falling short. But when his brother's friend Yuuya starts to tutor him, his patient nature begins to get through to Jun. When Jun realizes Yuuya's feelings for Ryou, he must confront his own feelings for Yuuya.
