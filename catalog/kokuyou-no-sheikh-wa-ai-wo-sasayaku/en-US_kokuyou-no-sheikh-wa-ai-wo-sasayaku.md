## Sinopse

A political marriage proposal finds its way to princess Lucia of the L'ignis Kingdom. Lucia absolutely adores the idea of fate and destiny. Her groom is the ruler of Ward Assahra, a wealthy desert country, Sheikh Afarzad. Lucia meets Afarzad for the first time at their lavish wedding and falls in love immediately. However, much to Lucia's dismay, Afarzad only sees her as his 'possession' and not as his wife...

(Source: AOC Translations)
