## Sinopse

The novel centers around a high school girl named Minami Kawashima who becomes the baseball team manager at Tokyo's Hodokubo High School. Minami accidentally buys Management: Tasks, Responsibilities, Practices—a classic productivity guide by the Austrian-American management guru Peter Drucker—and uses it to rally her dispirited team.
