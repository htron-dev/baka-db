## Sinopse

Sugiura-kun became an assistant for the manga artist he admired most, only to find out that Sensei is a complete slut. Being attacked in bed immediately after he began working, every day he must deal with the perverted and frustrating Sensei. Despite this, Sugiura-kun sometimes finds himself thinking "Maybe I like Sensei". Also includes a one-shot! A cute, slightly perverted and moving debut comic!
