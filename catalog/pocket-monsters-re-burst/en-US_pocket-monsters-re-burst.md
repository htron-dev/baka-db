## Sinopse

Pocket Monsters RéBURST follows the story of a boy named Ryouga who leaves his small village on a journey to meet his father and a mysterious warrior. The manga is unique in that its protagonist, Ryouga, has the ability to transform into a half-human, half-pokemon type creature.
