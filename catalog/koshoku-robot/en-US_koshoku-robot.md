## Sinopse

The "heartwarming story" follows the interaction between a dietary support robot and its owner. The salaryman is on an extended work trip from his family, but he won this support robot as a prize. Instead of eating out everyday like he did before, the salaryman learns to cook for himself with the robot.

(Source: ANN)
