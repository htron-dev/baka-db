## Sinopse

Morio Ai is a lonely girl, daughter of a famous actress, who divorced many times.
Ai, unhappy with her family's stuation, petulant at school, disrespectful with her teacher, meets a handsome boy. Toshiya Kishida, that's his name, an ex-employee who has a undefined relationship with a lady called Yukiko.

Although this is the first monographic volume of Fuyumi Souryo, the graphic quality and the narrative are very good, a testimony of the subit talent of the writer.
