## Sinopse

The eighteenth century, Tibet. Kang Shiva, a thirteen-year-old doctor-apprentice boy, lives in a mountain village. One day, when he comes home from the herb collection, the bride and the party who were marrying were staying in his house. The bride's name is Moshi-La. She is the bride of Kang Shiva, and came all the way from a foreign country...!

A heartwarming story of the daily life in Tibet of a gentle boy and his mysterious bride is woven.
