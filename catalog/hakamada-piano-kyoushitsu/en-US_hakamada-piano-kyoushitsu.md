## Sinopse

Shinichirou Hakamada opens a Piano Salon in a Western-style buildings surrounded by roses (a.k.a. "rose-house"). One day, a high school girl came in to take a lesson. Hot looking and cool guidance, she also gathers many individuals, each will be granted a dream. Starting in the salon with happy, laughter and tears!
