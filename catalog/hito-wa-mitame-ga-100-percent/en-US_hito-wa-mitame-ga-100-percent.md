## Sinopse

Jounouchi, Satou, and Maeda are three women who work as researchers but have turned their back on femininity based on various excuses. However, they begin a special research project on "beauty."

So begins the comedy-filled life of three women who try and fail at being feminine in a quest for self-evaluation.
