## Sinopse

Hanaura auto-proclaimed himself to be trash, and among other things, he chose to be alone in his classroom. However, all of the sudden, he is confessed by the most popular guy in school, Tsuzuki, who likes him…? Being approached by Tsuzuki, Hanaura can’t reject him properly; but being together with Tsuzuki ends up being perfect, but when it comes to himself, he can't return those feelings to begin with…
