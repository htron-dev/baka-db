## Sinopse

A series of Naruto novels. Naruto's allies and enemies take center stage in these fast-paced adventures, which each volume focusing on a particular clanmate, ally, team...or villain.

1. Kakashi Hiden: Hyouten no Ikazuchi (Kakashi Secret Legend: Lightning in the Frozen Sky) [Higashiyama Akira]
2. Shikamaru Hiden: Yami no Shijima ni Ukabu Kumo [Yano Takashi]
3. Sakura Hiden: Shiren, Harukaze ni Nosete [Hinata Shou]
4. Konohagakure Hiden: Shuugen Biyori [Oosaki Tomohito]
5. Gaara Hiden: Sajin Gensou [Ukyo Kodachi]
6. Akatsuki Hiden: Sakimidareru Aku no Hana [Shin Towada]
