## Sinopse

An amazing baseball player by the name of Keijirou Okamoto enters a high school that cancelled its baseball club 3 years ago due to its lack of success. After hearing this, Keijirou is determined to revive the baseball club by gathering students from other clubs including the tennis and track and field club. He not only wants to make a team, but wants to make it to the Koushien.
