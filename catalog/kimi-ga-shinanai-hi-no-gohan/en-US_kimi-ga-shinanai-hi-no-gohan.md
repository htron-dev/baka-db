## Sinopse

When Semimaru was a little kid, his friend Mika fell into a coma due to a traffic accident. Years later she suddenly visits him in what resembles a spirit form. "Before I die I would like to eat something delicious," she says. But what can Semimaru do if he was never good at cooking?
