## Sinopse

Collection of three stories:

1-4. Toki wo Yuku Uma
Four-part tale of love and loss featuring a musical snowglobe horse that passes through many hands on its journey around the world. It evokes old memories and half-forgotten dreams in those who gaze into it, thus changing their lives forever.

5-10. Star Kid no Fumou na Bouken (☆KID【スター・キッド】の不毛な冒険)
Comic adventures about a group of elves.

11-33. Keiko-chan no Kyouji Seikatsu!? (K 子【ケーコ】ちゃんの教授生活!?)
Autobiographical vignettes about life as a manga professor.
