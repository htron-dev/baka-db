## Sinopse

Because they encountered in a dark street, a mad and deformed man named Kyouichi, Keiji Masuda and his colleagues have to die one by one, killed by members of an ever-growing sect worshiping the seemingly invincible Kyouichi and bearing the same mark he has on the neck.
