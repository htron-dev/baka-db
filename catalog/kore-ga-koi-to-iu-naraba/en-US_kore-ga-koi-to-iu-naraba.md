## Sinopse

Suzuki has always been compared to her sister whether it was with her own love interests that liked her sister or between grades. Being tired of it all, she decides to go to a high school as far as possible from her sister's school. She enjoys her new school very much. One day, after a volleyball game, she sees her senpai crying. After promising to tell no one what she saw, he starts to talk to her.
