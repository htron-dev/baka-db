## Sinopse

Story 1: Haruka always believes that Fujii, her favorite actor, is her fated lover and that they will meet even though she is only one star among the billions. One day she got into an accident and woke up in the body of Saki, a popular actress who is supposed to co-star with Fujii on his new film.

Story 2: "We can get the stars if we tried to grab it by our hands. And at that time I want to be with you."

Story 3: "I used to be an ugly girl. ... I want to change. I want to be beautiful. My personal stylist will make me more beautiful. And one day I will be a special girl."
