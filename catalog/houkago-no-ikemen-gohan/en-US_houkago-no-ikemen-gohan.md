## Sinopse

Seventeen year old Utako Amina is frequently worried about her stomach growling, since she gets hungry so easily. This ultimately has her very hot classmate, Okano-kun, ask her out to eat. Contrary to his appearance, he is both a big eater and very fond of sweets.
