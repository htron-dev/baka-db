## Sinopse

Adaptation of Laclos' "Dangerous Liaisons"
by Shibata Yukihiro (Takarazuka Dance Co.)

The beautiful young nobleman, the viscount Valmont, and Lady Meltuieu, amuse themselves with a risky game of love.
The two wear masks lying that they are in love but only end up hurting each other.

A forbidden love story that takes place in 19th century high society Paris.

Features three other short stories as well.
