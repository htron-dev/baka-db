## Sinopse

University student Kouhei Yunoki continues to wait for his lover to contact him. One day, when he comes to, he's in his friend's room. However, he's on a bed, and is stark naked... His "friend" Yuzurihara is a sociable guy with good looks, and is someone who he somehow got close to in university.
