## Sinopse

Hokusan University Hospital is where all the elite, skilful, and (most importantly) good-looking doctors of tomorrow practice their trade. But what happens when the good doctors themselves are struck with an illness... one of the romantic kind? Is there a remedy to be found anywhere? Three lonely doctors attempt to find the perfect balance between their professional careers and their love lives, in this heart-warming collection of tender love stories!

(Source: Hakuren Yuki)
