## Sinopse

At the infamous QP club, the club for Quality People, it is always surrounded by high school girls who wants to join the place in order to get close to the 2 hot club members.
Sadly, because of the girl fight incident in front of the club, and because it's a club for supernatural researching, the club change its policy that from now on the only people who can be a member of this place must have supernatural power within them.
Among the rarity, there are only two girls who can get in this club, and they are Agawa Chihiro and Kishida Ai. See what t kind of incidents they get into after they got in this club.
