## Sinopse

Sequel to Bungaku Shoujo Light Novel Series, where Touko Amano graduated and Konoha Inoue is now the president of Literature Club. The series is in Nano Hinosaka's point of view.

Minarai (Bangai) series includes 3 volumes: Volume 1 with "Hatsukoi" (First Love), Volume 2 with "Soushin" (Sadness), and Volume 3 with "Sotsugyou" (Graduation).
