## Sinopse

The story of Kenta, a gay illustrator living an open life until his best (straight) friend from childhood shows up looking for work in Tokyo. Half overjoyed and half terrified, Kenta goes back in the closet to take his friend on as a roommate, hiding his feelings and juggling two lifestyles...
