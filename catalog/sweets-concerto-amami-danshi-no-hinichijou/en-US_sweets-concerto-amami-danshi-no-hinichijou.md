## Sinopse

Sweet treats for the boys who do their best...!

Scattered with the sweets and handsome boys that girls love. Though they love sweets, they just can't tell others... A cool, elite office worker, a handsome band member, and a high school boy who would even cross-dress just for sweets... A compilation of stories woven together by a variety of sweets boys.
