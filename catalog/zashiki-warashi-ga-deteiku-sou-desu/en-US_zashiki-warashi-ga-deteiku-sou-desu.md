## Sinopse

Zashiki warashi live in storage rooms and pull pranks, but those who see them will be visited with good fortune. These invisible occupants dwell in Sui's cheap apartment. Prepare for a shocking encounter at the forbidden room, suspicious special circumstances, and a man causing trouble for everyone with his body filled with misery...
