## Sinopse

Each television episode of Chain Chronicle: Haecceitas no Hikari will air weekly with one of 12 "Chein Kuro-Nikuru", a wordplay on Chain Chronicle's Japanese pronunciation and the Japanese term for black shorts.

(Source: ANN)
