## Sinopse

Megumi, an aspiring manga artist, Yuki, an avid cosplayer, and Eri, an amateur writer, are all fujoshi and the best of friends. Together, they go through the ups and downs of high school life, from homework to making deadlines for doujin events, but especially when it comes to romance.
