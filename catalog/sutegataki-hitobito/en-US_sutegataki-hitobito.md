## Sinopse

The story follows Yūsuke Mamiana, an unemployed, broke simpleton who is obsessed with sex, after he returns to his hometown. While there, he encounters the bruised Kyōko, the only person who does not judge him. The two begin living together and work toward finding the meaning of happiness for each of them.

(Source: ANN)
