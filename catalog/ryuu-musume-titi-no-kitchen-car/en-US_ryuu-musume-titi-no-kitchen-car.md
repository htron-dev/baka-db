## Sinopse

Titi, a dragonkin, moves from her rural village to the big city full of humans, beastmen, and other various races. Her goal is to become the best chef in this food battleground, but naïve Titi lacks money and a place to live. It's the warm and fluffy gourmet fantasy story of a struggling, but plucky girl!
