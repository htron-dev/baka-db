## Sinopse

Liling-Po, a master thief, has finally been captured by the government. But in order to be set free, Liling-Po must do one thing: Retrieve these eight magical, yet mysterious treasures that had been recently stolen from the government. So now, he must go on a journey with two other officials, Mei-Toku and Bu-cho, and find the Eight Great Treasures.

(Source: ANN)
