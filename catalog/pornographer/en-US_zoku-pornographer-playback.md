## Sinopse

Rio, incompetent with technology, usually with a phone on 0% battery, and Kuzumi, a slave to his new job as an advertiser with no time to write to Rio, struggle to work through their long-distance relationship. Rio one day childishly runs away from home and, after a series of events, hurts his arm (again) and starts rooming with the instigator of his injury. Kuzumi, on the other hand, tries to confront Rio to do something about their relationship... but can Kuzumi find him and manage to bring him back?

(Source: Fantasista Inc.)
