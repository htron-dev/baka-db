## Sinopse

Suwa Fuyuki is a man taking up law while his father is a full fledged lawyer of a yakuza group being lead by Komine Mitora, a man he hated ever since. But fate struck and Fuyuki's father is now ill, leaving Fuyuki to decide whether to handle the responsibility of being Mitora group's lawyer or not. What will happen to the both of them and the yakuza group?
