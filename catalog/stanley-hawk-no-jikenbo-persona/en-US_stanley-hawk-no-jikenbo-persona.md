## Sinopse

The serial murders of young homosexual men make an uproar in the city. While struggling to solve the case, "thug detective" Stanley Hawk finds himself trapped in a strange cat-and-mouse game with his Chief of Public Affairs, the cold beauty Alistair Rosefield, and the PD's counselor, a Japanese psychologist named Jin Misao.
