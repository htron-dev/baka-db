## Sinopse

A collection of one-shots by manga-ka Kawahara Kazune.

Shuugaku Ryokou:
Watanabe Saori is going on her year-end class trip to Kyoto. Her friends are excited b/c drama and romance always seem to be in the air, but with who...??

Koigokoro:
Ever since Yamazaki Takuya introduced himself as a "short kid but with a lot of power", Nagata Kiyomi has always noticed him. True, he is short, but he somehow always seems to stand out from everyone else. During the annual school sports festival, she tells him as much, but...??

Sekkaku Natsu dashi:
Miyazaki Mitsuyo has a secret. She has been taking extra summer science classes even though she doesn't need to just so she can see her crush Tanaka Issei in class. She's determined to tell him how he feels by the end of the summer, but things are always more complicated than she expects...
