## Sinopse

The action fantasy manga's story takes place during the Cold War between the United States and the Soviet Union. A coup d'état breaks out in a certain small country, and as a result three princesses are pursued by their homeland. Sometimes with guns and sometimes with power, the sisters flee their pursuers.

(Source: ANN)
