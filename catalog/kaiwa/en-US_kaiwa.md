## Sinopse

Choco has a crush on her classmate Ikeda, who plays soccer. One day, while fawning over Ikeda, her odd but beautiful classmate Monaka overhears her talking to herself. Monaka promises not to tell anyone and decides to help Choco win over Ikeda. Together they become friends as they end up in bizarre situations one after another.
