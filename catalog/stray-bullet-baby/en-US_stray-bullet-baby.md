## Sinopse

Movie advertising agency employee Murakami Chihiro has someone he yearns for. His customer is fashion magazine editor Motona Kiyoharu. An incredible worker with an attractive smile, he is the one person Chihiro really desires, but they don't get any opportunity to get closer. However one fateful day, at a drinking party Chihiro looks after a drunken Motona and takes him home...!?
