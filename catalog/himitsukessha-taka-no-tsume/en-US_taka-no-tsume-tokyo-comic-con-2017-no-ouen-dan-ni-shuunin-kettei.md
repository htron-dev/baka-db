## Sinopse

A webisode where the Eagle Talon cast breaks the 4th wall to say that they are the official appointed cheer squad of the 2017 Tokyo Comic Con. They go on to explain what the Comic Con is about and plug their newest film DC Super Heroes vs. Taka no Tsume-dan.
