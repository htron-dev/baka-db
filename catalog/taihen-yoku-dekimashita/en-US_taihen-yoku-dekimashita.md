## Sinopse

Nonoyama whose friend is only a stuffed animal spent her elementary and junior high school years as a true truant because of neglect and bullying. Now having the chance to reset her life as a high school student in an out of town school, she's determined to make friends and enjoy her school life! However, her high school life is a rocky road from day one...

(Source: Paper Dolls Project)
