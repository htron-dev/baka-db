## Sinopse

Miyo, a Japanese young girl (15 years old), must turn live in Tokyo. She lived in France since the age of 6 years. She leaves therefore her friends and Nicols (the boy she's in love with). Is she going to succeed in integrating in her new school, making new friends? Or will she want to go back to France?
