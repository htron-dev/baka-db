## Sinopse

DJ and music producer Yasutaka Nakata known for his work with Perfume and Kyary Pamyu Pamyu Nakata recruited the characters from Isobe Isobee Monogatari to star in a promotional video for his new single "Seiseseiseiseiseseisei."
