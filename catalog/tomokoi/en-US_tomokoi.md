## Sinopse

A high school debut with a classmate. A kabedon from a cram school friend. A date with a hobby friend. A love confession from a childhood friend. This is a collection of four love stories between friends! Experience a dreamlike love from a male friend that will fulfill a girl's desire for a pounding heart. This is the first omnibus by an up and coming shoujo mangaka!
