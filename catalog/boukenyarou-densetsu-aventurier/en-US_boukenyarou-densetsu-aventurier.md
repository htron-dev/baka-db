## Sinopse

Anthology of imaginary explorer tales.

Since the mists of time, man always pursued a dream, the one to illuminate dark zones of the universe and explore the most remote lands of earth. That's how multiple legends transmitted through generations were born, telling us the story of the men of courage - the explorers.
