## Sinopse

A collection of nine surreal short stories sharing a theme of water.

1. Obon (Bon)
2. Watashitachi wa Mada Tochuu (We’re Still on Our Way)
3. Fusion
4. Nante Kanashii Hoshi (Such Sorrowful Stars)
5. Engai no Kisetsu (Salt Damage Season)
6. Umi no Otenki (Sea Weather)
7. Gyuu (Squish)
8. Meltism
9. Ki Setsu Sui (Season Time Water)
