## Sinopse

Kuragehime Gaiden: Barakura - Bara no Aru Kurashi is a spin-off manga of Kuragehime, about middle-aged Japanese women who love everything Korean. Chiyoko (Chieko's mother) is a K-pop fan. Together with her girlfriends, they decide to go to South Korea to pursue actor Bae Yongjoon.
