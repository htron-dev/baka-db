## Sinopse

Four gruesome novellas from Japan's master of horror manga. A young girl adores her science professor and his fine work to lessen pollution in the oceans, which is causing sealife to mutate. But when she tells her teacher of her strange dream, his reaction is altogether different to what she expects...
