## Sinopse

The Time of Gods has ended. What remains are human and Gumsue. Gumsue are people who can turn into animals, and some of them have more power than humans. However, while humans hunt them they choose to hide themselves. Hojeo is a strong Gumsue who is on the humans' side for his friend, Taesoo. However, the more cruel the humans' hunting becomes, the more conflicted Hojeo becomes. Meanwhile, the Gumsue have started moving...
