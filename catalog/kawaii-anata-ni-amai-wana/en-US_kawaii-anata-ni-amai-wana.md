## Sinopse

In the sickroom of Keisei high school, school doctor Katagiri is often visited by Atsuki, the sickly and handsome "school prince". During tea and sweets they converged, but can be Atsuki's feelings be true or just an escape from the loneliness after the death of his parents? And does a love between teacher and pupil even can have a future?
