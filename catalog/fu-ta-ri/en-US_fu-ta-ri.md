## Sinopse

A very touching story about the daily life of Haga Yasuyuki and Kudou Aiko. They form a seemingly normal young couple, but Aiko has an incurable heart disease, which means that she'll die very soon. It is up to Yasuyuki to try to make Aiko's last days worth living.

(Source: MU)
