## Sinopse

The story revolves around an underground barrier beneath Tokyo that separates our world from the evil spirits of another world. For centuries, the Hirasaka family has been entrusted with protecting this barrier. Within the past ten years, a conspiracy of unknown parties has been dismantling the barrier, piece by piece, and the future of Tokyo rests in one man who vows vengeance. [ANN]
