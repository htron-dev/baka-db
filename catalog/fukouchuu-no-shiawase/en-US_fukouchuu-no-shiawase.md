## Sinopse

Since he was young, businessman Yaotomi Eiju has been blessed with unfathomable good luck. He leads an extremely easy-going life. Then one day, his good luck suddenly disappears, and bad luck starts to follow him at every turn. After being advised to get help, Yaotomi decides to visit a shrine on his way home after work, where he happens upon a crude jizou statue. And when he arrives home, a man he has never seen before is standing there...

(Source: Homesha, Fujoshi Bitches)
