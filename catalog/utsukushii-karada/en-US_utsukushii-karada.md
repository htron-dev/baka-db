## Sinopse

"Just thinking I'm about to ink his body... fills me with intense euphoria."
Rookie tattoo artist Yuuji gets an unusual job—to tattoo a painting of master artist Taisen Hanada onto the body of a beautiful young man called Michiru. For 15 years, Michiru has been modeling for Taisen's works, living only for him. Michiru's beautiful face and body are the source of Taisen's inspiration and object of devotion, and Michiru in turn idolizes Taisen. Tasked with tattooing that lovely body, Yuuji senses his inner beast stirring at the sight of Michiru's smooth, fair limbs. "If only I could engrave myself on him, inside and out..." A tattoo artist inscribes a masterpiece of love and desire on an innocent young beauty whose body and soul belong to another...
