## Sinopse

Commercials for Nissin Foods' Hungry to Win campaign. The first episode is with Manchester United. It features Wayne Rooney, Robin van Persie, and Angel Di Maria. The second and third episodes are a crossovers with Shin Tennis no Ouji-sama.
