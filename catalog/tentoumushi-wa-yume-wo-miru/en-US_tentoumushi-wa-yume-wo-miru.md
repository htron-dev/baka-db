## Sinopse

The 4th year of the Showa Era, Tokyo. In the cafe "Tentoumushi," a secret customer visits today to meet the "boy who appeared in the dream." A Showa modern fantasy of Takatsuki, a high school student with a mysterious ability, and a new waitress, Miwako!
