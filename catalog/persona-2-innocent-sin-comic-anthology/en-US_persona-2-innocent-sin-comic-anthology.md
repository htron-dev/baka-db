## Sinopse

An anthology of short stories by various mangaka, published by Ichijinsha in honor of the Persona 2: Innocent Sin PSP remake.

1. P [Taihei Hiromi]
2. Uwasa no Akuma wo Buttaose! [Katou Michiru]
3. Satomi Tadashi nite [Yanagiya Maro]
4. Kimi no Ushiro [Kari Erika]
5. Saa Docchi!? [Sanada Juan]
6. Soreyuke Gold Tankentai [Tsukiyama Ryuuji]
7. Nana Shimai Gakuen Shinbunbu Buchou no Shinsou [KAY]
8. Hataraku Oneesan [Ookami Wakana]
9. Nayameru Seishounen [Aoba Hayato]
10. Hara ga Hette wa [Sakazaki Mei]
11. Sengen Shimashita. [Shiroishi Kotoni]
12. Uwasa no Gas Chamber [Itsuki Makoto]
13. Komatta Ippiki Ookami [Hayase Reku]
14. Houkago Uwasa Jikan [Iwai Tomo]
