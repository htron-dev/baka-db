## Sinopse

A 4-koma anthology of Touken Ranbu -ONLINE- by various artists.

It's the year 2205 AD, stories of the old days are slowly being modified by unknown, evil forces. As a sage, you have the ability to bring life to inanimate objects. Your duty is to bring these blades to life, create an army, and defeat the enemies!
