## Sinopse

A web-exclusive collaboration between Santen an eye drops company and One Piece. The commercial is promoting the new Sante FX eye drops in a stylish Luffy model and Trafalgar Law model. On October 31, 2017 another commercial was released with the same animation but showcasing a Sanji model and Zoro model instead.
