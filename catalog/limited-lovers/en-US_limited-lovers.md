## Sinopse

Kakinomoto Karin, the daughter of a millionaire, lost her ability to walk due to an accident. During her stay at the hospital, it was love at first sight with the medical researcher, Okita Teppei! Not only did she buy his house, but insisted Teppei spend 2 hours with her everyday as a condition if he wants to continue to live in his house. Karin, who only wanted to win the heart of Teppei, will one day find out that not only does Teppei already have a fiancee, but he has a scary split personality too...?!

(Source: Esthétique)
