## Sinopse

Kinpatsu Ouji:
Yoshioka Saori is a timid teacher and the advisor of the astronomy club. Because of her appearance she's often mistaken for a ghost and she's not getting many students to join the club because she scares them away.

One day a mysterious blond transfer student, Kanou Yuuichi, come to her class and want to join the astronomy club. What is he up to?

Include five other one-shots from different mangaka:

Hyoi Ouji - Yoshino Satsuki
Nakimushi Ouji - Hanazawa Chika
Doukyo Ouji - Tanigawa Nico
Meimon Ouji - Hayama Seri
Jyosou Ouji - Fujita Marumi
