## Sinopse

Melody's parents died a few months ago and now she's moving to a new town with a grandfather whom she never met. One day, she accidentally breaks into her grandfather's attic and finds a portrait of herself in a luxurious costume, wearing the same locket her parents gave her. What is Melody's relation with the girl in the picture?
