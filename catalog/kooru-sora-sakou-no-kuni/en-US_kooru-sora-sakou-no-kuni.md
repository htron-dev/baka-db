## Sinopse

A young girl is an artist apprentice who decorates domes with rare "fragments" inside of them. The fragments are made by silver sand storms that "imprints" the scenery as it existed during the storm. One day she goes with her master to sell her first dome and a storm comes to her village. When she goes to find her master at her village she finds a strange boy instead and is then picked up by a dangerous group of people...
