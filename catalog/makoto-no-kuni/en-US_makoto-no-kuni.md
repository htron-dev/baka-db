## Sinopse

The series focuses on Saitou Hajime and Hijikata Toshizou of the Shinsengumi, the police force from the Bakumatsu era that spelled the end of the shoguns' rule over Japan. The new story is set in the Aizu region of northern Japan.

(Source: ANN)
