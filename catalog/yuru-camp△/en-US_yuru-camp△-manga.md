Rin, a girl camping by herself at the base of Mt. Fuji.

Nadeshiko, a girl who came to see Mt. Fuji on her bicycle.

The scenery the two witness over a supper of cup ramen marks the start of a new friendship and many adventures to come, camping in the great outdoors!
