## Sinopse

At the heart of the stories is the fictional lives of the voice actors who play the roles of the characters in Fushigi Yuugi with particular attention paid to Nakago.

In here, Miaka is transformed into a super idol, Tamahome is a spoiled prima donna and Nakago, Tamahome's wicked and evil adversary, is a person living in a hovel, who comes late to the recordings and is totally under the thumb of Tamahome who scolds him every time.

(Source: aiminako)
