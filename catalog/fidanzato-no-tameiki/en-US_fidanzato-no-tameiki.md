## Sinopse

Seven very loosely connected stories that take place at all girl's private school, "Himeyuri Jogakuin."

1. Sirena (Ningyo)
2. Rivolzione (Kakumei)
3. Restrizione (Sokubaku)
4. Rose e beatia (Bara to Yajuu)
5. Splendore (Kouka)
6. Tendazione (Yuuwaku)
7. Silenzio (Seijaku)

Note: Italian titles were used in the original.
