## Sinopse

Ever wondered about the inner workings of a Maid Cafe? Maybe you've wondered how the "maids" are like before or after work? Or perhaps you've wondered how they deal with a job where pleasing the customer is their priority? Well, enter a delicate, attractive girl who gets convinced, and sucked into such a job, and see this light-hearted story unfold!
