## Sinopse

Oomiya-kun is often late for class so as a punishment, he is forced to join the Track and Field Club. However, that's where he discovers Nazuna Yuizaki, the girl he has a crush on, training seriously with Shot Put. Can he use this opportunity to get her to finally notice him?
