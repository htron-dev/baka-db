## Sinopse

Department store "Take" has been targeted by multiple instances of shoplifters. The store hired a girl to arrest these shoplifters. Her name is Tatsugawa Torami. She specializes in catching shoplifters during the act and flaunting her large breasts. Will she be able to eradicate the shoplifters from this department store?
