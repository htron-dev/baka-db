## Sinopse

A continuation of the gripping and cutting-edge Confidential Confessions, this series deals exclusively with the seedy underbelly of the sex industry. Using emotionally moving storylines and multi-dimensional characters, here is a shocking look into the hard-hitting issues that teens face today. Enlightening without being preachy, Confidential Confessions speaks to teens in a heartfelt, authentic way.
