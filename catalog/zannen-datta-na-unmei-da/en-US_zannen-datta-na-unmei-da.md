## Sinopse

One day, nightclub host and womanizer Yuuto Izumi is suddenly kidnapped by men in black suits. They question him about seducing the fiancée of a certain heir to a business empire and reprimand him for breaking up the engagement... But the heir in question turns out to be Kaname Mikoshiba, a classmate from high school!! Kaname has a proposition for his old friend Yuuto. He's willing to forgive him on the condition that he become Kaname's possession. Yuuto is confused, and he doesn't know what to think - until he's taken to the Mikoshiba estate where Kaname professes his love to him, gets on top of him, and...!! Don't miss this exciting rom-com!
