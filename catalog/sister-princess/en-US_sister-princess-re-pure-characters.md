## Sinopse

Specials of Sister Princess Re Pure.

"Character's" has twelve mini-episodes of each of the girls: Karen, Mamoru, Aria, Hinako, Kaho, Rinrin, Chikage, Yotsuba, Haruka, Marie, Shirayuki and Sakuya, with about nine minutes long each, emphasizing the unique way in which each one of the girls see Wataru, bringing the perspectives and expectations that each has for him.
