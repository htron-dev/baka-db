## Sinopse

On Kimura Yuusuke's first day of high school, he picks up a 10-yen coin when suddenly, a magical 10-yen coin fairy able to grant his wishes appears from the coin. Or so she claims... Chaos ensues in this eccentric comedy of love and magic.
