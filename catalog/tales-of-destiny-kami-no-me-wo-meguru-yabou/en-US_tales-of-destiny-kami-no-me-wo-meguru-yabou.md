Stan Aileron is a youth of strong sword man who is taking the trip to the major nation Seingald to realize the his dream of becoming a soldier. Stan finds an old-fashioned sword in ancient ruins. However, the sword starts talking to him, calling itself Dymlos. Stan who became the owner of Dymlos that is special sword swordian acts with Rutee who is the owner of swordian similarly. Dymlos becomes the key to the fame, fortune, and adventure Stahn seeks as the young man meets other Swordians, quickly becoming embroiled in a battle that ambition over huge and extremely powerful lens called the Eye of Atamoni (God's Eye) causes.
