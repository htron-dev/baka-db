## Sinopse

Motto! Ganbare! Kieruna!! Shikiso Usuko-san is a heart-full comedy depicting a female college student, Ms. Koko who has a low presence and is not easily recognized by people. In the first chapter of the new chapter, Shikiso-san who graduated from university aims to be a nursery teacher.
