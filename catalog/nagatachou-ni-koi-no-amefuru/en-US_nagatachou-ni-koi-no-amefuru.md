## Sinopse

This volume contains 4 one-shots by author Hasebe Yuri.

Nagatachou ni Koi no Ame Furu:
Takasugi Masaomi is a young, promising member of Parliament, but his handsome face and playboy behavior greatly annoy Sau, his secretary, who is constantly forced to rearrange his packed schedule to accommodate his dates...

Nettai Suisou Shounen:
High school teacher Aida Mirei was asked to teach extra lessons for Sakurai Sou, a student who missed too many classes because of his job as an idol star. The first day goes normally, except at the very end when...??

Koi to Joyuu to Uso to Kare:
Yukitani Kumiko, an actress that always seems to be involved in some scandalous love affair, is in a new movie with co-star Hijiri Takumi. Their managers are intent on making sure the movie will be a success with no more scandals!

Makin' Lovely Man:
Kodama Erika, who works at a cosmetics counter at a department store, encounters a very handsome young businessman who asks her to do his make-up!
