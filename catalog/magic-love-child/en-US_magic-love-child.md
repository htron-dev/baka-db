## Sinopse

Jane is a magician working at a night club in London. One day he got dumped by his girlfriend. She left him while he's sleeping without giving him any explanation. On the same day that she left, he got fired from his job and since then his life is getting downhill.

7 years later while he's hired to perform a magician show at the Foster Home, he met a little girl there and is soon learned that she is his girlfriend's daughter. He is in shocked, Diana, his girlfriend is now dead and this little girl is now convinced that Jane is the one who can take her to meet her real dad.

Jane doesn't want to get in any more trouble. He tries to leaves the town as soon as possible after knowing this but faith brings him and that little girl to have to lives together.
