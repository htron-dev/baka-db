## Sinopse

Loved by men and despised by women, University student Otonashi Karen is a major "burikko" (two-faced, usually nicer to boys) whose love for Takeda Gunji can't be denied. This two-book series shows how far Karen is willing to go, to get Gunji to fall in love with her.
