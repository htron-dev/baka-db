## Sinopse

Nakayama Satoshi was once a pro soccer player in the J League, but after a disagreement with a referee turns heated he's forced to retire. Unable to give up his passion for the sport, he becomes a teacher and heads the Shiba High School soccer team, putting love of the game above all else.
