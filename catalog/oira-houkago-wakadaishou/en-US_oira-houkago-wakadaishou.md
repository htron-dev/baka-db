## Sinopse

Meet our hero Wakadaishou! He doesn't have much interest in school; he saves his energy for all the excitement he can stir up after school.

This is an early Adachi Mitsuru 'youth manga,' filled with engaging characters, comic scenes, and a young hero with a zest for life.

(Source: MU)
