## Sinopse

Noah, the last survivor from Earth, aims for a new land with the spaceship that was handed down to him by his scientist mother. He arrives at a planet where all living things possess "magic." While he is out doing field work, he meets a girl who claims to be the demon lord's daughter. A cyborg youth traverses a planet full of mysteries in this isekai sci-fi fantasy.

(Source: AlphaPolis, translated)
