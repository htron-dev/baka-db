## Sinopse

The drop-dead-gorgeous and high sex appeal Hibiki Akasaka is going through breakup blues. Soon after being dumped, she's asked to become her schoolmate Aoyama-kun's mistress?! Aoyama-kun will try to gain Hibiki's affections through different means, so let's just hope he doesn't nosebleed to death in the process.
