## Sinopse

"The story of a man who runs at the forefront of the voice acting industry."

Manga adaptation of the first book written by the voice actor renowned for his roles as numerous protagonists in various series, Kaji Yuki. The book tells the story of the troubles Kaji shared back in his early days, and how they now form his passion towards this occupation of "voice acting." A toast to all who are working towards their dreams! What does it mean to hold a dream? And what do we do to make it a reality?
