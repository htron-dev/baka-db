## Sinopse

Kingdom Hearts: Another Report is a 64-page book that was given exclusively to those who pre-ordered Kingdom Hearts II: Final Mix+ as a bonus. It includes the manga chapter "After the Door is Closed" and the short story "Roxas: Somewhere in Time".
