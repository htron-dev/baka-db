## Sinopse

Fumino works at a small town postal office. She was the only one works there because people are now using the more convenient e-mails, they seldom use letters anymore. However, Fumino really loves letters. Because according to her, letters are filled with emotions of the writer. This story is about her daily job at the small town post office, how many unexpected things and miracle could arrive in this small town because of the letters that she helps to deliver.
