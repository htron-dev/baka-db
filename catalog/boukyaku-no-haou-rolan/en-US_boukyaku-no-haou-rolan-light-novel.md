## Sinopse

The selection of Rolan as the Commander of the Royal Family's Guard was a dangerous bet that could well undermine the survival of the empire.

Around them, a mixture of mystery and speculation swirls to lead Loran to his fate; LT Weapons, relics from The Lost World, Monsters appearing from History and the ambitions of Amentei, the leader of the Enemy forces.
