## Sinopse

A joint work between Slayers creator Kanzaka Hajime and Majutsushi Orphen Hagure Tabi creator Yoshinobu Akita.

It was originally published in the special edition of Dragon Magazine "Fantasia Battle Royale" April issue 2001, and was published in book form on June 30, 2005 accompanied by a radio drama.
