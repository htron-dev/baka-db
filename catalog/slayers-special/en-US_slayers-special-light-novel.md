## Sinopse

Slayers Special is a novel series written by Hajime Kanzaka and illustrated by Rui Araizumi.
Each volume consists of one-shot stories (sometimes, 2-chapters stories) about a pre-Slayers Lina Inverse and Naga the Serpent.
(Source: Kanzaka Wiki).
