## Sinopse

Keiichi works at a cafe with particularly handsome waitstaff & lately he's had the feeling someone is following him on his way home. One day Keiichi decides to face his stalker head on & it turns out to be Michiyuki, an art school student who is exhibiting his work at the same cafe Keiichi works. When Keiichi asks Michiyuki why he is stalking him, his answer blows Keiichi away!
