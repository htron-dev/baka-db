## Sinopse

A revenge drama by a reincarnated daughter and a devil! Queen Christian was put in prison by her half-sister and executed at the young age of sixteen.

Sixteen years have passed since then, and there is one girl who thinks about the Queen's birthday festival. This is Theresia, the daughter of the Duke. She was the ex-queen who traded with the beautiful devil and reincarnated! The curtain of revenge by the reincarnated daughter and the devil rises!

(Source: Square Enix, translated)
