## Sinopse

High-school girl Azusa Asahina has an attractive figure, top grades, and is recognised in school as the perfect pretty girl. However, her real self is actually a mega idol-otaku! "My favorite is my everything; I'd even throw away my life for him!!" During the concert of her favorite idol Chikashi Chida, she gets involved in a certain incident and ends up inside his body...!? An idol otaku x idol member boy-girl body swap high tension love story!!
