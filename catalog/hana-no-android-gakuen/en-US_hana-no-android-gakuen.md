## Sinopse

The fictional girl's high school called “Android Gakuen” is located somewhere in Japan, the capital of cell phone sexy. There are 15 beautiful students each with their own unique character and are the manga personification of the slick Android devices they represent. In the story the Android girls work hard together and have a pleasant school life. And just like the real world, they interact with their fellow cool students like iPhone, BlackBerry and Windows Phone that attend rival high schools.

(Source: ANN)
