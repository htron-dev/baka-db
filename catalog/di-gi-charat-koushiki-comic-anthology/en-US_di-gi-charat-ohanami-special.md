## Sinopse

The Ohamami Special consisted of four independent episodes of Di Gi Charat with no continuation that aired in Spring 2001 in Japan.

• Ohanami Special Episode 1 - The Evil Settles Down  
• Ohanami Special Episode 2 - Sakura Sakura
• Ohanami Special Episode 3 - Long Haired Onii-san-nyu
• Ohanami Special Episode 4 - Don`t Call me Rabi-en-Rose
