## Sinopse

Two stories about two couples.

In the first, an omega who gets dumped begins to start his heat in the middle of the street and gets picked up by a nice beta, brought home and cared for. He decides to take advantage of the kind-hearted beta, until the beta overworks himself...

In the second, a beta keeps losing out to other alphas around him and he takes out his anger by having sex with a sweet omega. It comes back to bite him when he abuses the omega, then gets dumped by his girlfriend who finds out he is two-timing, before he resigns from his company and goes into a slump. Unexpectedly, the omega picks him up from rock bottom...
