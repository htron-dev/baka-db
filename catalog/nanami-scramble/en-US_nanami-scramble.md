## Sinopse

This is the 2nd volume in Kitagawa Miyuki's series "Miyuki no Tokimeki Love Memory". The story involves a high school girl named Nanami who's very good at judo and a boy named Ren who's on the school's judo team, but is not as strong as she is. Find out how their friendship and the judo team evolves!

Note: This series was published in magazine "Coronet" (not currently one of the serialization options on MAL).
