## Sinopse

Chapter 1-3: Ore no Ki mo shiranaide
Ninomiya Kou starts as a part-timer in a Dining Café to find the place overrun by gay clients. The next shock is finding his co-worker Shudou Naoki playing around with the guys during his break. And the last shock is that he finds himself falling for same gay player.

Chapter 5: Kare no Ki mo shiraide
