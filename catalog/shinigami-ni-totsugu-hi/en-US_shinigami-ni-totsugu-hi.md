## Sinopse

Shinigami ni Totsugu Hi tells a story of a young girl who wants to die. She enters the forest where a death god lives so that he can take her live. The death godsays she won't die anytime soon, not until she marries, has children and grows them well. And so, the young girl says, "Marry me."
