## Sinopse

Introducing Kouda Chirumi, the super maid that can handle any job given to her. What is her only weakness? She will break any and everything in the house. Harada Taiki, a senior in high school and the son of manga author Harada Junichi, worries that everything in the house will end up being broken due to Chirumi. With his little brother Ken, Taiki will have to work with the clumsy Chirumi in this wacky and funny family.
