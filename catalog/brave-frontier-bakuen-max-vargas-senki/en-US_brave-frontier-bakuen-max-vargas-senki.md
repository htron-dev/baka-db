## Sinopse

Based on the mobile RPG game Brave Frontier.

This manga follows the flame swordsman Vargas in his younger days before he became one of the Six Heroes. He aspires to become the strongest swordsman in Grand Gaia and become a legend. See a different side of the heroes of old through this exciting adventure!

(Source: Batoto)
