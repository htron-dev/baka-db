## Sinopse

P and Q are two alien girls from planet Fuiren and their queen's special bodyguards. When she gets lost, they journey to Earth to find her. They try to live as normal schoolgirls, but since they can transform to and from cockroaches and frequently end up fighting off monsters, a neighborhood boy begins to gets suspicious.
