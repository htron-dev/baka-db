## Sinopse

On the morning of her entrance ceremony, first-year high school student Kofuku Ichinomiya heads toward a shrine to pray for a happy high school life. While at the shrine, she encounters Miya Hanamori practicing mikagura, a type of ritualistic dance, and a mysterious woman who guides Kofuku through the steps to this dance.
