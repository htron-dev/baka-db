## Sinopse

Misae, a survivor of the new left-wing group Ten no Koe, travels to Numazu, Shizuoka Prefecture, and begins working as a junior high school teacher, hiding her true identity. However, the classes she teaches are full of troubled students. As she teaches them, Misae gradually awakens to her true calling as a teacher.

(Source: Shogakukan, translated)
