## Sinopse

Aihara Haruka moved to a small town in the middle of the second year in high school. Far from all the life she had in big cities, she wanted to start a blog. For content, she started taking pictures of the place in her daily life, but find it difficult. A girl gave her advice, and Haruka got invited to the photography club soon after. At first, she can’t say that she’s only taking pictures for her blog, but with them, she starts to realize the fun of doing photography and learns more about it.
