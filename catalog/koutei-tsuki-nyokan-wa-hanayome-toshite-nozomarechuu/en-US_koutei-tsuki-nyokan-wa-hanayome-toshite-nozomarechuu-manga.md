## Sinopse

Lise, a knight and daughter of a baron of the Kingdom of Olwen, lost her pervious life protecting the young imperial prince Egbert. After death, she wakes up in the future with all her memories! But somehow she's become engaged to an imperial prince?

The story of a (former) lady knight... trying to dissolve her engagement?!

A romantic comedy about a reborn court lady with a secret!

(Source: Ichijinsha, translated)
