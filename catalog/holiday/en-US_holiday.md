## Sinopse

Fashion-challenged Yuki gets help from her older childhood friend Koudai to try and fix up her uncoordinated look so she can get a boyfriend. Yuki begins to fall for the closet-otaku Koudai, but when she meets his perfect ex-girlfriend Atsuko, Yuki wonders how she could ever compare!

(Source: An Endless Story)
