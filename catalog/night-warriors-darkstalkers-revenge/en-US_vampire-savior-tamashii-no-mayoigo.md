## Sinopse

Based on the Vampire Savior installment, from the DarkStalkers video game series produced by CAPCOM.

In the world of makai, there are horrible monsters that humans fear, called Darkstalkers. Lillith a fellow Darkstalker, falls to Earth (London) and befriends John. What will he do when he finds out what she is? Will Lillith survive Master Jedah's attacks??
