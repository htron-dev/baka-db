## Sinopse

It's about an Otaku guy who fell in love with this beautiful lady and tries his best to get that girl with a lot of help from 2channelers (people who goes/posts on 2ch).

This story has become very famous in Japan and they now have novels, several series of Manga (both Shoujo and Shounen), movie, drama, play, etc.
