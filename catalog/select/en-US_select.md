## Sinopse

Hirai is the number one student in his class, but he's deliberately deliquent with certain assignments in order to be "alone" with his favorite Sensei, Onoda. Hirai makes no secret of his feelings for Onoda, yet in Onoda's heart he refuses to accept Hirai's feelings...and his own. Will Hirai's persistent determination help Onoda grip the situation? or will Hirai be forced to give up?

Includes 4 other short stories.
