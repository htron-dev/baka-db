## Sinopse

Tokizou Kanda is 42 years old. He has been in unrequited love with his best friend from high school, Ryuuji Shiba, for 25 years. He has always depended on Shiba, but there is no possibility of his telling his straight friend about his feelings. "To live my life with my best friend at my side isn’t so bad." But now, at the age of 42, there is an unexpected chance. Could their relationship possible change...?

(Source: Printemps Shuppan, translated)
