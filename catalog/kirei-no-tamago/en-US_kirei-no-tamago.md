## Sinopse

Never being clean or neat, Asami wanted to change herself and so entered a hair salon school. However, as soon as she enters, troubles follow and she is immediately depressed... She meets a boy named Nii Raito who is the type to talk without reserve. She is told, "It doesn't suit you", and all of a sudden her hair is cut...?!. She must show him that she can be a good hair stylist!

(Source: Shinnen)
