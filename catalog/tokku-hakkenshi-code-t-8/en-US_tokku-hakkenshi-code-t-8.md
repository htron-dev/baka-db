## Sinopse

The souls of the Hakkenshi are reincarnated in the present! Inuzuka Shino is a high school student who shares the name of the legendary Hakkenshi, "Devotion." The name has caused him nothing but stress all his life. Now, because of his name, he's gotten himself wrapped up in a conflict with the Blight, creatures from a giant hole that has suddenly emerged in the city. An exciting fantasy/action manga from Rando Ayamine, the artist who brought you Get Backers, and writers Saki and Kentarou Kurimoto.
