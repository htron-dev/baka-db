## Sinopse

A series of one-shots that ran in Monthly Melody (before it became Melody).

1. Megane Dorobou (The Glasses Thief)
2. Conveni Honey (Convenience Store Honey); (Shounen Ai)
3. Otona no Seifuku (Adult-Uniform)
4. Reizouko ni Chocholate (Refrigerator Chocolate)

Note: Stories 1 and 3 were collected in Bus Hashiru.
