## Sinopse

Naomi, Makoto, Yuji, and Kaede all have psychic powers of some sort and make up the Supernatural Phenomenon Research Group of their school. They battle the Zeyrey, terrible monsters invisible to most people’s eyes, out to suck the life-energy of humans.
