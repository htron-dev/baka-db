## Sinopse

In Calamity Creek Shiori is a girl following in her detective father's footsteps to unlock a story of swirling hatred and murder between men and women.

Contains four works in all that display the 'mystery and romance' of the Shiori series; the title story "Instead of Saying Goodbye", "The Smile in the Mirror", "Phosphorescence of Darkness" and "BLUE ROSE".
