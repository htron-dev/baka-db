## Sinopse

In the Danna-sama to Mitsugetsuchuu series:

V.1 - Danna-sama to Mitsugetsuchuu
Sakuya and Shuji are a couple of love struck newlyweds. Shuji is a hardnosed section chief who, unbeknownst to his employees, sneaks away on his coffee breaks to gaze lovingly at pictures of his sweet Sakuya. Sakuya, on the other hand, struggles with the simplest of household chores desperate to please his loving, hardworking husband. With the sudden intrusion of a handsome next-door neighbor, Sakuya’s days are about to be just as stimulating as his sexy nights with Shuji!

V.2 - Toro Toro Honey Hen
Newlyweds Sakuya and Shuji are as lovey-dovey as ever. To celebrate, they sneak away on a steamy honeymoon to the hot springs! Too bad they don’t know Hanabusa, Sakuya’s longtime caretaker, is waiting for their return to snatch Sakuya away! Will Shuji be able to save his sweet Sakuya, or will Hanabusa finally get his way?
