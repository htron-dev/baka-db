## Sinopse

Web commercials for Livesense's online part-time job search service Machbaito. Machbaito provides a match bonus of up to ¥10,000 to individuals hired using their service. To deliver this message, they created the mascot Ichiman, a fearless hero who charges in to any situation to deliver new hires their money.
