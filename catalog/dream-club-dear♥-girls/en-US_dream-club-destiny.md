## Sinopse

Yumeno lives alone and his only interaction with girls is through a recurring dream of several beautiful girls calling to him. To his surprise, one day he sees one of his dream girls in real life! Chasing after her, he finds himself at a hostess club called the Dream Club. Are his dreams actually coming true?
