## Sinopse

Hiragana Danshi are in the mission to try to find their comrades in the world where Japanese languages have been disappeared.

This is a movie adaptation of the Hiragana Danshi multimedia project which is one of the projects that started from a monthly variety show on Nihon TV titled "Ichara," which gets ideas for 2D characters from the audience.
