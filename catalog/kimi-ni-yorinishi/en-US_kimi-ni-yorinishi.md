## Sinopse

Yamato met a pretty man, Onodera, on the night of his father’s funeral. Onodera told him, “Are you sensei’s son? You really look like him.” It bothered Yamato a lot when Onodera kissed him when they met later to tidy his late father’s works. Was Yamato a substitute for his late father?

(Source: Attractive Fascinante)
