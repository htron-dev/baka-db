## Sinopse

Ayako, a normal high school girl caught up in the experiment of an enigmatic genius, is divided in two. Except other people doesn't know she's the real one! In the midst of the confusion of sharing her life, she remembers her sister who had died suddenly. What lies beyond the feelings she remembers and the feelings she’s forgotten...
