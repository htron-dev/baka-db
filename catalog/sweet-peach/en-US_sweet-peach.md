## Sinopse

After becoming an adult, Kisaragi Touka is attacked by an evil blob of darkness. But it's alright because she is saved by her school nurse who is dressed like a maid, and a samurai girl with fox ears, who kisses her. She is then taken through a gate to another world where she is met by a hamster... who also kisses her.
