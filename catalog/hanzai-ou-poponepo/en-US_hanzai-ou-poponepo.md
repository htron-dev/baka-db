## Sinopse

The one feared by all, "Criminal King Poponepo," has been arrested, but he immediately escapes prison. During his escape, he abducts the main character, Maruki, and implants the IC chip that identifies Poponepo as a criminal into Maruki. Now, Maruki is being arrested as the new "Criminal King." Will Maruki be able to live up to the notoriety of the Criminal King?
