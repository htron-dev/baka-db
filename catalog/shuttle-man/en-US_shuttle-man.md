## Sinopse

In South Korea, students who buys bread and run errands under the force of the school bullies are called the 'Bread Shuttles'. Young-soo Lee is living a miserable life as the bread shuttle of Iljin, and then a weird, logical but weird 'Messiah' named Gun Kim shows up in the school.
