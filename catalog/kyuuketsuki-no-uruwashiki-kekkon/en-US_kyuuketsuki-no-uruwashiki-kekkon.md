## Sinopse

This is compilation of 3 loosely connected stories of vampire/human romances.

1. Itsuwari wa Tsuki no Shita de Ai ni Naru (The Lie Becomes Love Under the Moon)
2. Hanabi to Ouji-sama (The Bride and Prince)
3. Bijo to Kyuuketsuki (The Beautiful Lady and the Vampire)
