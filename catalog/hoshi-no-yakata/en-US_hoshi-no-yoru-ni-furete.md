## Sinopse

This volume is a companion piece to Hoshi no Yakata. The third chapter is a prequel to the series, showing Sakurai's past before he became the master of Hoshi no Yakata as well as how the mansion came into his possession. The remaining chapters feature short side stories that are more loosely connected to the main storyline of Hoshi no Yakata.
