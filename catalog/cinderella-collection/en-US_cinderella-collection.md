## Sinopse

Shin is a top grade student in primary grade 6. In people's eyes, she's always serious. She's a girl with poor taste, who has no interest in fashion. Half threatened by the fashion King in her class, Omone Asahi, Shin entered the Readers' model interview...! Surprisingly, she gave a stunning performance. She begins her journey to become the legendary super-model...

(Source: Fateful Encounters)
