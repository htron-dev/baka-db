A youthful railway manga! A all-girls high school railway club enters the stage! With four beautiful train-loving girls, get ready to be lured by railway travels!! This time's trip, Traveling Hotel, or as many fans call it, the Hokutousei, travels to the northernmost region, Sapporo! Everybody, let's look forward to boarding!!
