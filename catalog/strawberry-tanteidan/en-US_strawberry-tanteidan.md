## Sinopse

Sani lives together with some other orphan children at Strawberry Orphanage. A lot of people belittled them because they didn't have parents.

One day Sani crashed into a person coming out of a building but she didn't take a good look at the person, and the next day there are news that there are a murder in the building the person coming out from and the suspected time of death is around the same time Sani crashed into the person. Then Sani and the other children tried to investigate the cases to gain recognition from the people who belittled them.
