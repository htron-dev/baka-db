## Sinopse

Nao is a second-year high school student who thinks that nothing good comes from adults, and wants to stay a child forever. But thanks to Mikasa, her handsome biology teacher who's shrouded in mystery, she starts to learn more and more about the world?! How far will today's lesson go?
