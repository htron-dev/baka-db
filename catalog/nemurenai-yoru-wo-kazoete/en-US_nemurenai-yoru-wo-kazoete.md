## Sinopse

This volume contains four one-shots by mangaka Kitagawa Miyuki.

Nemurenai Yoru wo Kazoete:
Hosaka Kotomi, a minor talent in the show-biz world, used to be a rising star until last year, when the media ruined her career by reporting a scandalous relationship between she and a married producer. Now she has trouble sleeping b/c she is frustrated and lonely. The last thing she needs is an AD with a big attitude and a sharp tongue, who knows about her past, but that's exactly who she finds in Kuse Hiroto...

1 Ounce no Koibito:
Mitamura Nana met Mizutani Toshiya when Toshiya rescued her from being hit on by a sketchy guy in Shibuya. They hit it off well enough, but they went their separate ways after hanging out briefly. After all, neither of them was serious about the other until...??

Itsuka Sukida to Itte:
Sakurai Masumi and Ichii Atsuki had a brief fling over a short weekend during the summer, and that was supposed to be it. But, come fall, their paths cross again under very unexpected circumstances...

You're My Only Shining Star:
Mayama Sayuki, an extra in show-biz, is a huge fan of young, handsome action star Fujisaki Kei. On the set of one of his movies, she gets the chance to work with him, but she also meets someone who claims to be his younger twin brother...??
