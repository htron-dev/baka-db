## Sinopse

Fuji Komori, a pâtissière in training, has found a part-time job as a kitchen staff in a butler café surrounded by gorgeous guys every day! But how is she going to survive there when she's afraid to talk to guys?! To make matters worse, Koyomi Yashiro, the star butler of the café, seems to enjoy picking on her! But as Koyomi's attitude towards her changes from contempt to affection, Fuji finds herself falling for him...

(Source: Shogakukan Asia)
