## Sinopse

Traveling to America on a goodwill mission, Princess Luna of Transylvania has a vision. A life form of pure energy named Genma that destroys everything in it's path is heading towards Earth. To save the planet, she must assemble psychics to confront Genma as she partners with an alien cyborg named Vega who once fought this menace before.
