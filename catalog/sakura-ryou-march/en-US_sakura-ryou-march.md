## Sinopse

One of the last things Sakura's single father did before he passed away was to arrange for his daughter to live at his alma mater's dormitory where his friend is the coach of the baseball team. On her move there, Sakura runs into Isshiki, who mistakes her for his blind date and forces a kiss. Only later does she discover that this rake is a dormmate and the pitcher of the baseball team...
