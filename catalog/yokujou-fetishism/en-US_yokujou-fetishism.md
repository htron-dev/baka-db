## Sinopse

What do a shy hostess, a surf instructor, and a darling "living doll" have in common? They all want nothing more than to seduce and satisfy their men! When the hottest fantasies suddenly spring to life, is there anything that this gang of gorgeous girls won't do?

Fetishisms: Gone Wild takes no prisoners in tales of unbridled lust and forbidden passion! Can a hot-springs inn turn into a hideaway for the sexiest weekend imaginable? And does a class of college-bound guys have the stamina to show their teacher how much they've really learned?
