## Sinopse

Yugami Makoto likes to travel to different Hot Springs. On his 100th visit to a Hot Spring he meets a beautiful foreign girl in the bath. Stunned by her beauty, he immediately falls for her. But there is more to the girl than meets the eye. And why is Makoto forced to work at the Inn afterwards?
