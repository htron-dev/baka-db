## Sinopse

About a sexless robot, Elena, who won't die, won't grow old, and of course will never be able to reproduce. But he has superior intelligence and physical power, and also can communicate with animals. Elena suffers a lot because he lives with human, but he realizes he can never live like a human. (Source: Prisms)
