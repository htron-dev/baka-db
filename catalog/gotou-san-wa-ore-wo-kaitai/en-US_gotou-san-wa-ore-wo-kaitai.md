## Sinopse

At a gay bar, a serious-looking businessman who goes by the name of "Gotou" approaches Akira, a high school senior. Clearly not used to this sort of scene, Gotou nervously asks Akira to name his price. The look of anxiety in his eyes perks Akira's interest...A free-spirited high schooler meets a shy and pure businessman in this heart-pounding love story!
