## Sinopse

An educational film about love and respect.

Tamiko (17) and Masao (15) are cousins. They grew up together as siblings but in adulthood they wanted to wed each other. However Tamiko's sister-in-law was jealous and tried to tear them apart by forcing Tamiko to marry another man.
