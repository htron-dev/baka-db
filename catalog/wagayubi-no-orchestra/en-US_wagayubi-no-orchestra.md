## Sinopse

1914. A young teacher who has studied music, arrived in Osaka, having declined to pursue his studies in France, to work in a school for the blind and deaf-mutes. He meet Issaku, a young deaf-mute, violent because not knowing communicate. The teacher will help him and also discover at the same time the complex world of silence and misunderstanding that surrounds him.
