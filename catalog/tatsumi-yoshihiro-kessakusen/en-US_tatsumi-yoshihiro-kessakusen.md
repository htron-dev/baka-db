## Sinopse

A "masterpiece collection" of 11 stories that were released from 1969 to 1973 by the father of the gekiga movement, Yoshihiro Tatsumi.

1. Namakemono
2. Ore no Hitler (My Hitler)
3. Kuroneko no Tango
4. Ana (The Hole)
5. Wakaremichi (Forked Road)
6. Chousou (Sky Burial)
7. Taihou
8. Futon no Naka
9. Hana Ijime
10. Michikusa Onna
11. Koppu no Naka no Taiyou
