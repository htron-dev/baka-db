## Sinopse

A collection of Suzuki Julietta's debut one-shot works published in Hana to Yume, Hana to Yume Plus, and Bessatsu Hana to Yume. Includes the one-shot which was the starting point for her later series, Kamisama Hajimemashita.

1. Asa ga Kuru
2. Hoshi ni Naru Hi
3. My Bloody Life
4. Sakurachiru
5. Ogamiya Uradaichou
6. Tsubaki Ori
