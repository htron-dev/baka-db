How in the world did it turn out like this?

I am so happy to be finally reunited with my long lost twin brother, Mizuki, whom I had been separated from since our parents divorced. And to top it off, my lover, Suda, who used to be my tutor, and I are very much in love with each other. However, everything changed in one night when the most unsuspecting person forced himself on me...
