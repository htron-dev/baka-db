## Sinopse

Kenji Sakamoto, a high school student, was trying to contact a current criminal who had committed a terrorist attack in Akihabara, Tokyo. Everything is to follow the disappearance of a childhood friend, Nao, who disappeared. What happened to them? The story is told from a corner of the northern Kanto one year before the incident!
