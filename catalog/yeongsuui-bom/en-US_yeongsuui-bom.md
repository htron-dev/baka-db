Spring was never Yungsuu's favourite season, until he met this girl. From the moment he captured her with his camera, he knew he was in love. She, Iyang, was charming (in his eyes), even if in reality, she is a more disgusting, agressive tomboy. Furthermore, he found and peeked at her artbook one day, revealing something that she has a rather shocking hobby that may change his mind (or not)...
