## Sinopse

Chihaya, an apprentice in a wedding planning company, is in charge of an unprecedented wedding between two men, with his job on the line. After the Director Kazusa, who is always belittling Chihaya, praises him for his determination at work, Chihaya, in full drunken impulse, kisses him.
