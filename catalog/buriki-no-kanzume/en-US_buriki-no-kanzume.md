## Sinopse

Sakurakawa Nabiki, who dreams to become a plush toy maker, joined the "craftsmanship club", a club for people who aim to become professionals in the handicrafts and technical art field. There she meets Fukamachi Itsuru, who dreams of becoming a silverwork designer. However, Nabiki has to attend supplementary lessons, and cannot do any club activities for one week...!? (Source: M-U)
