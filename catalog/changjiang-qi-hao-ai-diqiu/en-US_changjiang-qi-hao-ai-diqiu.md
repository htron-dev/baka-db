## Sinopse

CJ7: The Cartoon loosely adopts the original plot of how a father and a son accidentally pick up an alien named CJ7.

Based on Stephen Chow's 2008 hit comedy CJ7, the film has a newly developed storyline in which CJ7 protects the environment and saves the earth.
