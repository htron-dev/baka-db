## Sinopse

Usotsuki na Season is four chapters long. In it we meet young female student Miyabi, who just entered a new school. But Miyabi has a secret, she can see dead people and other paranormal things. To solve this, she wears a special pendant that supresses her powers. However, in her new school, there will be some problems that will make her have to use her special gift!
