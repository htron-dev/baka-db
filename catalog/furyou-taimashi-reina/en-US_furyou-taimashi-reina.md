## Sinopse

Reina Suzuno, a transfer student with a Sukujan, blonde hair and loose socks looks totally like a criminal. Is she a stalker? It turns out that Reina is not a stalker, she is actually an exorcist, an excellent exorcist, the intimidating behavior she has is actually a unique method of exorcism, which exterminates the evil spirits that roam the earth.
