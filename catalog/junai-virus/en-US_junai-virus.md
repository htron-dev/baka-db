## Sinopse

Collection of 4 one shots:

1. Lover's Virus:
   Shimatsu becomes a 'guardian angel' to well known playboy Senmami. What is the reason for Shimatsu's dedication?

2. Piano Lesson:
   Loads of angst in this story about a piano prodigy who's reunited with his best friend after 4 years of absence. They then live happily ever after... or not. Tarako Kotobuki's oneshot has both clean art and a decent, angst-filled plot.

3. Loveless:
   Yasuri attempts suicide with his best friend's girlfriend. Is he prepared to pay the price for his selfish actions?

4. Monkey Punch:
   Twins that were torn apart at a tender age due to a rare disease but through times of betrayal and violence can Motouya and Uryuu still find love?
