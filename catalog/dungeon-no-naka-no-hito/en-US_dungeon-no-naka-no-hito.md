## Sinopse

Clay is a girl who belongs to the thieves' guild. After receiving rigorous training from her father, she delves past the 8th floor of an unexplored dungeon. Going deeper, she encounters a wizard who claims to be the dungeon's caretaker!? This is the story of the hidden side of the dungeon.
