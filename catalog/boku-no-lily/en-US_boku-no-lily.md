## Sinopse

This is the story about a girl who was named Lily by her grandfather, who hates her name because it becomes a laughing and teasing matter. Her grandfather passes away, and she comes to disbelieve miracles. One day she meets Abe, a guy who once knew her grandpa and, thanks to him and to her grandfather she learns to like her beautiful name and realizes the world is full of miracles.
