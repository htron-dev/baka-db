## Sinopse

The popular shoujo mangaka, Yoshino Chiaki, got confessed by his editor in charge, Hatori Yoshiyuki, who is his childhood friend—they even ended up with more than that. What will happen to Yoshino who swing between, love, friendship and work...? The fully loaded of know-how publishing industry of love, which turns editors to the extent of pale, unfolds on the stage of publishing company, Marukawa Shoten.
