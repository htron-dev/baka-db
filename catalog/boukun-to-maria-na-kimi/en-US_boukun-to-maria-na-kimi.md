## Sinopse

"From today's onward you will be my Maria!"
A group of the charismatic delinquent high school boys are quite famous in Ikumi's school. And their own leader has chosen Ikumi to be her own Maria!? Ikumi's duty couldn't be harder since she has now to take care of the gang's leader.

Contents of the book. Compilation of oneshot stories previous published on Sho-comi magazine: Binetsu Chocolate / Fukiken na Rabusain / Ubawareru Amaku Tsuyoku
