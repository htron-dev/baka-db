## Sinopse

Yosuke Ejima was gravely injured in a bus hijacking incident involving the mafia. He inherited the power of the Aztec priests to "rip hearts out" and resurrected as a special A-class assassin. By using this power, Yosuke took his beloved wife back from the mafia and should have been living a happy life, but...?!

(Source: Media Do International/Beaglee)
