## Sinopse

Despite the title this manga has nothing to do with sex, just young people rebelling against the world.

The manga is about the adventures of a girl, Kaho. In the first chapter she goes on holiday to Okinawa and falls in love with Yuki, a car-stealing punk.

Often considered Kamijo's best work.
