## Sinopse

A low-price, high-quality apartment in the metropolitan area of Tokyo is nothing short of a miracle! It exists, but there's a catch—tenants must be good-looking and pass an interview by the landlord!? This is a love comedy about what happens when beautiful people are all gathered under one roof!
