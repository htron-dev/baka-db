## Sinopse

The Marquess of Mollet—known as famous handsome playboy in the France palace—because of peculiar accident that occurred in his dominion, brought him and brother and sister twins together. Marquess immediately fell in love with Gabriella. However, the twins had a little secret...

On the other hand, recently a bandit specializing in kidnapping people named "The Wildcat Angel" appeared in the vicinity.
