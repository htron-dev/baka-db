## Sinopse

A collaboration between Kamiusagi Rope and Nipponham's Mori no Kaori product line. Meant to showcase sausages, bacon, ham, etc. that have a rustic forest flavor with no added colors and reduced sodium. Episodes were available to view by entering a code online that was given on the inner label of a Mori no Kaori's product package.
