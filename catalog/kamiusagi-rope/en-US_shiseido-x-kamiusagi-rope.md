## Sinopse

A collaboration between Kamiusagi Rope and two of Shiseido's product lines: Anessa and Fullmake Washable Base (FWB). These episodes were included on the 6th DVD of Kamiusagi Rope: Warau Asa ni wa Fukuraitaru tte Maji ssuka!? as episode 23 and 24 released on March 18, 2015.
