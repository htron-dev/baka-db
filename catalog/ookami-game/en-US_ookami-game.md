## Sinopse

"Who the hell is the wolf? A game of death and life!"

Twelve men and women suddenly found themselves in a building and get caught up in a mysterious game called "The Wolf Game." They are divided into two groups: sheep and a wolf. Whoever is the wolf must kill one of them.

(Source: Kadokawa, translated)
