## Sinopse

About to start a new chapter in his life as a college freshman in Tokyo and trying to find an apartment to stay in, Kanata Mijou comes across real estate broker Jirou Nekome, who informs him of a special property in the Yuuraku-chou district: a room with its own kitchen and toilet, and a monthly rent of 40,000 yen. Surprised by the relatively cheap rent by Tokyo standards, Kanata signs on the rental agreement and is ushered to the property by Jirou.

However, instead of the lively yet congenial district in Tokyo that he was expecting, Kanata is taken to a different Yuuraku-chou: a town where ayakashi walk the streets, a place that sits between this world and the next…
