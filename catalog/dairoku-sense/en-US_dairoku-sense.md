## Sinopse

Rui Yotsubaki, a famous illusionist boy, is an outspoken and intractable person. Sumire Shirahara is a policewoman with superhuman strength that will leave you with opened mouth. Fortunately or not, the meeting of resembling fire and water duo results in solving difficult cases thanks to Rui's ability to "feel the five senses of the dead!"

(Source: Kodansha, translated)
