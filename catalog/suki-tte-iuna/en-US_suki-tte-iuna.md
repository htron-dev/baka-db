## Sinopse

Tokita Eiji, the handsome student council president, admiration of all students, and Okamoto Oriya, the totally normal student with his totally normal school life.

Those two had nothing in common, but one day Eiji suddenly started intense love attacks on Oriya. Cold shoulder, violent treatment, none of those were able to push Eiji away. What will Oriya do, being the interest of Eiji's perverted love?!

The popular school life love comedy about the normal boy getting all loved by the handsome student council president.
