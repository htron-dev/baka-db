## Sinopse

Shinomiya Kasumi launches an undefined activity club, "Good Job Club" in her junior high school, imitating the same club from her brother's high school. In a small club room, she enjoys her school life with Seira, a sharp tongue beautiful girl, and Geraldine, a girl from Canada who communicates using a white board.
