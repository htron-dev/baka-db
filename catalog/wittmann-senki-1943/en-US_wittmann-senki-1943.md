## Sinopse

In the summer of 1943, the German military went on an offensive to retake the strategic region of Kursk on the Eastern Front. The Tiger unit commanded by Michael Wittman dealt a heavy blow to the Soviets at first, but facing a Soviet counteroffensive, his unit was forced to retreat. A story of true military greatness, as the demands of managing this retreat showed Wittmann to be the brilliant commander he was.
