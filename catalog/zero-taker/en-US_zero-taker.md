## Sinopse

A terrible virus turning its victims into bloodthirsty mutants, only the team "Zero Taker" is able to stop this new breed of criminals. Kyle, a member of the SWAT, discovers that he is a carrier of the virus itself, but is then joins "Zero Taker" to participate in the fight.
