## Sinopse

Hikaru's father was selected as a candidate for the Japanese judo team in the Olympics but dies tragically in a car accident before accomplishing his dream.

Bullied by his classmates and having no experience in judo, Hikaru will try to become stronger and follow in his father's footsteps.

(Source: Shueisha, translated)
