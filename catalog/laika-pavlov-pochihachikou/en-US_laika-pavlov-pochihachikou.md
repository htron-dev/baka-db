## Sinopse

A collection of six of Yotsuhara Furiko's short stories depicting the love between various girls.

1. Laika, Pavlov, Pochihachi-kou
2. Koi wo Egaku Hito (A Person Who Draws Love)
3. Kuchisaki Onna to Teppeki no Shojo (Lip-service Girl and Iron-Walled Maiden)
4. Karasu Nureba ni Hikarimono (That Which Shines on the Soaked Crow Feathers)
5. Koibumi Miman (Insufficient Love-letter)
6. Chopsticks
