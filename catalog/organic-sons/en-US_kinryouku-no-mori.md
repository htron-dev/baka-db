## Sinopse

Showa Year 18 (1943) is a turbulent year in Japanese history!

Shaken by WWII, Japan as a nation struggled to find its identity between feudalism and modern era! Will Sasuke’s forbidden love for his young master ever be realized when their status was shattered along with traditions…?

Also included is a sequel to Organic Sons!!
