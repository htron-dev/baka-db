## Sinopse

Sora,the son of a farmers family who lives in a small village comes to Tokyo. He visits a gay bar curious to experience sex with a man at least once before his marriage. There he meets Taiyou and the sex they have makes Sora know that he is really gay. But the coming out of a farm boy and the relationship with Taiyou may not come all that easy to achieve.
