Akihabara is a cyber host club, there, the host "Prince" is the favorite and number one of all customers and to get him they get into big battles!

Akari, a customer of the cyber host club and pines for "Prince" but she's not good at all at using a computer. So Touba becomes her instructor, but this love at first sight—will it be a love-triangle with the arrival of Touba?!
