## Sinopse

Minato confessed to Tsukasa, his childhood friend but was turned down because she had always thought of him as more of a little brother. Devastated but not willing to give up, he went to consult Tooru, an extremely popular boy in his class for advice. To his surprise, after hearing about his failed confession, Tooru confessed to him that he is in fact his first love! The curtain now rises for Minato's unbelievable triangle relationship...
