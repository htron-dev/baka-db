## Sinopse

A series following a knight in service to the Duke of Somerset, one of the supporters of the House of Lancaster in the Wars of the Roses, a conflict in 15th-century England between the supporters of two rival houses with crests of red and white roses. What is this knight thinking, as he faces the encroaching enemy...?!

(Source: Kadokawa, translated)
