## Sinopse

Yuzawa Ryou comes to Seiryou Private High School, a school known for their strong sports clubs, with a letter of recommendation to join their kendo club. But luck brings him to a tennis club instead. Without any known experience in tennis, he got into a double match to decide which pair will get accepted into the tennis club.
