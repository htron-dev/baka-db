## Sinopse

Along a certain coastline lies a little, white mansion used as an apartment. Each room bears the name of an animal—Tiger, Wolf, Snake, Cheetah, Cat, Rabbit, and Crocodile—which the tennants use to address each other. One day, a new resident comes to the mansion, and their everyday lives begin to change...
