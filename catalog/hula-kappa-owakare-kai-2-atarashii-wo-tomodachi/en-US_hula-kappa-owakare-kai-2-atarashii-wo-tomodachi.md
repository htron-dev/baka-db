## Sinopse

An alternative episode 78 created from TV viewer's ideas. In this special, when Takeda-kun returns to introduce himself again, a new character comes along with him called Ayaka-chan. After introductions of everyone, Pochi the seal comes dancing on screen with two other seals never before seen in the show.
