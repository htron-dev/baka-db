## Sinopse

The Subaki family is in for the surprise when they got visited by a woman who claims to be the wife of their eldest son. However, their eldest son's now presumed dead. While there's no proof whether she's really married to their son or not, their father decided to let the girl stay over their house. How will her presence affect the Subaki's family?
