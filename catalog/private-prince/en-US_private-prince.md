## Sinopse

Matsuno Kaoru works for a department store that sells very elegant men's clothing. At this department store, there is a mannequin named Mister Veirne that brings about good business to the store. Kaoru begins to notice how life-like Mister Veirne really is until one day, Mister Veirne comes to life! And so begins a very interesting relationship between the prince-turned-mannequin Sado Veirne and Kaoru.
