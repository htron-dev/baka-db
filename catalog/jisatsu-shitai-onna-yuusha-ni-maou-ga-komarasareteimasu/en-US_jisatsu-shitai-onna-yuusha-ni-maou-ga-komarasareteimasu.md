## Sinopse

High school girl Mana, who despairing at life committed suicide, is reluctantly revived in another world. As an immortal female hero... In desperation, Mana travels to the Devil's castle to be rid of her immortality. As even the devil can't kill Mana for good, she threatens him "If you can't kill me, I'll kill you."

New sensation, suicide desire comedy series!
