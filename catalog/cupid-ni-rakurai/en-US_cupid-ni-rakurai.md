## Sinopse

Shingo Nagata plays cupid in school by investigating the target of affection. He finds out what their ideal type is, their favorite food, gives advice when needed and receives food as a reward. One day, a group of girls who are attracted to "ice princes" ask him to find out the cause of the huge scar in the forehead of the cold Ao.
