## Sinopse

"Three seconds. That's how long you have left to live"

Just by taking off his glasses and directing his sight at the thug... just by doing so, they would die. One day Fujieda Souma saved the childhood friend he had been reunited with, Suzugamori, from a thug, by killing them with the ability 'Basilisk' he had taken from a cursed undead "Lost Man."

However, that was the start of a big misunderstanding—
