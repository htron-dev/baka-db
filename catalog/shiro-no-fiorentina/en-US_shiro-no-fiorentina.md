## Sinopse

A story about a girl who lives in Renaissance Italy and has the talent of a great artist. Without any formal training, her paintings are beyond beautiful. However, that was a time period where women were viewed as inappropriate to become a painter. But she perseveres and becomes an artist who is recognized by likes of Michangelo and Leonardo da Vinci.
