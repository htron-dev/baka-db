## Sinopse

Lately Sayumi often has a nightmare about a war in historical Japan. The dream started after she almost died because of sickness. Her mother looked disturbed when she mentioned the dream to her.
Then her family started to die one after another and all of her cousins started to become obsessed about some paintings and hidden treasures.
