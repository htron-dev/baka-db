## Sinopse

Stylist Arata has been secretly in love with Masanobu for 8 years. However, their relationship has never gone beyond friendship. Meanwhile, Masanobu's eldest son, Masaomi, has unexpectedly confessed to him. "Since I was little, I've been wanting you, Arata—" Under his manly, demanding kiss, Arata couldn't help falling for the eldest son of the man he loves…!? How in the world... A homey love story!!

(Source: Fantasy Shrine)
