## Sinopse

Hyun Min is the sole survivor of an accident which took the lives of his parents. Is there a special reason why he was the only one who survived? Is it a curse or a blessing?

One day he discovers a book in his parents belongings. His life will never be the same again... What will the future hold for him? Is there something in his family's past?

One clan is after him and another is out to protect him. What will happen in this action packed manwha straight from Korea? (Source: mangaupdates)
