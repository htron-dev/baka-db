## Sinopse

High school junior Sakimoto Wakaba is a normal girl, no matter how you look at her. Everyone around her is thinking about the future, but Wakaba still doesn't know what she wants to do. Then one day, she meets voice actress Hosaka Touko by accident, and Wakaba's future changes in a big way...
