## Sinopse

Touka Academy is an elite private all boys' school. Now, Mikaru who is acting as a princess will have to perform a real kiss on stage for the school's festival. Even though the prince is Juusaku, his best friend, it's still very embarrassing for him. Also what will happen to the relationship between the student council's Vice President Higashimurayama and President Mitaka...?!
