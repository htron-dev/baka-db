## Sinopse

Ichigo finds out a day before her high school entrance exam that her best friend has been going out with her crush. Then she gets saved by a kind guy who gives her a cake in order to cheer her up but she doesn't manage to hear his name. She is afraid that she will never meet him again, but is that what fate has in store for them?
