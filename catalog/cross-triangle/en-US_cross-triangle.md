## Sinopse

On Kurusu's 16th birthday, her classmate Ogami makes his confession. Kurusu is delighted but also embarassed because she knows the feelings of her friend Kiraha towards that boy. Suddenly, evil mysterious creatures attack them. So Kurusu must face her destiny, with Ogami charged to protect her...
