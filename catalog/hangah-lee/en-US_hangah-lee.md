Hang-A is 17 years old. She lives together with Nabi in a remote village called Yeonjang in the East. Hang-A has the gift to draw her dreams and can be characterized using their drawings to predict the future. Unfortunately, she can't draw very good, so that misinterpretation is inevitable.
