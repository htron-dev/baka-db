## Sinopse

Since he was young, Sho has been in love with his older brother’s childhood friend, Kento. However, Kento rejects his feelings as nothing more but a whim. Will this rejection drive Sho over-the-edge of madness…!? No matter how much he embraces Kento, it’s never enough! Will this obsessive love ever find a happy ending!? Also included in the collection, a story of a pervert in the train and a gangster love story. Don’t miss this hot collection of gorgeous men!

(Source: Fantasy Shrine)
