## Sinopse

Meet Makoto, a tall girl and a bit of a tomboy who has been doing some modeling, who accidentally discovers that she has one hell of a vertical leap and is persuaded to try out for a volleyball team. We follow her as she discovers her natural talent and desire to make the Olympics…
