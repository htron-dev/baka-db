## Sinopse

After hundreds years of reign, Waïlam, the emperor of Walvalia, is diying without an heir capable of taking his place : his daughter is unable to invoke the tutelary god which assures him of victory in battles. The peace within the confederation is threatened...
But on the small planet Emyülle, a woman has been hiding the truth for nearly 50 years : she had given birth to the child of Waïlam...
