## Sinopse

At Oouchi High School Judo Club, a young master reigns: Wakami Chiinke. Superhumanly strong and overwhelming beautiful, Wakami is admired and people congregate to see him. However, he has a secret, he is a man who men like. Surrounding the middle Judo boys high school livelihood, the mystery, Wakami Chiinke, approaches and the fighting gag romance begins.

(Source: MU)
