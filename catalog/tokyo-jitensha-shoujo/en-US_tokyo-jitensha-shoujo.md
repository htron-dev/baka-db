## Sinopse

Looking forward to the hustle and bustle of Tokyo, Shimano Iruka, an innocent girl, riding a bicycle from a island to Tokyo alone, meets with various companions, in order to become a "Tokyo girl." So, She starts the "Tokyo adventure" that not be seen by others.
