Haruto didn't say goodbye to his best friend Noah when he was shipped off on an investigative mission to another planet. Now, years later, after the earth has been almost destroyed and Haruto is near death in the desert, Noah appears and saves him! But Noah hasn't aged a day, and seems to be acting different...
