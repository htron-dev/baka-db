## Sinopse

Johnathan Perkins, son of Vietnam War hero "Perky" Perkins, is an U.S. exchange officer serving in British Special Air Service (SAS). He and Sergei, a Russian Spetsnaz exchange officer, participate covert SAS actions behind enemy lines alongside British soldiers in Operation Desert Storm.
