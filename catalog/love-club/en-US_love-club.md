## Sinopse

Hino Soichiro, who has paranormal powers, is tricked by his mother Souko to transfer to Seiga Private High School. Waiting for him there is his childhood friend Aoyama Itsuki, who is president of the Love Club which is otherwise known as the Ghostbusters.

Vol. 4 includes extra story not related to Rabu Club called "Scramble Night".
