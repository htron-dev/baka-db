## Sinopse

Rise, daughter of a famous actress, bumps into a little boy by accident and loses a lunch box. But when the boy comes to return the lunch box he seems to have forgotten everything about himself. Rise offers the little boy to stay at her place, and the next day the boy is not a little anymore?!
