## Sinopse

A unique, unforgettable collection of stories that examines our innermost secrets and hidden desires. Here, two female cousins learn that living together may bring them closer to each other... or drive them apart; friends discover the true meaning of "loving yourself", and clashing neighbors make a startling discovery about what is lacking in their lives. With uninhibited authenticity and pathos, passion is stranger than fiction!
