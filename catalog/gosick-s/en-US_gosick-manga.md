## Sinopse

Kujo Kazuya is studying abroad in Sovuria (a fictional country in Europe), where he meets the mysterious Victorique. Possessing a brilliant mind, which she calls "fountain of wisdom," she seems to know everything, although she never attends classes and is restricted to her room and the schools library.
