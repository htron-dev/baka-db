## Sinopse

Theatre of the events is the Chinese mainland in the late Ming period. Shisanmei, a beautiful woman with no memory reaches a lawless zone, dominated by bandits of all kinds. With the "Swords of the Seven Stars", a gift from an old hermit, she embarks on a new journey escorting Anji, a boy who aims to rescue his father that is wrongfully imprisoned. Soon a third mysterious element joins the singular group of travelers...
