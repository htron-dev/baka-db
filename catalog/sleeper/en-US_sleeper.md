## Sinopse

10 years ago there was explosion that broke seals of the demon world... that time demons came to the human world. to keep them away, special ward was created, which kept demons in one place... but not only demons.. also humans who survived explosion were imprisoned there, few years after explosion special delegation was send to check how the things going inside the ward, and to save humas left there... but there were no longer any humans... there were ouma... fusion of demon and human that could use demonic powers while having the rationality of humans....
Sleeper is a band which has 6 members... among them there is Mika, whose powers are not know yet and Row, who sometimes can't hold his demonic nature... this is their story...
