## Sinopse

Rozen Maiden 0 is set in Tokyo during Japan's Taisho era. It follows a clumsy girl named Okiku who moves from the countryside to work in a large western-style mansion in the imperial capital. One day, she discovers a mysterious doll in the mansion's attic.
