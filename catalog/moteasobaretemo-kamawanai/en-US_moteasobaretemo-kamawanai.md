## Sinopse

Yuma expresses her love for Kira, a handsome guy with great looks. She believes her love will definitely be rejected, but unexpectedly, Kira accept it.

Yuma thinks she's going out with a polite and elegant guy. However, Kira shows his unpredictable side!! A thrilling romance!!

Included one-shots: Chuujitsu naru Ookami wo, Anata shika Inai kara!
