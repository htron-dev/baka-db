## Sinopse

Takkun is a four-year-old boy. His daddy, Shuu-chan, has temporarily been posted away to New York for work, so he lives together with his mummy, Saki. Takkun is a big crybaby. But he is an honest and lovely child, growing up quickly as he goes through various experiences. So precious it hurts!! This is the heartwarming love-comedy of No.1 Idol Takkun that makes you laugh and cry!
