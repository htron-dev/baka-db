## Sinopse

Shino Chigasaki is a high school girl who dreams of experiencing a romance likes those in shoujo manga. On the day of her high school entrance ceremony, she drops her commuter pass and the guy who picks it up bears a striking resemblance to the handsome boy in her dream...! Shino has been shot through the chest with Ichinose-kun's little gesture?!

(Source: Kodansha, translated)
