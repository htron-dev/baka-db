## Sinopse

24-year-old Terada Aki is a part-time worker who's unsuccessful in job hunting. After the boyfriend she had been living with cheated on her, she ends up sharing an apartment with a former junior high school classmate, and her first boyfriend Hongyou Tomoya. About the last two years he has been helpless but by chance reacts to Aki, so he asks her to help to cure him...
