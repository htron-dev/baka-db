## Sinopse

University student Sasaki Kento is a loner. However, one day a beautiful girl called Itou Natsuki sits in the always-empty seat next to him. "This seat is free, right? If you don't mind, can I sit next to you?" With this "chance" meeting Kento's black-and-white life starts to add a bit of color.

(Source: Kadokawa, translated)
