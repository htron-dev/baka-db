## Sinopse

The Avengers team gets together to throw a surprise party for Tony Stark. Things take a turn for the worse, when zombies appear on site, and the heroes ditch the celebrations to stop the outbreak. Facing a legion of the undead, some of the superheroes become infected themselves.
