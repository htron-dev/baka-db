## Sinopse

The story of a tomboyish girl, Haneko Tonda (lit. "Hopping Jumping Girl"). Haneko has a fascination with all things western.

Created by Kitazawa Rakuten, and serialized in his manga magazine, Jiji Manga, beginning in 1928.
