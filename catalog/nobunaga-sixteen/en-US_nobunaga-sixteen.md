## Sinopse

The shrine owner's daughter, Sayaka, feel in love with her childhood friend, Nobuhiko. One day Nobuhiko body was possessed by Oda Nobunaga! Nobunaga now lives as a high school student at the time of no war, because of that Sayaka's days become so horrendous. Nobunaga 's chaser from underworld keeps appearing! Ranmaru, Nobunaga's follower and Toyotomi Hideyoshi also appeared! Along with it all, how will Sayaka love life be?
