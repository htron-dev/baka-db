## Sinopse

Tonko House has partnered with Japanese stop-motion house "dwarf" and CG studio Megalis on the new project. Tsutsumi is the directing showrunner of a stop-motion/CGI animated TV series called “Oni,” about a human girl being raised by a mythical Japanese god.

Tonko House plans to show test footage of Oni at its first-ever Tonko House Film Festival this spring in Tokyo.

(source: Variety.com)
