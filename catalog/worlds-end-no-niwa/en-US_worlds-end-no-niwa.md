## Sinopse

Tsukasa Mutou just started to live on his own. Moving in he finds a tree growing into his room, leading him to meet an "interesting" neighbor with a flourishing garden. Mutou begins spending more time at the home of his neighbor, Uemitsu, feeling drawn to more than just the beauty of the garden.
