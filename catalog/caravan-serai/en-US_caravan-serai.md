## Sinopse

Contains four different stories:

Caravan Serai - Follows the unusual adventures of two young men as they travel to different cities in the desert.

Sekai No Hate No Ido - A princess is ordered by her mother to find a fountain who's water is said to grant a person happiness, and bring her back the water in...a wicker basket?

Rakuen No Izumi - Kaze No Ya and Yukka live in the fifth world which consists of seven total worlds, and it believed that the soul will travel to sixth world when one dies. Kaze No Ya sets out to find Yukka after she disappears mysteriously one night from her family's tent.

Hakuryu Tei Kitan - A young man stops over at the White Dragon Inn while traveling in the mountains where it is said a dragon god resides, and there is an unusual abundance of various animal. After getting up to get a drink of water the young man sees something very surprising as well as worrying...
