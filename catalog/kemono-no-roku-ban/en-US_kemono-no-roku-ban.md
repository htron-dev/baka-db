## Sinopse

High school student Totono Amatsuga has a secret which no one must ever know about. His school's new counselor, Nika Agou, has a secret hope, held in her heart. When the two of them cross paths during the springtime, their everyday lives of loneliness come to an end. Their battle to fulfill their purposes, and to become the person they each want to be, starts now!
