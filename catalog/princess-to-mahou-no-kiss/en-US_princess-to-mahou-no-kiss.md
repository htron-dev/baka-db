## Sinopse

Adaptation of the Disney movie with the same title. A prince is turned into a frog and a girl kisses him, turning herself into a frog. The two frogs then go for an adventure.

Preceded by an eight-page section in color presenting the main characters (titled "Introduction of main characters" [capitalisation sic!] with "主要キャラクター紹介" [Shuyō Character Shōkai] written below) with screenshots from the movie. The comic pages are monochrome: the first third has red ink on white pages, the second third has green ink on white pages and the last third has blue ink on white pages.
