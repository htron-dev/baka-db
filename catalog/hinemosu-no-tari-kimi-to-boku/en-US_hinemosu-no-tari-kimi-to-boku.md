## Sinopse

Naoyuki is a kindhearted guy who likes to take care of others. Unfortunately, his hopelessly romantic nature often results in rejections, and his bad taste in men leads him to untrustworthy lovers with terrible morals. Meanwhile, Naoyuki’s childhood friend and next door neighbour Yowu continues to harbour a one-sided crush on him and watches sadly from the sidelines...
