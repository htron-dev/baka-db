## Sinopse

Despite being a wealthy elite girl, Tamamura Komachi is too arrogant, leaving her with zero friends and a truant lifestyle. Her nickname is Inkya-Hime (The Gloomy Princess). To return her to a model lifestyle, the friend-type humanoid Nanado came along! Will she be able to get Komachi to attend school and make friends!?

(Source: Houbunsha, translated)
