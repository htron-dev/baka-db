## Sinopse

Hot Triangle is a collection of oneshots.

Miharu, an ordinary high school student, is always accompanied by two very popular, good-looking guys. Just because he's weak and helpless... Miharu thinks that somehow, he should become more independent, but his actions set the suppressed emotions of those two guys on fire...!?
