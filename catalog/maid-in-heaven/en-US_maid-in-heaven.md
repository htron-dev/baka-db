## Sinopse

In place of his sick grandma, Midori must become a maid for the young and handsome Asagi. Midori spends every day with his master, servicing his every need. However, every time Midori gets close to his master’s ‘satisfaction,’ there is an interruption. Will this pretty-boy maid ever get to complete his services? (Source: M-U)
