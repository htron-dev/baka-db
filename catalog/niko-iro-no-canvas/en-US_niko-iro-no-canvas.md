## Sinopse

The manga centers around Niko Sorano, a girl who leaves her hometown in Kagawa, a small prefecture located on the island of Shikoku, to go to Osaka and study at Osaka University of Arts. She is late on her first day of class and meets some unique students, such as the handsome producer Soichiro and the beautiful illustrator Mizuki.
