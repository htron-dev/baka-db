## Sinopse

Welcome to a dazzling girl's school life drama, where a fanatical Vice Principal will stop at nothing to bring her all-girl's school back to order. But the delinquent 1st year Jitsuko has her own priorities... which may have to do with a certain older homeroom teacher.
