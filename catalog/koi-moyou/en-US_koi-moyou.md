## Sinopse

Compilation of short stories:

-   KOI-MOYOU ~Sururi~ (Speedy)
    Kanami agreed to date Hoshitani on impulse, but over time as she gets to know Hoshitani better, she learns that he slept with his ex-girlfriend, Sae, which makes her feel insecure. This is doubled when Sae's boyfriend, Takatou, kisses Kanami...

-   KOI-MOYOU ~Tokubetsu~ (Special)
    The popular teacher of the school, Sagara, finds a student, Nanami, looking for something in the grass. As he watches her struggle to find a lost object, he finds himself falling in love with her.

-   KOI-MOYOU ~Kokoro no Akira~ (Whereabouts of the heart)
    High school doctor Mai (24) has a "white robe obsession" as her first love was a doctor who treated her when she was 5 years old. She's currently dating a doctor, Konno, who she is deeply in love with even though she knows he's cheating on her. One day she finds someone who looks exactly like her first love.

-   KOI-MOYOU ~Healing Love~
    I was in love, and I was hurt to the extent that I would no longer fall in love... The guy I met was broken-hearted, too.
    A friendship and love that started with a shared passion for books.
