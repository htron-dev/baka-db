## Sinopse

Quick tempered, dirt poor brawler Kotarou Mikata can't hold down job because he always ends up beating the crap out of some errant customer. Just when he's at wit's end, "S" offers Mikata a job as a "hero." But what exactly does it mean to be a hero? Join Mikata on his WTF journey from brawler to hero in the action romantic comedy "Seigi no Mikata"!
