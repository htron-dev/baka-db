## Sinopse

The pilot of Inuwari-san, it was originally meant to be a TV series before being canceled. The pilot was screened at the 2009 Tokyo International Anime Fair and developed as part of Miyagi Prefecture's Miyagi e-Brand Establishment Support Project.

Inuwari-san is a canine (inu) police officer (omawari-san) who loves law, order, and tea. Inuwari-san vows to be the infalliable "sweeper of evil."

(Source: ANN)
