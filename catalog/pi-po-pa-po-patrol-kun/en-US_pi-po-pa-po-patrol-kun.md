## Sinopse

Patrol-kun is a talking police car, existing in a world where talking vehicles and humans live together. He starts his first day at a police station meeting up with his partner, a human police recruit by the name of Hajime. Together, they patrol the streets of Tokyo, learning various life lessons along the way.
