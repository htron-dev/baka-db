## Sinopse

It's Summer in Okinawa, 2112, and Teppei Sugo is a pilot taking part in military operations of the 15th joint task force of the National Defense Forces. Three months later, unmanned armed drones attack Tokyo and the Ministry of Defense begins. In order to investigate the case, Tomomi Masaoka, an Enforcer within the Criminal Investigation Department's Division 1, goes to the National Defense military base and, with the assistance of Inspector Aoyanagi and drone pilot Sugo, uncovers the truth behind the case.

(Source: Psycho-Pass Wiki)
