## Sinopse

A special booklet that was bundled with Comic Yuri Hime volume 19 containing mini-sequels to stories from Yuri Hime, Yuri Hime S, and Yuri Hime Wildrose. The booklet was only delivered to customers who have subscribed to Comic Yuri Hime magazine.
