## Sinopse

Earth launched an initiative to protect themselves from an incoming asteroid by constructing a moon base. From there, the objective was to use missiles to obliterate the asteroid. However, the plan fell through and the asteroid crashed into the planet. But during these events, a certain individual was left on the moon, while everyone else returned to Earth. His name is Moon Yoo, the last man alive.
