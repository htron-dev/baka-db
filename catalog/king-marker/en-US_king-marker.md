## Sinopse

Rei, a young high schooler gets a strange mail on her new cell phone. An infinite symbol. When she opens it, a young boy magically appears and tells Rei that she must grade his performance as king. Now, this young boy, Rouel, is under the care of her and her younger brother, Ichita. So what adventures will begin from opening this mail?
