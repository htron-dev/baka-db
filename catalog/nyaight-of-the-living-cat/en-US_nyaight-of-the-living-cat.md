## Sinopse

The world is now ruled by cats. A pandemic breakout of the virus NN has turned humans into cats one after another and the remaining ones have to fight for their survival. This is the story of a man who has lost many of his friends to the cats and his fight to save humanity.

The world's eagerly awaited new age of zombies has arrived! A sudden and shocking cat horror.
