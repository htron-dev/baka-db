## Sinopse

Izumi Shou is the tough girl with a weakness for pretty boys. Takamura is the tough boy, and definitely not pretty. When he steals her first kiss, she wants him dead – and she’s capable of doing it. Thus begins a relationship that runs 30 volumes, and the art style gets more involved as the relationship does.
