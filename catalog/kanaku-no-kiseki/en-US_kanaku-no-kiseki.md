## Sinopse

A thousand years ago there was a scarlet haired witch named Marl. Marl was a noble woman, she traveled around the world, teaching people magic to benefit their lives.

I am Kanaku, a graduate from the school of magic. To fulfill a personal wish, I decided to retrace Marl's footsteps and visit the epitaphs she left in Arencia.

"I'm going to tag along with to visit the epitaphs!"
Julie, a charming girl who is gifted with magic talents suddenly decided to accompany my journey. It was a bitter sweet trip, which guided us to the unexpected truth, the beginning of a fantasy journey!

(Source: back cover of the novel)
