## Sinopse

With the Kansai Gang on his heels, Mishima fled to Tokyo and was taken in by Segawa, the young, next leader of the Jinbo Group. Even though, Segawa appears cool and detached on the outside, Mishima has made up his mind to follow Segawa no matter what. However, Mishima learned that Segawa has a sworn brother…

Also included in the book, 3 one-shots of related characters.
