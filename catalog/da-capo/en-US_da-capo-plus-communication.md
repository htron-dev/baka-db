The second series of novels based on visual novel "Plus Communication".

Each book contains different subtitle:

1. Sakura no Rasen (桜の螺旋)
2. Sakura no Kousaku (桜の交錯)
3. Sakura no Dolce (桜色のドルチェ)
4. Sakura-tachi no Partita (桜たちのパルティータ)
