## Sinopse

Takashi Hinami is always transferring schools because of his parents' jobs, and he has gotten pretty bored of the attention. He uses the rumors about him to achieve his goal, which is to maintain his mediocre daily life and to quietly go on to the next school, until the rumors about his classmate Sakura make him take a special interest in him. It seems like Takashi isn't as ordinary as he pretends to be...
