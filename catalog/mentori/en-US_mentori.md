## Sinopse

The "Mentori" mascot originates from a series of line stamps meant to evoke the feeling of "mendou da ga toriaezu henshin" (it's annoying, but I guess I'll reply). The anime follows the birds in a gag-filled comedy quite often breaking the 4th wall.

(Source: ANN)
