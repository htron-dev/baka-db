## Sinopse

Asahi, Tamaki and Yakata are high school kids are smart. They wanted to surprise their homeroom teacher who had moved, by making independent films using an 8 mm movie camera was accidentally discovered by them. But, it turns out making movies is not easy and cheap! Many hurdles must be passed. Can they solve it with a little time?!
