## Sinopse

The action serial made popular in the Robot anthology.

The first book (released February 2008) collects the first five "DRAGON FLY" chapters in full colour, retouched and corrected, and with the addition of a prologue and 0th story that ties the story together.
Also includes bonus maps, biographies and chronology guides.

It is unknown whether the further chapters from Robot (and Gelatin) will be collected into separate volumes as well.
