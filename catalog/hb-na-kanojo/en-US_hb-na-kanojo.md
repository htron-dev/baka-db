## Sinopse

Being a hentai mangaka isn't exactly an illustrious position. Its scandalous content doesn't usually mean the writer is always in sexy situations! This is not the case for Sonata and Subaru, however, a mangaka and her assistant, who learn that the best way to write about sex is to have sex. Things get twice as fun when Sonata's hot rival, Azami, gets in the mix and wants a bite of Subaru, too!
