## Sinopse

Innocent Meguru has grown up under the watchful eye of her overprotective older brother. She's gone to all girls schools all though middle school, but now that she's going to a co-ed high school, she's ready to experience "love!"

On her way to school, she encounters Arima, a boy who's not so innocent. But will Meguru's brother let her figure out what love is...!?
