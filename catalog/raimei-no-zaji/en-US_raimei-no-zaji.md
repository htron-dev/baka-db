## Sinopse

Zaji, a young stranger who is characterized by carrying a scar below his left eye and a cold stare, escapes from the organization "Home" to find someone. Having grown up and been educated, trained and probably changed to "Home" as a soldier first, they send their murderers to make him pay his betrayal.
