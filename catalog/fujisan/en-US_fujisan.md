## Sinopse

The "Fuji-san," Mount Fuji, is a national symbol for the Japanese: the volcanic mountain snowfall is a symbol of strength and wisdom. Akira Saso relate by a collection of short stories linked by a common theme: the emergence of this giant majestic systematic, disrupting the lives of characters stuck in random existences.
