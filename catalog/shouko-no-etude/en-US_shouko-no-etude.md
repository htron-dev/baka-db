## Sinopse

A family drama. The young Shouko lost both her parents in a car accident and, desperate, asks to be hosted by her uncles, working for them full time. She begins a life of sacrifices and faces hardships to integrate family, because of jealousy of her cousin. But happiness is lingering around the corner?
