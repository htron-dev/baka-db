## Sinopse

A young witch, Ema, saves the life of a small human boy who is being hunted for his soul by other witches. Ema takes it upon herself to raise the poor child as her own, but can a human boy and a witch really become family? She will have to fight to overcome the hatred between witches and humans as well as her own dark secret in order to protect her new happiness.
