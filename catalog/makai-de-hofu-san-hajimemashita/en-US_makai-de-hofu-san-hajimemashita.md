## Sinopse

Minazuki Hoshi who served as the male carer in the nursery school is suddenly summoned by the demon king. Threatened, Hoshi decides to take care the job as a male carer of a magician and monster children... Life begins with surprises and a temptation of awkwardness with cute girls such as Liberia of Satan, Little Maiden of Succubus or Kufei of the Demon Wolf.
