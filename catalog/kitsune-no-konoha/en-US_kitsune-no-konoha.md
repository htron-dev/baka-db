## Sinopse

Konoha is a wild fox that has fallen in love with Mizuno, a convenient store worker. Wanting to get closer to him, she transforms into a human (with a tail), just to do it for her loved one. Although, it makes Kureha, her best friend became worried of her...?

A first love story of a sweet yet naive wild fox with the addition of the background of a nice and calm town with hills.

Included one-shots:
Kaguya to Takeru
Inari-kun no Kyuudou
