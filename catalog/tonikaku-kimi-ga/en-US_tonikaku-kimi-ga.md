## Sinopse

Koito Matsuda, daughter of a policeman, believes people are pure and hate crooked behavior. At her first day of high school she meets Taiga Akiyama, who repeated a year and was known for cause problems. However Koito still believes he is a good person and tries to get him fit in their class.
