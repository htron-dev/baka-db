## Sinopse

This volume contains two short stories by Akaishi Michiyo-sensei.

News 6:30

Aspiring to follow in the footsteps of her idol Niimi Toshihiko, a popular young news anchor, Jinnouchi Akira studies hard, applies, and is accepted as MATV's newest newscaster hire. But many trials await her there involving success at her job and an unrequited love...

Soshite Tenshi wa Hohoenda

College student Narusawa Setsuka's life has been filled with tragedies, which is why she is unphased when someone at her university says he's never seen anyone so ill-fated. She gets by somehow always with the love and support of her protective boyfriend Kurusu You. But is what You provides simply just innocent love and support...??
