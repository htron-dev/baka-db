## Sinopse

Floor staff Yuki Nanase fell in love with the popular chief, Junnosuke Higuchi. However, Juunosuke’s entire attitude, discipline, perseverance, and diligent lead to one fact; Junnosuke loves the boss of the Marina Café. This was the cafe where Nanase and Junnosuke worked with childhood friend Yura Manabe. Seeing the disciplined Junnosuke makes Nanase realize that she could not reach him. That is enough to make Nanase give up and supporting Junnosuke's relationship with Yura. Moreover, she saw Junnosuke and Yura was embracing. One day, Marina Café has a "Cake Parade". Nanase was "disturbed" by a naughty guest. Junnosuke helps her out then and then suddenly, he expresses his feelings for Nanase. Nanase though is rather happy to not answer him. This makes Junnosuke confused, did Nanase like him or not? Plus, if their relationship was known to the guests as well as the fans of Junnosuke, Marina Café would be empty...
