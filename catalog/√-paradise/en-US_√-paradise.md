## Sinopse

Haruhei Sakura entered in an exam school, then he fell in love with a girl who also goes to the same school. She is the daughter of a millionaire, but queerly works a part-time job in a cabaret. He wishes her to quit her job and hope she will be intent on studying for the entrance examination of college with him.
