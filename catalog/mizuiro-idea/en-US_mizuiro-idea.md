## Sinopse

A light hearted comedy which revolves around the hearty efforts of Wakaba to make her dream come true. She becomes friends with Hidaga in the hopes that they can join the school's manga club. However, the manga club has not been active lately due to something that happened in the past and Hidaga seems to be hiding something too. Wakaba has plans for the manga club to ultimately achieve her dream.
