## Sinopse

Nakahara Mitsuboshi is a young man who holds many part-time jobs to pay for his mother's hospital fees. His world changes when he mistakenly gets a job at a French restaurant. Feeling challenged and intrigued, he sets off to delve into the world of French cuisine.
