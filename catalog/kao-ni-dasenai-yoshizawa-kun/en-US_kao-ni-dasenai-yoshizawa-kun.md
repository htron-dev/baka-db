## Sinopse

High school sophomore Yoshizawa Yoshio is unable to show expressions on his face, leading to constant misunderstandings from those around him. One day, after an encounter with classmate Jun Souma, Yoshizawa discovers that Souma is able to read his expressions.... and so this fateful encounter begins to change his daily!
