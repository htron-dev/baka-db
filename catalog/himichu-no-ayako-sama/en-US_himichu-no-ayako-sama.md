## Sinopse

In a school where the children of the elites gather to study, there exist a society dedicated to the eradication of anything they deem 'poisonous' mainly, otaku goods. It just so happens that on the day of inspection Rentarou Ootaki brought his beloved doujins goods to school after waiting in line all night for them, how will he fair?
