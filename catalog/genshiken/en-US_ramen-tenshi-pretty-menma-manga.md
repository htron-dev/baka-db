## Sinopse

Since the Meiji Era, Torigara Kaoru's family has always operated a ramen shop out of their home. But ever since Torigara's father passed away, the ramen shop has lost its fame. In order to save the shop, a Ramen Angel appears and intrudes on his life and helps him save all that is Ramen!
