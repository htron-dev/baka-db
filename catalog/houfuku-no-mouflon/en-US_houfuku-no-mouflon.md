## Sinopse

Comedian, Sano Youhei, is aboard an airliner when it is hijacked by terrorists. In an act of bravery, he helps stop the terrorists and helps land the plane safely. It is an event that drags him into a conspiracy much larger than a single hijacking of an airliner.
