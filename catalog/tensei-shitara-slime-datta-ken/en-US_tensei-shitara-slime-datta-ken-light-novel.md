## Sinopse

After being killed by a passing robber, a normal 37-year-old guy finds himself reincarnated in another world as a blind slime with unique skills. With a new name "Rimuru Tempest" he got after meeting his new friend, the "catastrophe level" Storm Dragon Veldora, he starts his slime life in another world with his increasing number of minions.

(Source: Guro Translation)
