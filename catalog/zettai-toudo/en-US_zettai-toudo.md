## Sinopse

As a result of complications with her first love, Minato has remained a virgin even as a working adult. Turning her back on men, she spends her days adoring a hot guy in a game. However, one morning she wakes up to find a guy who looks just like her first love right next to her. Who is this man? Does this mean she will have another chance at love?
