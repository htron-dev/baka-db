## Sinopse

Eiji Otsuka presents a comical horror fantasy based on the theme of urban legends. This tells the story of a young folklore scholar’s success in solving cases assigned by the urban legend problems handler, nicknamed “Todenka,” along with the help of Slit-Mouth Woman and Man-faced Dog.

(Source: JManga)
