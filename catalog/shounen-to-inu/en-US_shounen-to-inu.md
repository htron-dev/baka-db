## Sinopse

Collection of one-shot stories about different dogs and their relationships with their owners.

1. Kubiwa: Shounen to Inu (Collar: The Boy and the Dog)
2. Sensei no Inu (The Teacher's Dog)
3. Lead: Seinen to Inu (Lead: The Man and the Dog)
4. Honoo no Inu (The Dog of Flames)
5. Obaa-san no Inu (The Granny's Dog)
6. Amígo Inu... (Amígo Dog)
7. Shiroi Yamainu (The White Mountain Dog)
