## Sinopse

Lerew lives in a small fishing village on the coast, and trades with a young boy called Einstein for medicine for his father. But one day, while attempting to fish in a dangerous area, Lerew is thrown overboard and attacked by sharks. However, even with his body mangled beyond recognition, he still doesn't wish to die...
