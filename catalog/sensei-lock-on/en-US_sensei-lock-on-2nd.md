## Sinopse

A year had passed since Kadokura Kyouko and Yamato Kazuki became a couple. Yamato pursues his dream to become a teacher. He is now a trainee teacher and Kyouko is his boss, but their status as a couple should not be found out.

Will Yamato manage to safely become a teacher or will their relationship get in the way of training?
