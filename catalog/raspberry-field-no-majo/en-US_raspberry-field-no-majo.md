## Sinopse

Alice attends an all girls' academy where all the students are required to live in the dorms. One day, in the dead of the night, she meets a boy--a devil who lives on secrets. Alice carelessly makes a contract with the boy and becomes a witch. Now, she will have to gather secrets for her master...!

(Source: Aerandria Scans)
