## Sinopse

Kaede, the heiress of the Kisaragi ninja clan has decided to run out on her arranged marriage, and take matters into her own hands. Searching for a companion on her own, she meets the most unlikeliest of guys; a total weakling. However she is soon intrigued by his courage, and chivalry. Can love blossom between these two kids from completely different worlds?

(Source: Solaris-SVU)
