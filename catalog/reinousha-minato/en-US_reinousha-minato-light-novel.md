## Sinopse

In our era, science is prospering more than ever. However, lurking in the corner of our world are paranormal creatures known only by a small number of peoples. Those creatures are called "Strangenesses". Obviously, some undertake the job to hunt down the Strangenesses. Shinto priestesses, monks, their designation varies, but among them is a man completely different from everyone else.
Minato Kujou is a cynical and sharp tongued young man who has no spiritual abilities at all. He is looked down upon by others in the spiritual profession and referred to as the “Zero Ability Individual”. Even so, to everyone's surprise and indignation, that man is successfully hunting down Strangenesses...
