## Sinopse

This is the story of a cowboy named "Wild Shot" Rock. He rides on his trusty buffalo, Gem, and with him he carries a shiny, red revolver. Word around town says his magic revolver has the ability to fire an unlimited amount of bullets. Follow Rock & Gem as they travel across the vast desert in search of danger and adventure!
