## Sinopse

75, 60, 45, and 20 years old. At the max, an age gap of 55 years. The four sisters were born from their father's unfettered sense of freedom. Though they've all been estranged with another, they are forced to reunite due to their father's failing health.

(Source: Kodansha, translated)
