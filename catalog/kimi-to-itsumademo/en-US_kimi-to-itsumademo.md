## Sinopse

"Forever with You" - a collection of the famous manga artist Naoki Yamamoto. The two works presented in this collection are made in the characteristic style of the narrative's bleak and attention to detail. The first story is called "Forever With You" tells the story of a girl who falls into a stranger's apartment. In the second we watch the girl who dreams of a very strange dream. Unremarkable at first glance subjects translate into unexpected endings.
