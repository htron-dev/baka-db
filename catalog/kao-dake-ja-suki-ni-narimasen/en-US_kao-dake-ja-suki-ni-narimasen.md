## Sinopse

As an incoming freshman, Chiken Sana chose to attend Minowa Sougou High School for its flexible school policies and also because her “eye candy” - Ugou Kanato - is rumored to be a student there. When she finally meets him due to a series of coincidences, she gets pulled into something unexpected…?!

What will happen next in this ultra fast-paced love comedy?!
