## Sinopse

A collection of short stories about love: Doing what you love, being with the one you love, and falling in love…

1-3. Doujinshi wo Tsukutte Mita. (I Tried Making a Doujinshi.) 4. Futatsu Negai. (Two Wishes.) 5. Suki to Ienai Koto (I Can’t Say I Love You) 6. Chiku Taku (Tick Tock) 7. Suki mo Yoi (Love Is Good)
