## Sinopse

With a letter as his guide a young man named Rui visits Rio. "I thought that you'd be crying by yourself," he says as he caresses Rio's cheek as if he was wiping away his tears. Although bewildered by Rui's actions initially, Rio soon realizes that he has been saved by those actions and from some point has started to look forward to them...
