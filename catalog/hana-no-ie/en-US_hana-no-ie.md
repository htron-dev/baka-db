## Sinopse

Kiichirou Nakai decides to help his friend, Hiyori Fujita out. Little did he know, he just got involved in a situation where he has to dress as a woman and pose as the bride of a kabuki actor! While acting as the body double of a runaway bride, Nakai falls in love with kabuki. The curtain rises on the stage of passion and jealousy...!!
