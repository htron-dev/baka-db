## Sinopse

Satoka is the daughter of a local shrine and lives together with Kotori, who may look like a normal and energetic girl, but is actually a matchmaker god in training. The story revolves around Satoka's childhood friend Shiori and the mysterious Hiwa dealing with their everyday lives and the relationships around them.
