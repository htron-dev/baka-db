## Sinopse

By a shiny day, the older brother of Shunta, a potter, made a beautiful clay doll looking like a girl and called Nene.
She was so cute that Shunta couldn't help wishing that she was a real girl... and then a miracle happened, the next day, Nene was alive, moving and creating living dolls by kissing clay dolls.
So begins the life of Nene the doll, a shiny life soon disturbed by a strange inventor, creator of a male android.
