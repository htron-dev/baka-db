## Sinopse

Free Life Fantasy Online... a.k.a. FLFO. Kotone Tsukishiro receives this game as a gift from her little sister and starts playing. The character's race she has selected was... one of the "Inhuman" races, usually used by enthusiasts. A race said to be bad even among Inhuman races that are already known to be quite difficult: a zombie.

(Source: Shousetsuka ni Narou, translated)
