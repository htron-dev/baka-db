## Sinopse

An ancient mask broke the peaceful life of the mysterious world and an inevitable war is about to break out. In order to stop this war, the new patriarch Lala invited the his human world friend Yugo to come back to help. But the mischievous Yugo brought her father Wang Dashan into the mysterious world. In the face of sudden changes and fear of the unknown, Wang Dashan, is scared and embarrassed losing his 'cool and reliable' dad cred. But his new take on the world might be what it needs to help stop the war for his daughter.
