## Sinopse

During his day off, chef Tsuburaya comes across Tatsumi, an arrogant customer at his cafe. Unaware of the cafe’s holiday, Tatsumi scolds him for not working that day as he had been looking forward to Tsuburaya's cooking. Tsuburaya offers to cook for him at home instead, leading to a series of meetings that slowly unravel their hidden desires.
