## Sinopse

Typical schoolgirl Nanami has just started high school and can't wait to spend as much time as possible with her first crush. But her blissful high school days take an unexpected turn when the sword-wielding demon slayer, Tomotaka, confronts her, claiming Nanami has an evil power within her that must be destroyed.
