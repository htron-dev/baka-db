## Sinopse

Seikakutou Geimu High School, a prestigious high school which every young lady gamer in Japan longs to be in. This is the tale of the hot-blooded battles between that school's various "e-Ojousamas", including the number one e-Ojousama in the country and the school, Ryuuko Shouryuuin.

(Source: Shueisha, translated)
