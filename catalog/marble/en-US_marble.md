## Sinopse

Chikamori is a chef with top-rate culinary skills, but is clumsy and unsociable. He would be in trouble without Kaji, the meticulous and attentive sommelier. Chikamori's trust in Kaji turns into affection, and then to love, but Kaji seems to be completely oblivious.
