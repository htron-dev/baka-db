## Sinopse

The story is about 2 high school boys, Suzuki Taira (cheerful idiot) and Satou Tomoharu (smart and cool). When they learn that their school will be shut down at the end of the school year, they decide to become "wish granters" and help other students solve their problems so that everyone will have a fun last year and leave the school without any regrets.
