## Sinopse

A short film screened simultaneously with Doraemon Movie 25: Nobita no Wan Nyan Jikuuden and Pa-Pa-Pa the ★ Movie: Perman - Tako de Pon! Ashi wa Pon!. It reflects on the history of the previous Doraemon films as well as introduces the characters (as well as characters from F. Fujio Fujiko's other works, not just Doraemon-exclusive ones).
