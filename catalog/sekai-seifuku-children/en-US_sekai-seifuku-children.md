## Sinopse

When Akane Morito moves to another high school, he meets the chance to take a turn in his gray and monotonal life. Seeking for a hobby club, the ends in the Mistery Club.

Once in, they wouldn't let him to get out without being a member. Then he discovers the objective of the club: to conquer the world.
