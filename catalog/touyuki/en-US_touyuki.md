We say that by following the precarious road of Sinolek, we arrive in a place where all the dreams come true. It is to concretize his that Ichizo, a small 10-year-old boy with a hat and a big staff, takes a journey. But on his road, he will have to cross swords with dream eater devils, will his dream be big enough to resist them? [Glénat]
