## Sinopse

As another day of hard work in the world of business comes to an end, another bloody seat-snatching battle between tired office men trying to return to their homes begins. However, there is one man who is rumored to win a seat for himself every single day on the fully packed trains of Japan. His alias: Meteorite Section Manager.
