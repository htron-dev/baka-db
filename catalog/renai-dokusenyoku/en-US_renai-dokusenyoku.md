## Sinopse

Takano is an art teacher who was almost raped by one of his students, but is rescued by his colleague, Ogata. Takano expresses his gratitude, only to have Ogata blackmail him. Takano thinks he has no choice but to consent to Ogata's request. However, as time passes, Takano discovers Ogata's true motives for their "relationship."
