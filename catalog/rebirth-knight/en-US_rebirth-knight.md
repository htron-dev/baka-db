## Sinopse

There are mysterious masked-men trying to conquer the earth!! And to defeat them, the state started a project team called "Rebirth Knight." Kang Chan, one of the strongest high schoolers, meets a girl named Chaewon and falls in love at first sight. However, Kang Chan hears from Chaewon that she is a member of the Rebirth Knight, and decides he wants to be a member too...
