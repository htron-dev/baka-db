## Sinopse

By day, Aoi Kannazuki and Haruka Minazuki are mere students. Whenever danger strikes, however, the two transform into the Twin Angels, an unstoppable force for fighting crime. Will the Twin Angels be able to save their city, or perhaps even the world while maintaining their "secret" identities?
