## Sinopse

Rio Kagami is now grown up and in college. He's also doing part time job as a model. That's when he met an editor named Ritsu Asahina, who has the same black hair and same green-colored eyes similar to his unrequited love Shingo. But are they of same species too? And how will their relationship turn out to be?
