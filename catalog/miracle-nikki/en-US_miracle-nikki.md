## Sinopse

Nikki was just an average girl who loved to coordinate clothes, but one fateful day, she dreams of a magical woman who begs for her help to save her world. Suddenly, she's in a different world, and her plush Momo can talk! Nikki will have to use her coordinating abilities to survive in this world...
