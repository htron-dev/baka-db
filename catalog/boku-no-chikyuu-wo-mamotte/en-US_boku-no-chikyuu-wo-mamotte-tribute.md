## Sinopse

A tribute volume for the Boku no Chikyuu wo Mamotte series, which has been running in Hana to Yume, released on the occasion of the 35th anniversary of the magazine.

Contains a chapter from the original artist Saki Hiwatari and six Shorts stories somehow related to Boku no Chikyuu wo Mamotte by:

-Furumiya Kazuko
-Hanamura Ui
-Fujitani Touko
-Yuwa Shiori
-Ikemi Runa
-Hirama Kaname
-Okabe Asagi
-Eiri Kaname
