## Sinopse

Ignition, telekinesis... the present day where supernatural abilities have become a natural occurrence...

In the middle of such an everyday life, Kamiya Kazato, is one of a small number of people in the world completely powerless. However, on his birthday he had a sudden manifestation of an ability which allows him to "rule" any sounds, and he was also the first person in the entire world who manifested such a rare ability... Kazato's ability went berserk, and the "Self-Defense Force," an administrative organ that maintains public order in the human society, because of the treat he possesses, acknowledged him as an elimination target. During his desperate escape from them, the evil organization who calls themselves "Anonymous," offers Kazato a helping hand――

(Source: Overlap, Novel Updates)
