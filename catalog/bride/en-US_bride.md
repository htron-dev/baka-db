## Sinopse

A beautiful tale about a 20 year old divorcee who cannot bare children and her second marriage to the 16 year old, fourth son of a prestigious family. The son who was ill since childhood became forsaken by his family. But in order to prevent his spirit from haunting the family after his death they forced him to marry Sou.
