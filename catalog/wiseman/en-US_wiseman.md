## Sinopse

Matoba is a reporter in Japan. He went to Arizona to search for a missing poet named Saeko. While he was at the desert of Arizona, Matoba met a strange man called Godo. This encounter resulted him to go through countless supernatural events that he tried to explain with scientific resasons but failed. Later, Matoba discovered he was on the way to became a "Wiseman" and would learn Godo and Saeko's objective that would change the whole world.
