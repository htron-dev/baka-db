## Sinopse

The Saotome group's heir Chihiro was abducted by the Russian mafia and for two years he was "trained" in a prostitution ring. After his two bodyguards were finally able to rescue him, Chihiro came back to Japan and took over his dead father's place as the head of the Saotome group. However, trouble is brewing underneath the calm...
