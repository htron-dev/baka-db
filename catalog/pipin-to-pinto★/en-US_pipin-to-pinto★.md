## Sinopse

Pinto is a 14 year-old boy who lives with his uncle and has a passion for photography. One day Pinto spots a girl on top of the town lighthouse, who flies off of it and lands squarely on his head. It turns out that this is his uncle’s wife’s little sister, named Pipin, who has come to live with them. Apparently, Pinto's uncle married an alien…
