A collection of short stories:

-   Ringo Hoppe no Koi (also: Blushing Love; Apple-Cheek Love)
    Takami has a HUGE problem! She blushes at the drop of a hat, so everyone knows what she is thinking! She finally makes a decision to stop letting her fear of embarrassment control her -- she gets a job where her crush is working so that she can confess her feelings to him... but then things go awry in an unexpected way!
