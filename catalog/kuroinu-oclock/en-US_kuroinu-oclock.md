## Sinopse

Onoda Aki is a high school boy, who can accurately predict a time of death. One day he finds out about his grandmother's imminent demise and looks for a better end for her. His searchs lead Aki to "Black Dog" company, which arranges an ideal death for whom, who already fated to die.
