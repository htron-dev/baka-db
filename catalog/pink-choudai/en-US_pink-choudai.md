## Sinopse

This is a compilation of four cute stories.

1. The girl feels insecure and unconfident in her boyfriend's love fore her. She wanted to find out and only thought of one solution--- that is, to play around?!

2. I love you, You love her, He loves me. What should i do in this mess confused

3. Up to what extent will a girl do when the guy she likes is too shy to make the first move?

4. The girl shows her great love for a boy that she likes... but what keeps him away from her?
