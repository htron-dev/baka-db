## Sinopse

Despite living with her overprotective father, Sumire still wants to experience a lot of things—most importantly, a life-changing romance. Her second year of high school has just begun when she meets her new seatmate. The catch? He's a mysterious 20-something troublemaker, Leo Ninomiya. Will this be a start of a love that will change her world?
