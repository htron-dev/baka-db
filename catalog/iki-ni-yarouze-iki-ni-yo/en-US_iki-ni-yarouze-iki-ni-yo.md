## Sinopse

Tomako is the star of the Fusou High School. Well loved by everyone, she is beautiful, a good student and nice to everybody. Tomako loves Shunpei. Shunpei is an ordinary student. There is nothing special about him. He's plain and people think that he is not a good match for Tomako at all. Yet, when Tomako confess her love to Shunpei, he rejected it because he wants to dedicate his life to boxing, which is a sports he loves.
