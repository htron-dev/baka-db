## Sinopse

Kuchiki, a flautist, was plagued by nightmares every night that forced her to seek the help of counsellor Matsuzaki. Ishikura, a police inspector, unexpectedly discovered a headless mummified body in an empty apartment and is now on the case. When Kuchiki regains her lost memories, the curtains also opens to a tragedy!

(Source: The Zero Alliance)
