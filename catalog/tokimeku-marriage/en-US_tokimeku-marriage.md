## Sinopse

Mary was the daughter of a wealthy family Himemiya in France. She came to Japan to find a prince of her childhood. Encounter with Yuuki, a guy who always helped her, making them close. One day, suddenly, just one of her pupils at the school is admitted as a prince of Mary. But why the heart of Mary has always focused on Yuuki?
