## Sinopse

Hoshino is an ace student with an interest in space and the occult. One day, on a bus to school, he met a shy bespectacled girl who was reading an occult magazine and he decided to talk to her. They shared the same interest and hit off well from the get go, so he decided to ask her out for a date.
