## Sinopse

JD lives a peaceful life with his grandfather on a small island, until bounty hunters show up looking for them! Will JD be able to put to use what his grandfather’s taught him to survive in a world of magical peril? And what is the nature of the Recast spell his grandfather’s cast on him?
