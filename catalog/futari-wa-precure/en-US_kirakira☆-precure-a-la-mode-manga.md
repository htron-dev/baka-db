## Sinopse

14-year-old Ichika Usami loves sweets and wishes to become a patisserie. When she encounters fairy Pekorin, she is able to transform into Cure Whip to protect Kirakiraru, an energy that represents the feelings put into making a sweet, from the Kirakiraru Thieves. She is later joined by four other girls.
