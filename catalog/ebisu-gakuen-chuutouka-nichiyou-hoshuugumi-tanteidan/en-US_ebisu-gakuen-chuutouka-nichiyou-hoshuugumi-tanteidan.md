## Sinopse

A long-awaited comic series which is a collaboration with the rising stars "EBiDAN39"! A teenage prodigy… and also a dropout?!

Follow the school life adventures of 6 unique and unconventional students! Due to his excessive absences, Riku Rokuoin, who is a genius with an IQ of 270 ,ends up needing to go to a special "Sunday Remedial Class" but the students he met there were ones of his kind!
