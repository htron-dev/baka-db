## Sinopse

Private St. Valentine Girls' Academy. In this academy a Cupid, Kokkuri-san and a Succubus, beings from outside the human realm, struggle every day to grant the wishes of girls fallen in love. The story starts to unfold when they meet Yukazaki Anju, a human girl with a mysterious power.
