## Sinopse

Short animated films of well-known Japanese folk tales, including the stories of Taro Maegami, The Riceball Family, The Master of Catching Stars, Thunder is the Bridegroom, and The Elephant's Sneeze. Shown as part of the children's variety
program, Tondeke Gutchonpa.

(Source: The Anime Encyclopedia)
