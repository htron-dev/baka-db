## Sinopse

Three North Korean spies are sent to a small South Korean village to await for further instructions. After years, the mission they are finally given is to kill themselves in order to protect government secrets. They must now decide whether to follow orders or to commit treason and be hunted down by the country that they had sacrificed everything for.
