## Sinopse

It is the year 2036. Mankind enjoys a life not so different from ours. However, people are accompanied by new partners, the 15cm tall "Shinki." They live side-by-side with people both as companions to share experiences and feelings with and as warriors to fight together. Those fighting Shinki are called Busou Shinki.
