## Sinopse

Sakugi Mitsuaki is a poor college student who gets hooked on figure modeling. In return for helping his class he receives some special putty—but the figure he builds with it suddenly comes to life. The figure is a life force called "orichalcum" that fulfills the wish of its creator. Their strange life together begins.
