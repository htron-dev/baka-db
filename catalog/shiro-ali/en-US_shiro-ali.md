## Sinopse

Out of the blue, protagonist Snow White finds herself transported to a strange world. Here, many unusual creatures roam, and all the signs and words are backwards.

To leave this world, Snow White must fulfill one condition: "Make Alice the King."

For the sake of returning back to her own world, Snow White must team up with Alice but… A strange yet wondrous fairytale-like fantasy awaits you.

(Source: Comic Sylph)
