## Sinopse

A bright and cheerful guy, Yuto. A smart and quiet gay guy, Ryou. A handsome delinquent, Kei. During their high school days, the three of them were always together, but Kei liked Ryou and Ryou like Yuto. Six months after their graduation, Yuto was reunited with the other two by chance at a bar. But he felt something had changed from their high school days because of the subtle mood between Kei and Ryou...?!

(Source: Kadokawa, translated)
