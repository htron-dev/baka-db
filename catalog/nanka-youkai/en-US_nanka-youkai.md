## Sinopse

Hide (Hi-De) meets a mysterious girl named Miru and soon find out that she has special power. Miru will sneeze whenever she sense spirit or detect that some strange occurrence are caused by the something paranormal. The two travel together and encounter all kinds of paranormal activity causing by ghost, demon, human spirit and divine being .
