## Sinopse

A collection consisting of the title story plus two short stories:

1. Owaru Heart Janee
   Rinzy's a famous hard rock musician. His ex-girlfriend, the model Katherine, commits suicide in front of him and he fails to help her. JP, a 16 year old homeless kid happens to be the only witness. In order to prevent exposure and scandal, Rinzy's manager, Stella, hires JP as Rinzy's assistant.
   (Source: Storm in Heaven)

2. Gingerman Nigeta
   An 80 page one-shot.

3. See You in Eden
   Oba Tamako tells of her time being at art high school.

(Source: MU)
