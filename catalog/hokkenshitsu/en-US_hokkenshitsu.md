## Sinopse

The gag comedy is set in a school nurse's office (hokenshitsu). A strange nurse deals with the unique issues of the female student body, such as an self-absorbed eye-patch-wearing girl, a character with frequent nosebleeds, a shy but well-endowed girl, and others.

(Source: ANN)
