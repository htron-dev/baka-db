## Sinopse

Fujisawa Kenji, 17 years old. Born in Yubari, Hokkaido, a boy with the dream of being a mob member to look out for those around him. After dropping out of school, Kenji went to his local Kumicho, who told him to learn the way of the Yakuza from a man known as "The Master Gangster" - Kujo Eiji. After successfully joining his gang, Kenji united to take vengeance on rival factions who had wronged them.
