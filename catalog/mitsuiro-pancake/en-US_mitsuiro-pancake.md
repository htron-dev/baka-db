## Sinopse

Pastry chef Chiharu takes in Tougo, a young man who is attractive but has no home and no job. When Tougo isn't able to do even the simplest domestic chore, he comes at Chiharu suggesting, "Let's do something that feels good." What will Chiharu do when he discovers his homeless border isn't so homeless after all?

(Source: Juné Manga)
