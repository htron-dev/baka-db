## Sinopse

The story is of a masked boy, Tani, a strong young man, who has a crush on a girl named Shima Rihoko, the school volleyball star. He gets into trouble trying to get her attention, besides facing the thugs of the school. He’s a very jealous guy, and will not let anyone get close to Shima-san.
