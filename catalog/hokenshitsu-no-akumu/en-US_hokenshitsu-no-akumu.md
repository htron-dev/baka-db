## Sinopse

Consist of 4 horror story:

1. A group of boys test their courage by walking alone in cemetery complex. At first they only want to scare one of the boy a bit but it turn to something much fatal and scary.

2. The school infirmary is famous for its ghost stories so Matsumoto was a bit afraid when she has to spend the morning resting there because of anemia. Then instead of seeing any ghost she saw mice, mice and a lot of mice.

3. Yamagishi met a new transfer student and they become a close friend instantly. One day, she discover her secret and the reason she hopped from on boyfriend to another.

4. A horror story about a mysterious broadcast from the school radio club.
