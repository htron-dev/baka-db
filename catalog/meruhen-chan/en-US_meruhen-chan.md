## Sinopse

A Scottish transfer student, Date has always dreamed of seeing faeries and living in a magical world. But due to her constant daydreaming, she fails to make any friends on her first week. However, when she gets hit on the head, she suddenly receives the 'sight' and can see a little pixie called Rabiko!

So she starts off the year, and might even make a friend along the way!
