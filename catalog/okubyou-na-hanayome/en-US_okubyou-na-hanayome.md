## Sinopse

“Marry me!” said the man who promptly left to study abroad after he popped the question! However, 7 years later, Tomo bumped into that man, Yoshinobu, who once promised they would be together forever! Out of desperation, Tomo lied and said he was already married. In response, Yoshinobu said he would get married, too…
