## Sinopse

Nakahara Daisuke loves train stations and station bentos. On his tenth marriage anniversary, his wife Yuko gives him a trip around Japan. He boards the Blue Train special express Fuji on a delicious trip. First stop: Kyushu!

(Source: Jmanga)

This is an up-to-date documentary type of manga featuring introduction and commentary on signature Ekiben (Train Station Bento) around Japan. It also introduces rail attractions along the way.
