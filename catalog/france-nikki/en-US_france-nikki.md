## Sinopse

This is the comedic daily life story of four French girls, living in Paris, who are huge fans of Japanese manga! Monique, Amelie, Tricia, and Chloe meet together to read and discuss volumes of popular series that they all love, like L'Attaque des Titans and Fairy Tail.
