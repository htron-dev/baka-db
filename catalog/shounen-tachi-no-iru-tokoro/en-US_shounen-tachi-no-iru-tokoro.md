## Sinopse

The story follows protagonist Sano Kiri, who wants to have an ordinary high school life. However, he falls in with Minamino Ryuu, his classmate with a "best friend dependence," and the mysterious Narasaki Subaru. His days are not quite as normal as he hoped.
