## Sinopse

Kouichi Azumi was forced to quit professional shogi at the age of 26 after failing to rank higher than 4th dan. Time passed and with a new positive outlook on life after time away from shogi, he finds himself drawn to the game again, and decides to give it another shot as an amateur.

(Source: Shogakukan, translated)
