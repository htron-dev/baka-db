## Sinopse

"Every day, I want to be held by a man!!" -- daydreams the kind-hearted and principled high school student, Mamoru. And finally, God has heard Mamoru's prayers! One day after school, he returns home to find three beautiful men waiting for him! The heavenly days are just beginning... he thinks, until something unbelievably twisted and perverted happens! What will happen from now on to both his lifestyle and his heart?
