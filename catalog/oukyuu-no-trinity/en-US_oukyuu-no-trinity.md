## Sinopse

The story takes place in Wardell Ceram, a country of flowers and trees. Luka and Sylvan, the "Wolf Claws," act as chivalrous thieves who steal from evil aristocrats. One day, the princess of the kingdom asks them to help her crack down on corrupt nobles.
