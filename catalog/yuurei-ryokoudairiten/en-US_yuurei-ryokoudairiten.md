## Sinopse

Touno Mariko started seeing ghosts on the day of her grandmother's funeral 5 years ago. During a visit to the graveyard, she accidently destroys a tombstone. In order to not get cursed, she has to back for it back and in order to due that, she starts working for the Soul Traveling Agency.
