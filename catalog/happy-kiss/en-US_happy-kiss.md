## Sinopse

Always unlucky Mugi Kurata is a kind-hearted 15-year-old high school student. After a series of misfortunes, just as she starts to question if good things will really happen, she meets fellow student Hinata Akabane who is willing to share his acclaimed good luck. However, she soon discovers that rather than lucky by nature, it's more a case of the law of the jungle?
