## Sinopse

Omega Kiyo is a pole dancer working in a club dedicated to alpha customers. Kiyo's dream is to be his childhood friend, Chii-chan's bride. But Chii-chan thinks of Kiyo like he is his little brother?! Kiyo, a tall and built omega with a complex and Chii-chan, a firm and reliable shorter alpha, need to find their happy ending!
