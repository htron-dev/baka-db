## Sinopse

Three years ago, after realizing he had feelings for, not his lover Keiko, but her little brother Sachi, Yoshino escaped the city. When he once again returns, will his love for Sachi remain unrequited or will he finally gather the courage to confess his long concealed desires? In the meantime, Tamotsu, Sachi’s childhood friend, continues to struggle with his own unrequited love. However, with the appearance of a younger classmate, Sora, he may finally be able to experience the happiness of mutual love. Tough guys need love too! (Source: Deux Press)
