## Sinopse

With the Kansai Gang on his heels, Mishima fled to Tokyo and collapsed at the doorstep of Segawa, the young, next leader of the Jinbo Group. Since Segawa took him in, Mishima had devoted his life to serve the extremely capable, outwardly harsh yet inwardly lonesome gang leader. Will his love ever reach the furthest ends of Segawa’s heart ?
