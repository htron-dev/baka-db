## Sinopse

Hakuyou is considered a heretic in his clan because of his white hair. At eighteen, he fled into the human world in search of freedom. There he met Shouji, a newspaper reporter, and while spending time with him, he got attracted steadily. But it's between a demon and human! What will be the outcome of a demon and human love story—?

(Source: Canis Major Scanlations)
