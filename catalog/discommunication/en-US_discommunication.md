## Sinopse

Discommunication is a comedy that follows an aspiring manga artist and her older brother. The story begins when the sister's work wins the Rookie of the Year award. Even though she should be happy, for some reason her face is sullen. The first chapter reveals the secret behind the sister's winning work.

(Source: ANN)
