## Sinopse

Shiratama works as a professor at a university in Tokyo—and he has a secret. The thing is, he brings his kitten to work with him. What's more is that the kitten can talk! A relaxing college comedy about the talking kitten and the adults around him!

(Source: Shueisha, translated)
