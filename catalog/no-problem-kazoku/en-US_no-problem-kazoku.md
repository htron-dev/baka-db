## Sinopse

Kokoro Orihara would not ask any of her classmates to come to her place. That is because her papa has taken a doll for his wife. Because of this oddity, she wants no one to know about her family and she distances herself from everyone at school. That is, until one day appears Mizushima, a classmate who understands her problem...
