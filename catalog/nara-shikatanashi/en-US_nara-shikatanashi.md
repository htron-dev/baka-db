## Sinopse

Follows a Buddha-faced girl named Shanako and her friend, a deer named Shikao. Every evening at Nara Park, Shanako discusses her troubles with the deer. Shanako and the duo's "straight man" Shikao talk out silly situations in a Kansai dialect.

(Source: ANN)
