## Sinopse

Masumi and Tetsuji go to the same university. Soon they must decide what they will be doing after graduation, a very trying time for all students. But recently something seems wrong with Masumi. Of course this is because he can’t see inside the heart of Tetsuji (who he has admired since entering the university.) And then he hears the news that Tetsuji has been stabbed?! Starting from this original story, “Nude Moments” will make your heart pound! A Koreaki Kamuro Collection.
