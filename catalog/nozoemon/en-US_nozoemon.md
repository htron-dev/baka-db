## Sinopse

A parody of Fujiko F. Fujio's Doraemon.

Nozoemon is an ero romantic comedy about a robotic girl who travels back in time to aid the protagonist Takashi with the help of futuristic toys such as a "4D Randsel" and an "Anything Box."

(Source: Batoto)
