## Sinopse

Ai Nekoya is a cute teenager who turns into a cat uncontrollably when he is under emotional distress. After being cornered by delinquents he gets help from a scary looking guy who turns out to be a real cat lover and his classmate to top it off. Will Ai be able to keep his shape shifting condition a secret?
