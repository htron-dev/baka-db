## Sinopse

Komatsu Momo is a 23-year-old normal office lady and all-around normal girl. She lives alone in the house her parents left her. The good news is that she's planning to get married soon! The lucky guy is her colleague at work. And that's why she firmly sets her mind on getting in touch with her brothers for the happy occasion... What will happen to her normal love and life when they get into the picture?!

(Source: Aerandria Scans)
