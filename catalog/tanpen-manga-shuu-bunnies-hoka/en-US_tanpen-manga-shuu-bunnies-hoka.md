## Sinopse

Collection of 9 short stories:

1. Shima Ikka (The Islanders)
   A man is stranded on a remote island that is inhabited by a strange hermetical family. Being here could be the best thing that would happen to him.

2. Education from Outerspace
3. Eisei Kashikan

4. Bunnies
   What if you could see your own future? For a curious reporter, this question becomes no longer theoretical as he ends up serving as an involuntary guinea pig for a mad scientist who claims to be able to do just that. But is what he sees really the future?

5. Locker Boueki (Locker Trade Agreement)
   A scientist from the frozen world of the future discovers a locker which is also a time portal to the past...

6. Wataru Seken
7. Tora no Mon
8. Wasureta Kusa
9. Ipanema no Musume (Girl from Ipanema)
