2. Hoshi ni Negai wo, Kimi ni wa Ai wo (Wishing on the Stars, for Your Love)
3. Hoshi nante Shinjinai! (I Won't Believe the Stars!)

5. Gentei☆Homestay (Restricted☆Homestay)
