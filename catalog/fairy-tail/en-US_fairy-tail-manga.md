## Sinopse

This artbook contains 3 side stories that were not included in the main volumes.

1. Mira-sensei no Henshin Mahou Lesson (Mira-Sensei's Transforming Magic Class)
2. Yousei no Izumi e Youkoso! (Welcome to Fairy Fountain)
3. Nagagutsu wo Haita Happy (Happy in Boots)

Also contains "Fairy Megane" (crossover one-shot between "Fairy Tail" and "Yankee-kun to Megane-chan").
