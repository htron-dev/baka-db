## Sinopse

Anthology about a goddess who can reverse time. Goddess appears in the form of two mysterious girls, one young and one older. If you make an offering of pomegranates to a certain temple, you will be offered a chance to return and change your past. But, in exchange the goddess will take the 2nd thing you hold most precious in your life.
