## Sinopse

U.C. 0105—within Zum City a One Year War memorial exhibition is about to be opened, and a very special guest is invited to talk about his experiences during the first Space war of the Universal Century—his name: Kai Shiden! Kai is both pleased and honored to be present and recalls his past on board of the White Base.
