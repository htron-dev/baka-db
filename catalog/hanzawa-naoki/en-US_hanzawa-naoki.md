## Sinopse

Naoki Hanzawa is the Loans Division Chief of the Osaka Nishi branch of the third largest megabank in the world, the Tokyo Chuo Bank. To meet the target loan amount set by the headquarter branch, the Branch Manager of the Osaka Nishi branch, Tadasu Asano, asks Hanzawa to handle a loan required by Nishi Osaka Steel. However, after obtaining the loan, the President of Nishi Osaka Steel, Mitsuru Higashida, declares bankruptcy and goes missing. Hanzawa must now race against time to retrieve the loan and uphold the capital and honor of the bank!

(Source: MU)
