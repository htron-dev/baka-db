## Sinopse

Everyone has at least one secret that cannot be said to anyone and it should stay hidden. Rina is 28 year old secretary. At work she is nice and stylish lady but at home she is total mess, just like her messy apartment. Narita is 25 years old sales manager who is good at cooking and cleaning (a bit of a cleaning nerd). One day he finds out her secret...
