## Sinopse

Criminals gather from all over the country in South Naniwa Prison. Every year on Christmas Eve, the men secretly gather inside the prison to compete. Everyone looks forward the excitement of the annual New Year's dish “Food” Battle! Who will win the coveted New Year's dish?
