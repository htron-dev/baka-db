## Sinopse

This manga is a sequel of Jungle Boy manga. This time, the story is focus on Takimoto and Kousai's relatoinship and a bit of back story about Takimoto's brother.

The last part of the manga is a one shot called Partita, about A boy, Shinya and Satoru and their trouble about family life.
