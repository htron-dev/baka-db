## Sinopse

In 2014, Microsoft Japan commissioned Manabu Himeda to create a web commercial for their Windows 2 in 1 PCs. In the musically-driven performance, Himeda acts out choreographed movements in real time to interact with an animated video he created that is being projected on the wall behind him.
