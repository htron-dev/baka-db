## Sinopse

Peking in the 20s - the time in which the old customs and fresh wind mix. What is the true reason for the rage of older brother Ruhai on young Mr. Rushan from house Tai with his love to the theater ...?

The dreams and the real life of the people who have made a mistake to the old dramatic art of the Peking opera are shown with the history all around famous actor Yang Luoxian. The splendid stage stands for the highest luck for the time being, and also for the everlasting admiration...
