## Sinopse

In order to live by his principles of never losing a fight, high schooler Ryousuke Kanzaki will run from everything to win! ...or at least that's what he wants to do. Even though he always tries stay away from trouble, he saves his classmate Kaede Ikasa from a fight. Was Kaede be able to steal Ryousuke's heart with his piercing gaze...? As the quickly grow closer, the transfer student Fumi Amano, who made a promise to marry Kaede, suddenly appears?! A heart pounding school love story about their unripe love!

(Source: Houbunsha, translated)
