## Sinopse

Two specials released September 3rd, 2014, in the final volume of the Blu-ray/DVD releases.

One of them is a recap of the whole Kill la Kill anime which is 8:43 minutes long, while the other is a sequel to the anime which is 27:18 minutes long.
