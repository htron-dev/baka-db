## Sinopse

In olden Britian, Orlando, who is in search for the key to becoming King, meets a beautiful priest by the name of Ethelred, who is on the verge of being killed as a living sacrifice.

Now begins the dangerous adventure of these two, who have met in a chaotic world surrounded by swords and spiritual beliefs...
