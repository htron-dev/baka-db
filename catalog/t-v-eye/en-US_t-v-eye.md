## Sinopse

Three incredible short stories filled with some darkness and obscurity.

T.V. Eye - Meet Isao and his crew of mischievous mistaken group of friends who are obsessed with their refrigerator and a TV that doesn't work.

Camera Obscura - A girl with a camera meets a guy in a club, takes him in, dines him, takes pictures and....

Channel 11 - Twins and another pair of twins that look like them but are dead? A Transvestite, a bar master and an encyclopedia too, what do they all have in common?
