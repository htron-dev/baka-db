## Sinopse

Manibe, the lover of a girl, Setona, born from a warrior family, becomes the chosen sacrifice to the village mountain gods. Rushing to the shrine, what Setona saw was an ominous presence and a mysterious man! “My love, even should you die and reincarnate, I will find you.” This is the story of a love that transcends the bounds of time.
