## Sinopse

Daisuke, after seeing a movie with gay in it, has a revelation: he might be in love with his best friend, Misaki. After this discovery, Daisuke decides to confess the next day. At first surprised, Misaki decides to go out with Daisuke. But most things are going in the right direction, the more their relationship deepens and Daisuke does not seem ready to move to the 'physical' phase...
