## Sinopse

This official anthology consists of nearly 100 stories by different authors, all based on the anime Tiger & Bunny. These short comedic manga stories expand on the characters of the Tiger & Bunny universe. See more of Origami's efforts to get popular, Fire Emblem giving advice, Blue Rose's ill-fated love life, and of course, Tiger and Barnaby's tenuous partnership.
