## Sinopse

Uju and her friends are crow tengu who attend a special tengu training school. These tengu gain power by summoning gods. In order to show up a snobby exchange student tengu, Uju decides to summon a legendary, sleeping god in an ancient shrine. But what will happen when her plan actually succeeds?
