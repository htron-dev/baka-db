## Sinopse

"See you at the Forest Bookshop"... A bookshop that brings joy to any booklover's heart. What if there's also both a kind, knowledgeable guy who wears glasses and the cool prince of glasses there?

Read on and watch as the story of Akari and the bookstore clerks start to intertwine.
