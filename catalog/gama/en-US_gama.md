## Sinopse

The world is constantly under threat of super-villains and monsters, and to fight against such powerful enemies, there are the superheroes.

Kitajishi Yuri and Miyuki are sisters who work in the Earth Defense Corps as counseling staff. Their clients include superheroes who are having crises in life as superheroes, losing their way and confidence in what they should do in a world where super-powered beings are not rare.
