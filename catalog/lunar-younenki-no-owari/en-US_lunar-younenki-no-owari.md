## Sinopse

Takes place during the time between Zophar's defeat and the epilogue of Lunar 2: Eternal Blue. Alice, a young girl that Hiro and Ruby befriend, is being chased by Ghaleon and Xenobia (for unknown reasons), and it is up to Hiro and his friends to protect her.
