## Sinopse

The story begins in a small village west of the capital and centers on childhood friends Yuugo and Tenia. While Yuugo focuses on being an herbalist, Tenia is a hunter. A force of Arisen knights passes through their town in search of more Arisen to join their ranks. Tenia reveals that she is an Arisen and wants to travel with them.

(Source: ANN)
