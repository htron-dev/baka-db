From Animenewsnetwork:Tsubasa and Tokimaru are brothers, though not related by blood. Tsubasa, who lost his mother at the sea, recently starts to see strange visions. In this foreign place they meet a mysterious pirate Shroud, who owns a great ship, and a strange man called Leda. Both men join the boys in their journey full of different adventures...
