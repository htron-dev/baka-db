## Sinopse

There was a legend about a guy named Kang Do-Young. He was perfect, great at sports, rich and good at fighting. Na Mirae was a girl who was the leader of a club called ‘Pink Lady’ and who also wanted to be Jiang of her whole school. For Do-Young, it was love at first sight while for Mirae, it wasn’t.

(Source: Fateful Encounters)
