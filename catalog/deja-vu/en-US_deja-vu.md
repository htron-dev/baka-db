## Sinopse

A collection of four oneshots (Spring, Summer, Fall and Winter). Every story is about two lovers who gets confronted with a cruel stroke of fate.

Besides the four main stories, this manwha has two more oneshots: Utility, a story about sexual abuse and suicide viewed by a child; and The Sea, about a couple that discover more about themselves during a trip to the sea.
