## Sinopse

We walk hand-in-hand in the yellow morning sunlight and in the orange sunset…
What wonderful school days we spend together.

Fujino Miyuki and Mizutani Jun are childhood friends and next door neighbors. This is their short love story in a literature club.

Contains the short stories Tear Potion, Katakoi Hime, Magical Chocolate and The Proof of Her Love.
