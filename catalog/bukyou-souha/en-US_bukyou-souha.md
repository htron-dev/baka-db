## Sinopse

Life is the most fragile thing. That's the world of "martial madmen". A lone drifter, Bowie, appears in the violent city of Longshanbó. There he meets a mysterious non-human girl, Shiyu, for whom he throws himself into a bloody battle in order to save her! A violent action work where life and death are constantly at stake!
