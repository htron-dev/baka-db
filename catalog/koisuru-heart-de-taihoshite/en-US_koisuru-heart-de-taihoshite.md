## Sinopse

Asuka, a woman detective, wishes to arrest phantom thief Kelberos, who is widely talked about in town, because he kissed her by force...! Besides the title story filled with love and action, the two of love comedy series of nurse Nana and doctor Hiura are included in this collection of short stories.
