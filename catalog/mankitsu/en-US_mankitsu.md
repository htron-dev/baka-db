## Sinopse

Youhei is a NEET who spends most of his time hanging out at a manga cafe. This particular cafe offers various hentai style services. After Youhei loses his virginity in one of the cafe's rooms, he decides he wants to try his best to get a job as a company man. But will the NEET be able to find a job? Or will he just keep having unexpected sexual encounters at the manga cafe?
