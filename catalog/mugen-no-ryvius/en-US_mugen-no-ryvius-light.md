## Sinopse

Mugen no Ryvius specials released on the 3-part fan disc "Mugen no Ryvius: Light." Three episodes were dedicated to a self-titled picture drama (but are animated normally). The other three episodes were dedicated "Avant-Title," a first-person POV style of specials.
