## Sinopse

From now on, Egypt, a four thousand year old civilization, will now collapse and enter into a war-torn era. It will be a period of power and angry waves. The small country, Esteria, was invaded and destroyed by neighbouring country, Urjna, and its king, Sneferu. Prince Sariokis vows to get revenge on Sneferu, and avenge Esteria. (Source: M-U)
