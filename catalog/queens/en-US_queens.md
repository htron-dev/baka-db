## Sinopse

Pil-Hyun Jung is a pretty boy whose teddy bear making hobby drives his father insane. With his psycho dad constantly threatening to chop off his manly bits, Pil-Hyun is determined to put his feminine ways behind him. Making manhwa artist Bok-Nam, the author of “How to Escape From Being a Pretty Boy”, his mentor, he’s a little taken aback when he learns Bok-Nam’s true identity. Can he become a manly man?
