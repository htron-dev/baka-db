## Sinopse

Hirano Haruto, a freshman, moves into a dormitory and has Morioka Toushi, a scary looking upperclassman assigned to him as his guidance manager. Rumors say that Morioka used to bully his junior back in middle school, but is he really as bad as everyone makes him out to be?
