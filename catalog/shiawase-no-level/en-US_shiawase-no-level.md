## Sinopse

Kaoru, an employee at the local animal hospital, is involved with the hospital's director, Nagi. Nagi has a tendency to shower cute things with attention, which causes much jealousy on Kaoru's part. One day, a stranger shows up at the hospital, and everything begins to taken on a new form.
