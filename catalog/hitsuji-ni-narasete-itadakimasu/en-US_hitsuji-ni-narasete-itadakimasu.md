## Sinopse

Stuck in a job that does not pay well, Baba is struggling to pay for his food and rent. After a drunken night with his coworkers, a package arrives at his door—an expensive sex toy he has no recollection of ordering. Now in debt with no way out, Baba is desperate for money.

Right before making a rash decision, a coworker suggests Baba an odd part-time job: become a test subject in an experimental laboratory with the goal of bringing VR technology into the sex industry. Left with no other choice, he visits the facility and meets the young and attractive scientist Iida, the one in charge of the experiments.

Although initially wary, Baba is elated to start the tests. The technology offers him a realistic experience with his favorite actress, Miori, and intense pleasure he has never felt before. However, he is unaware that behind Miori's sensual actions is someone beyond his imagination...
