## Sinopse

Just before the start of summer vacation Hazuki Aikawa receives a lover letter from a boy in her school whose name she doesn't know. Too overwhelmed she immediately turns him down. However, his smiling face when he thanks her for her answer moves Hazuki. An innocent first love story is set in motion.
