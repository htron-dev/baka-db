## Sinopse

Yoake Itaru, 38, divorced.
Profession: Investigator and confidential informant for an insurance agency.
Specialty: Cases others tend to avoid.

Every day there are mysterious "accidents" that are actually clever cover-ups of suicides and murders. And the parties concerned will appeal to him. And so, the colorless days of this man will continue...
