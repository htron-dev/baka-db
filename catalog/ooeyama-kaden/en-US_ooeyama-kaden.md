## Sinopse

Demons reside on the peak of Mount Ooeyama. A samurai climbs the mountain to conquer them. But the road is long and a young girl accompanies him. This young girl, Fuji no Ha, wants to see Ibaraki, a demon, before the samurai can kill him…
