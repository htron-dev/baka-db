## Sinopse

This is a manga about the variuos family members of the Hagami residence. Meet the oldest son Aki, the eldest daughter Hirono, the second daughter Honoka, the parents, the grandparents, the dog Komaru and the cat poseidon. The first chapter is about Hirono and a peculiar ghost experience.
