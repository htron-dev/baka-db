## Sinopse

-- So, you will be the one who’ll kill me. --

The legendary swordsman Minerva from the Knights of the Principality who could foresee the future and uses overwhelming sword techniques encounters Chris, a child mercenery branded with curse of the mystic beasts capable of destroying the fate in his suroundings. Minerva was fated to die on that day but her encounter with Chris twisted her fate. Set in the fictional medieval Europe thus starts the journey of their companionship and their war against the kingdom.
