## Sinopse

Fumiya is a guy with a lot problems in real life, his job interviews are a disaster and the relationship with Chiemi,his girlfriend, does not go well at all.

One day, however, something unexpected happens: before the poor Fumiya appears a beautiful and enigmatic high school student, Yuki, telling him: "Do you dare to go ...".

From that moment the life of our protagonist changes radically.
