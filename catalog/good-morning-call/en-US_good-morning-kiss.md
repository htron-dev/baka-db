## Sinopse

The series takes place a few years after GMC. Nao and Hisashi are in college and are living in across from each other in an apartment complex. When Nao's father pays a visit and learns of Hisashi he takes Nao back to her family. Hisashi realizes how much he hates being away from her and proposes.
