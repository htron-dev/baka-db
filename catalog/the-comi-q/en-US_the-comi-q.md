## Sinopse

In the not so distant future, prisons have instituted work programs to help ease prisoners back into society. Some of the more artistically inclined are recruited to help draw backgrounds for manga artists. In struggling manga creator Ryouta Sakamaki's case, his assistant is the infamous Halloween Murderer, but he's calling out for help through his art...
