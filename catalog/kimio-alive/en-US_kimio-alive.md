## Sinopse

A 1000% emotional tale about a hot, up and coming, passionate teenage YouTuber!! Kimio Hasegawa is a high school freshman who has too many things that he wants to do. "I want to live doing the things I like. This feeling is what I'd call a 'dream.'"

Just what is the method that he has come up with in order to fulfill his dream!? The curtain has now risen on this tale that might bring courage to those who need it!!

(Source: Kodansha, translated)
