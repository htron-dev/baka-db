From StarryHeaven:
High school models Sakurai Erika and Tsukioka Hazumi are true princesses brimming with confidence who know no fear. As such, they're rivals who fight everyday... One day, after her return from Paris, Erika witnesses Hazumi hugging the current top male model Kai...! Could she have finally found Hazumi's greatest weak spot!?
