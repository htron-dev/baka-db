## Sinopse

Kohinata Mako is a 30 year old office worker. She thought she had done a pretty good job working at her company and maintaining a good relationship with her boyfriend, but suddenly she finds herself in precarious positions on both ends. This story is about how she gets back on her feet through work and love.
