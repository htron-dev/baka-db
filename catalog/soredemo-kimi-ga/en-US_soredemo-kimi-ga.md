## Sinopse

A love story between a plain girl and a shining boy. Muta meets Kasai Kippei by chance during a supplementary exam. Their relationship starts to grow when Kasai suddenly starts to talk to Muta, which leads Muta to have feelings for him.

Volume 2: Shiritai no wa Kimi no Koto
