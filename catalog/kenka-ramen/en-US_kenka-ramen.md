## Sinopse

In this passionate gourmet comic, ex-bikie gang leader Yoshitsune Genda faces off with his rivals in the world of ramen. Each encounter brings him closer to ramen mastery. His father, owner and chef of the struggling Gantetsu Ramen, uses his bare hands to lift ramen out of boiling water. For Yoshitsune, the leader of the Salamander Gang, every day is one brawl after another. One day he meets the fiery Remi, who lures him into a contract negotiation to sell his father's ramen shop! What will happen to Yoshitsune!?

(Source: Media Do International/Nihonbungeisha)
