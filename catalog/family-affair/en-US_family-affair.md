## Sinopse

Tooru lost his parents at a very young age and had been living with his grandfather ever since. But while his grandfather is recovering from a surgery, he suddenly opens up to his grandson and tells him that he will inherit his entire fortune when he dies. Now, his relatives who weren't even close with him, start pushing themselves on to him so they can claim the position as his legal guardian.
