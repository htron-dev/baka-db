## Sinopse

After learning on her birthday that her best friend is dating the boy she loves, Hitomi falls asleep crying and wishing her life could change. She wakes up in a desert, where a young man wearing a turban tells her she is a "jin", a god with mysterious powers. In the world of the Arabian Nights, Hitomi is involved in a quarrel for the throne of the kingdom and will have to face adventure... and love.
