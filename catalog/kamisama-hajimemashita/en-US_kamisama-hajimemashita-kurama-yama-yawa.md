## Sinopse

Collection of four short stories related to the Kurama-yama arc.

First narrated by Jirou, tells of his relationship with Suirou and his first encounter with the thunder beast. The second, narrated by Suirou, is about his relationship with Kurama (Shinjurou) and the events that led to his injury. The third is about Nanami trying to figure out whether or not Tomoe really said he liked her at the end of the Kurama-Yama arc. And the last one is about Botanmaru befriending a small tenma.
