## Sinopse

Suspicion is a manga by Osamu Tezuka, and also the name of one of his books in Kodansha's line of "Osamu Tezuka Manga Complete Works" books containing a collection of Tezuka's short stories. The stories included in this book are "Suspicion" (1982), "Insect Collector" (1979), "Insect Collector - The Butterfly Road Smells of Death" (1979), "Volcanic Eruption" (1982), "Peace Concert" (1984), "Activist Student" (1968), and "Old Folk's Home" (1972). (Source: Manga Updates)
