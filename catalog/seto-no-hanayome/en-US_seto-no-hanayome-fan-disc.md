## Sinopse

Fan discs containing shorts that weren't included in the TV series.

1. Aoi Koi-hen
2. Ibara Koiji-hen

Music videos from both discs are not included in the entry, as they don't contain any new scenes.

The same specials were included in the Blueray box set, but divided into 6 episodes of 2 minutes each.
