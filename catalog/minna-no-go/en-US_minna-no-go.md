## Sinopse

This is a story set in the Three Kingdoms era, from the mangaka of the horror series Aphorism. Sun Ce (Sonsaku) dies and is succeeded by his younger brother, Sun Quan (Sonken). Assisted by Sun Ce's best friend, Zhou Yu (Shuyu), Sun Quan takes his first steps as the new ruler of China!
