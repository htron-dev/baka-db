## Sinopse

In appearance, Sakurako is a model student. But behind this pure image she gives relies a different truth: she in an inveterate yaoi fan. A terrible secret which, if it get to be revealed, will tarnish her reputation and her mother's, who is a novelist.

When two boys from Sakurako's school dicover who she really is, things aren't going to be as easy for her anymore...
