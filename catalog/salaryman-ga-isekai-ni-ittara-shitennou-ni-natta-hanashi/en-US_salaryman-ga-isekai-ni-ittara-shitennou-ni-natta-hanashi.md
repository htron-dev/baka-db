## Sinopse

Dennosuke Uchimura, is a simple Salaryman, who one day dies run over, and is invoked in another world, by the Demon King himself, who offers him employment as one of his Four Heavenly Kings. Can Dennosuke survive as a servant of the Demon King, and meet his expectations?
