## Sinopse

The TV cartoon "Bear-Haunted Monster Project" is a continuation of the plots in "Bear-Haunted Adventure Diary" and "Bear-Haunted Adventure Diary 2". Especially in the "Bear-Haunted Adventure Diary 2", the development of the star character "Genius Wei", the villain "Genius Wei" reappears, and behind the scenes Boss Wang will also appear in this movie, Boss Wang is going to be real.
The film tells the story of Tiancaiwei creating an "invincible mechanical monster" to create a forest crisis, and "Xiong Qiang" teaming up to guard the homeland to meet the viewing needs of children aged 6-12. The role of the film is rich in emotions and the storyline is ups and downs, which fully meets the viewing needs of children aged 6-12, and brings a variety of character education to the child audience.
Tian Caiwei discovered an energy stone, he wanted to use the energy stone to create an invincible monster that dominates the land, sea and air, shocking the scientific community. In order to collect the abilities of the animals, the cunning Genius Wei created a variety of lifelike collectors to confuse the animals and seize the abilities. For the harmony and tranquility of the forest, Bald Head Qiang, Xiong Da, and Xiong Er joined the small animals to fight against Tiancaiwei and Dongdongyao, staged a series of amusing or crisis-ridden power battles...(translated from baidu baike)
