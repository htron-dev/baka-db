## Sinopse

The story begins with a car accident one rainy night on the forest highway. Logger Vick retrieves a suitcase from the scene of the accident and reveals what’s inside. From then on, peace and quiet are no longer within the forest. The lives of Vick and the bears will be forever changed, as they are thrown into the middle of a huge conspiracy. Who is behind this conspiracy? And what deeper secret does it hold?
