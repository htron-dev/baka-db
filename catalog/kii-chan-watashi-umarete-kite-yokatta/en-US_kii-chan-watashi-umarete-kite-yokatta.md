## Sinopse

An educational film about how a girl with a disability is able to overcome her restricted lifestyle and sew a yukata for her sister's wedding. The lesson is that those with disabilities are not limited to a life of nothing, that with encouragement and positive attitudes of the people around them--they are able to great things like everyone else leaving with the tag line of the movie "I'm glad I was born".
