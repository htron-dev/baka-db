## Sinopse

You-Geon is your average high school student. One day, however, he's saved from drowning by a girl his age. This girl happens to be a North Korean agent that infiltrated You-Geon's country. When he learns her secret the girl is determined to stay by You-Geon's side at all times to make sure he doesn't tell anyone about her identity.
