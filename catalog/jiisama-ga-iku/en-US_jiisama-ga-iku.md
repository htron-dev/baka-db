## Sinopse

The story centers on 73-year-old Seitarou Asao, who runs a tea plantation. Due to a god's mistake, he gets transported to another world in the place of his grandchild. He then starts a second life 10 years younger as 63-year-old Seitarou. Through his wisdom of an old man and "assistance magic," he is able to live a leisurely life in his new home.

(Source: ANN)
