## Sinopse

An adaptation from Japanese's folklore, Momotarou. After fighting with Kintarou from his class about his parents, Momotarou is shocked to find out about his birth origin and decides to run away from home. He gets lost and later makes 3 friends in a wood, a werewolf, were-monkey and were-hawk.
