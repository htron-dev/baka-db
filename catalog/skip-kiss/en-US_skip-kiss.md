## Sinopse

Hamura is a sheltered and very friendly high school student. He always admire Miyake from afar who is very reserved and troubled. An incident in the locker room has finally open up a chance for Hamura to be friends with Miyake. But does Miyake want a friend... or could they even be friends? Between Miyake's troubled past, Hamura's jealous close friend and preverted tutor, how will these two make any type of relationship...
