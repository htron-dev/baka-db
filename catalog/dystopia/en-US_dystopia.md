## Sinopse

Something is killing humans who have received mysterious powers through a seemingly divine source, and only the vampire Angela stands between them and utter annihilation. Her only friend is the vampire known only as Alliance, who secretly desires to create a vampire world called Dystopia! Will he use his unique abilities to assist Angela...or to destroy her?
