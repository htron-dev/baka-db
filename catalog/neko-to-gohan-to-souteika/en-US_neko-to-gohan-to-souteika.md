## Sinopse

The story begins when a girl arrives at the door of top book designer Tatsumi, asking for a job as an assistant. Thanks to her cleaning of his house, Tatsumi is able to take another look at his worn-out life. The manga is a story of a book designer who lives near the Chuo line of trains with his cat.

(Source: ANN)
