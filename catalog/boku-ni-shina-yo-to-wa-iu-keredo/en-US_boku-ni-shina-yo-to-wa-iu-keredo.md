## Sinopse

Yayoi Yukihira is moving to Tokyo in search of love. She has more success than could ever have Imagined when she meets three very different men: Hosumi, a handsome co-worker, Masato Watanabe, her friend and neighbour, and Ryusuke Suga who is a charming playboy. But it's very hard to choose!
