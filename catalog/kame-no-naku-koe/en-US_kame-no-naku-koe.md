## Sinopse

Nakagawa Makoto is a serious city hall official who secretly draws shoujo manga as a hobby. One day, he meets a high school girl, Kouzuki Kureha, at a restaurant, and ends up showing her his work. Touched by Nakagawa's manga, Kureha forces him to go to a publishing company in Tokyo!

This is the beginning of an banging off-road journey...
