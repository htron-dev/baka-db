## Sinopse

Anne Sullivan is the woman who became a tutor for Helen Keller, a girl who was deaf, dumb, and blind. Anne decides to be her tutor to give Helen joy of life, no matter how little. She stays with Helen all the time and dedicates herself to Helen's education. However, things doesn't go as well as she expects and she has a very difficult time day to day. On the other side, she has hope, and teaches braille to Helen. One summer’s day, Helen was given well water in her hand, and finally she understood the meaning of the word 'Water'.

(Source: Official Site)
