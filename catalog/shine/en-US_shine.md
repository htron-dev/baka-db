## Sinopse

This manga contains three stories.

Story 1: Shine

Maki is a shy girl secretly in love with his sempai at the photography club. Just when the mood is getting better between them, she meets Akihara, a very rude and straightforward guy she can't really deal with.

Story 2: Slowly, my Lovely

Fuuka has been really nervous since her classmate Kenji Tokuda confessed to her. Even though she hasn't given him an answer yet, he acts as if nothing has happened...?

Story 3: Before the Promise

During winter break, Yui met Hisashi in the mountain and liked him almost instantly. But then she had to leave without even telling him, so when they meet again months later, he's not very pleased to see her.
