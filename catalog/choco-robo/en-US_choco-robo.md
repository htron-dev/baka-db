## Sinopse

The Tokyo reflected in the eyes of a beauty named Choco is filled with grosteque monsters. Even today. the wandering Choco is in search of a "prey". What is the meaning of "a certain game" that she has to clear regardless of its risks? This is a gun-action suspense story filled with lust and violence that goes beyond the critical level.
