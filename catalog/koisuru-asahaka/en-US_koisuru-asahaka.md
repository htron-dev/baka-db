## Sinopse

Meet Akamitsu, an ordinary senior in high school. He does nothing but study. One day, on his way back from school, he runs into a mysterious, handsome guy who ends up deflowering him!!! To top it off, this mysterious, handsome guy claims to be an incubus?!! And, so, the utterly average Akamitsu is in for the ride of a lifetime...
