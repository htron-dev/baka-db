## Sinopse

Mikajima, a real go-getter scout for gay porn videos, sets his eyes on Nitta, who has a wiry physique and hides his youthful face with glasses. He casually started talking to him downtown, and successfully lured Nitta using their common interest: books. Afterwards, in order to bring out Nitta's talent as a porn actor, Mikajima molests Nitta. With this obscene development, will Nitta fall into the hands of Mikajima?
