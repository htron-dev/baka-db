## Sinopse

In Tokyo, suspicious cases are occurring where the victims have all of their blood drained from them. Mikogami Keigo, a detective who also wants revenge for his lover who was killed 10 years ago, pursues the culprit, the "Man with two faces." What is the identity of the silver-haired girl who observes him...?
