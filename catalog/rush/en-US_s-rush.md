## Sinopse

Focuses on ru2sh! member Ryou and popular actor Shun Ogura. After Ryou uses details of Ogura's past to blackmail him into leaving Yuu and Hinata alone, the two somehow keep running into each other. Slowly, Ryou's malicious glee and Ogura's feelings of shame and unease begin to transform into something else...
