## Sinopse

Kunai Kumori comes from a long line of vampire ninjas and upon his 14th birthday, his father decides to entrust the family duties to him. He is now responsible for tracking down and killing other vampire ninjas. Armed only with his father’s sword and a strange ability that allows him to grow taller when he drinks the blood of others, he sets out on his mission. (Source: Baka-Updates)
