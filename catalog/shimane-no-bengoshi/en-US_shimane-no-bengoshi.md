## Sinopse

Among roughly twenty thousand lawyers in Japan, near half of them reside in the Tokyo Metropolitan area, and the rural prefecture of Shimane has only 26 lawyers. Mizuho Yamazaki, a fresh lawyer at the age of 26, starts her career as the 27th lawyer of Shimane Prefecture.

(Source: ANN)
