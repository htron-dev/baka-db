## Sinopse

Here is Pita-ten as you've never seen it before!
Koge-Donbo's loveable characters; Kotarou, Misha and Shia, are all here, illustrated in a unique, fresh style by the some of the biggest fans of the bestselling manga! Different manga-ka from Japan add their personal touch to the romantic series.
But it wouldn't be Pita-ten without an original tale from Koge-Donbo.
