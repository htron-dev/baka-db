## Sinopse

Story 1. Lately, nearing her 16th birthday, Emi is haunted by ghosts of a woman with severed head and her black cat.

Story 2. When trying to draw water from a newly restored old well, Sonomi and Keiko saw a pair of hand trying to climb the bucket's rope.

Story 3. Yumiko was a shy girl, but after meeting a ghost of girl standing outside of her house she feel different and more confident.

Story 4. Sakiko's mother is always harsh toward her. Once, she even tried to poison her friend and put the blame on her. Oneday, after arguing with Sakiko's father, she went missing.
