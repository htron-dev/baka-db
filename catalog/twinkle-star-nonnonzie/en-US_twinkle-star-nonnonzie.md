## Sinopse

A science fiction story set in 2264 which follows the adventures of 18 year old Nonnonzie, a beautiful young archeologist. Humans have moved out among the stars, and Nonnonzie spends her time searching the galaxy for "star pieces" and items such as pudding. Nonnonzie really likes pudding.

The series is full of Takemoto's signature cute characters.
