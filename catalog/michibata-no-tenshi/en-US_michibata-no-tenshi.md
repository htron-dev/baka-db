## Sinopse

Kyatoru who holds the mysterious book known as the Book of Ega. Her parents are murdered by someone who is after the book and she leaves on a journey to escape. During her rough journey, the ones who saved Kyatoru from exhaustion was people of the Arch family who makes a living as a Chinese Circus! A chinese fantasy of dream and adventure. (Source: M-U)
