## Sinopse

Story about a salaryman who was going to get married soon but his bride run away with another guy. His life sucks. He had to borrow a lot of money to buy a new house for his married life but ended up with nothing. Then his boss offered a solution: make his house into a girls' dormitory for female employees in the company. His new life begins here.
