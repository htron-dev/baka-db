## Sinopse

Hinata is an introverted geek who sits next to Mikage, a gal, in class. Hinata realizes that Mikage is a former friend from elementary school, and attempts to rekindle their friendship. Hinata is very meek and timid while Mikage is often overly enthusiastic often giving off the wrong vibe for what she is thinking...
