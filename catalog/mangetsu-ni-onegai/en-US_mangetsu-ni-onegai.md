## Sinopse

Once upon a full moon night, Purin and her boyfriend flew from the moon to the earth on a date. The wind was strong and they got separated away. Purin is firstly, picked up by a girl, mistaken her for a stuffed toy. In fact she and her boyfriend came from a moon and they can use magic to grant people's wish.

However, Purin is still amateur and she can only use huge magic spell only on a full moon night. She's also need her boyfriend's power to help them go back to the Moon.

Read the story of how Purin helps each girl that met her succeed in love and follow her journey to find her boyfriend.
