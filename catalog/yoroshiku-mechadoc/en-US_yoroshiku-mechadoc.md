## Sinopse

Jun Kazami works at the tuning shop "Mechadoc," and with the help of his friends, he upgrades regular, non-racing cars to take on his rivals in car races! The pages are full of authentic depiction of various machine parts, not to mention adrenaline-filled races that will leave you breathless.

(Source: Shueisha, translated)
