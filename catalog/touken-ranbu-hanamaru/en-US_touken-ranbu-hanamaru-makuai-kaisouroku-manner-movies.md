## Sinopse

Manner movies that aired right before the showing of Touken Ranbu: Hanamaru - Makuai Kaisouroku. The film was only shown for two weeks and each manner movie ran for a week. It shows the characters in chibi form in a theater teaching the viewers good etiquette before watching their film.
