## Sinopse

Yukihito Daigo is a teenage hacker who has been infected by the 'Dandelion' virus, which is said to brainwash humans and give them a new personality, combined with extraordinary speed and physical strength. But is that all there is to Dandelion? Daigo and his friends embark on a mission to discover the truth.
