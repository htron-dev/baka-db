## Sinopse

Based on an ero-game by the same name, Melty Moment follows Takeru as he gets closer to several girls in high school. He ends up leading an election committee that is filled with cute girls. There's shy little Natsuki, serious little Misao, his childhood friend Yuuka, classmate Sumire, and talented Aoi. Who will he end up with?
