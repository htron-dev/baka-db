## Sinopse

With the ability to see the negative emotions of people, and to see it as if they're monsters, whose character can be different and weird, she is Deng. Because no one can understand how she feels, a wall has been built around her heart. However, after living with her innocent brother and with the help with some good friends and Karas, the person that can consume evil intentions, her world has begun to change…
