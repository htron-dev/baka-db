## Sinopse

The future when humans live underground in order to escape radioactivity after the nuclear war.

ESP people who sometimes come into existence were called "P" that meant "Perplexer", evaded and persecuted.

Stella tries to notice her son's Srow is P and to commit suicide with him.
However, only Srow survives, and he is taken by Yang who is a close friend of Stella.
