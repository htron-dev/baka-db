## Sinopse

Lin, 17, is not a high school like any other. she makes strange dreams but she also have the power to see ghosts and talk to them. She still leads a pretty ordinary life. Until she is attacked by a monster and rescued by a young man with mysterious powers. He made ​​a surprising revelation: she's the guardian of the sacred Jade of Genesis and must protect it.
