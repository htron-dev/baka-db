## Sinopse

After waking up from a coma due to a traffic accident, 2nd year high school student Hiromi Setouchi finds himself in the body of an unknown girl called Sayu Nishimura. However, Nishimura suggests that they spend the rest of their lives in their new bodies...? A tale of how two people cope with the ups and downs of youth, while in each other's bodies!
