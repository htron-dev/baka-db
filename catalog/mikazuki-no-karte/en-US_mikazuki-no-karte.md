## Sinopse

Touka Myoujoubashi is a girl who has been hospitalized her entire life due to a rare allergy to sunlight, and thus has not been able to experience love. To prevent Touka from ending it all, Doctor Yuki Tsukisu provides her with the ultimate prescription by acting as her girlfriend.
