## Sinopse

Manga based of the Tales of Xillia Game for the PS3.

The story focuses on Milla Maxwell, a girl who boasts the title of "master of spirits." Faced with a change in which a large amount of spirits are disappearing, she leaves her shrine in Ni Akeria to find the origin of the irregularity. In front of a lab in Il Fahn, the city of night, she has a meeting with medical student Jude Mathis which changes both of their fates and the fate of the world for eternity.

(Source: ANN)
