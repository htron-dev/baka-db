## Sinopse

There's a girl who gets really excited over certain features in boys, such as their hair, hands, eyes... and basically everything else. She thinks herself a complete pervert, regardless of her childhood friend's protests.

Suddenly, a boy who has all of these beautiful traits comes storming into her life. The poor girl never saw it coming. Too bad this boy isn't as nice as his features portray him to be!

(Source: Wild\*Hope)
