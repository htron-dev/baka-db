## Sinopse

Continuing on from the previous film, this OVA features ghost Yat-chan teaching traffic safety to a martian visiting Earth. The martian is able to see and speak with the spirit of Yat-chan and Yat-chan vows to make sure the martian doesn't meet the same fate as him.
