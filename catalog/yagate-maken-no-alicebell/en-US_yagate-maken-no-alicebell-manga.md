## Sinopse

Seiji Harada usually avoids girls due to the fact that he believes that they are the source of all problems. As he enters his high school years, he is forced to join an all-girl school that recently began admitting male students. Now surrounded by all girls, he begins to realize that all members of this high school possess special powers, including himself.
