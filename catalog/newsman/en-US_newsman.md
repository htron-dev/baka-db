## Sinopse

Mineo Mutsu, an irreverencious reporter works for the Maiasa newspaper. He refuses to accept the misdoings of his job. He keeps on fighting and searching on how to reveal affairs everyone wants to kept quiet.

But by making such a racket, wouldn't Mineo risk his own career?
