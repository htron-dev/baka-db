## Sinopse

Kahori Onozuka presents us sweet and loving stories of teenagers. That need of talking about the feeling of love amongst the young is authentic andaddictive. She rpesents, through her 4 characters, the teenhood without makeup, with their questions and doubts. We find on them all the complexity of our teen desires and their hardships growing up and finding their place.

(from the back cover of the French edition by Editions Delcourt)
