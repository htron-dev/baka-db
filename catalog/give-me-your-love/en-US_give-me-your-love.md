## Sinopse

Compilation of various boy-love stories.

1. Give me your love- A coach's attempts to get a reaction from his expressionless student becomes sexual harassment.

2. I want to become love- Sequel to one.

3. Lets hold hands- Attracted to his stepbrother, kid leaves home and becomes an AV camera man, only to get mixed up in a police investigation with detective stepbrother.

4. Atonement- Gymnast feels guilty over his former friend's childhood trauma. Especially since the guy seems to be following him.

5. Switch- Helping his twin brother end an affair with a student gets a teacher tied up with a bomb on his chest.

6. The moon shine secretly- Hayami craves human blood, and Abe faints at the sight of it. So why are these two work rivals spending so much time together?
