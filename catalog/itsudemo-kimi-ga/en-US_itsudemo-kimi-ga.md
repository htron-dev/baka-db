## Sinopse

Takashi Aozuki, the best friend of Minase's beloved little brother transferred to the old dormitory where Minase lives. Minase's heart raise its beat to see Takashi grown mature. Meanwhile, Takashi had feelings for Minase. Minase was going to ask his little brother Tetsuo about Takashi but Takashi seems to have some secret!
