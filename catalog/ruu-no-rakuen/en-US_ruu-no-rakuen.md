## Sinopse

A cute story about a girl who loves dolphins (her father runs the aquarium where they live) and visits them often at a local theme park who becomes interested in a brooding young man - without realizing that his family owns the theme park where her beloved dolphins live, and whose father may be shutting the aquarium down...

(Source: Day of the River)
