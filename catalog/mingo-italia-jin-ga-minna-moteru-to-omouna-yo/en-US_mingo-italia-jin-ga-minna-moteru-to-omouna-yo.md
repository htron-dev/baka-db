## Sinopse

His love for the otaku culture led Italian Mingo to Japan in a quest to find a Japanese girlfriend. However, to maintain his lifestyle, he has to become a model. In a place where Italians are supposed to be popular, how could our low-esteemed otaku amico fit in?
