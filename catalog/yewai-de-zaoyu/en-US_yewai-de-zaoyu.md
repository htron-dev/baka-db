## Sinopse

The little flower pig's food was snatched by a crow, so he follows the crow far away. However, the little pig who was left behind began crying because he couldn't find the little flower pig. The little yellow dog asks the little pig why he was crying and the little yellow dog then helps the little pig find the little flower pig.
