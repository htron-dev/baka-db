Undoubtedly, to call Ranto, the fortune-teller detective. But, what if he appears wearing a talking t-shirt, a fussy girlfriend, hairdresser that falls in love easily, and a Sushi's King candidate?
The resulting situation just can be this funny comedy by Satoru Akahori and Hitoshi Okuda.
