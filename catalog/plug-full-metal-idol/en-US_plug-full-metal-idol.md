## Sinopse

A Dangerous USA weapon called "Auto-doll" was created for the purpose to destroy enemies of war against USA. The Auto Doll calls herself "Plug" and a Japanese talent agent scouts her as #1 Japanese Idol! As they go through trials in order to become the best Idol in Japan. Opposing organizations attempt to dispose of Plug!!
