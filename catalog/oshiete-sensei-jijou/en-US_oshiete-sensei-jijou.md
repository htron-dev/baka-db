## Sinopse

This book contains 5 short oneshot stories about a middle school tutor and a student who loves her, a family-oriented girl who meets an egotistic guy, a girl obsessed with chests who's secret is found out by a cute popular guy, a girl who worries about her boyfriend who's 2 years older than her, and a shoujo manga obsessed girl who finds her prince!
