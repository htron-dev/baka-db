## Sinopse

Asuka Miyazaki, 33, finally realizes after their best friend's marriage that they are attracted to women. However, they are not a lesbian, as they are x-gender/non-binary. The story follows Asuka's journey to figure out how they'll manage to get into a happy relationship with a woman.
