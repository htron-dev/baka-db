## Sinopse

Hime is a natural blonde and with that comes basic expectations: for her to excel at everything and to speak English proficiently, and unfortunately to be a complete ditz and have many boyfriends. When Minami catches her studying English by listening to 'English for Dummies' the two become friends. But soon Minami falls prey to the rumors and distances himself from Hime. Will the two ever be able to regain their friendship?
