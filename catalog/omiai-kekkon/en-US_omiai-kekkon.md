## Sinopse

One long comedy between two families who attempt japanese style marriage meeting and various short stories: mysterious story of oriental art detective curators, comedy about two families like cat and dog, the friendship story between a rich intelligent boy but in delicate health and a foolish but healthy poor boy, two experimental sketches about two mythical boys and man/robot.
