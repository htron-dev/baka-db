## Sinopse

Dylan is coming home to Saint Paul! Maddie, who lives in Dylan's parents' boardinghouse, is in a restless mood. Dylan was a person Maddie admired, her first love, which ended unrequited. But now, fourteen years later, things are different. Maddie has a boyfriend, so despite living under the same roof as Dylan, she shouldn't have anything to worry about. On the other hand, upon seeing him again, Maddie finds Dylan more attractive than ever...
