## Sinopse

Tatsuya intends to extort a male subway groper for money, but ends up being threatened and confined himself. But then the groper, Morinaka, leaves Tatusya alone and molests another man right in front of him. Then, on the 8th floor of a building, foolish passion is unleashed!
