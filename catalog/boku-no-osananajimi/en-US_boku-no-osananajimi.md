## Sinopse

Boku no Osananajimi is a collection of short stories by Yuuki Fujinari about the romantic tensions between childhood friends.

The six stories included are:
Futari no Tokei
Afuredasu Omoi
Massugu ni Mitsumete
MAJIKURU ROMANSU
Fujun na Oresama, Junna Kare
Fuku Kaichou to Fuuki Iin no Juunan
