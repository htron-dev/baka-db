## Sinopse

Jun has been married to her husband, Takeyori, for eight years. They haven't had sex in a long time, and Jun is starting to wonder if they'll ever have kids. They get along, but nothing is moving forward. Through the tension of these unknowns, Jun decides to go back to working full-time. Soon after, Jun and Takeyori meet people that could change the future of their marriage.
