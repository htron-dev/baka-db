## Sinopse

A high school freshman visits an ex-teacher who has become a recluse. This teacher, a beautiful and loving woman, who helped him until high school, but gave up teaching and decided to lead a life of seclusion. He accidentally saw her drinking blood and having an aversion to light, leading to the possibility that she might be a vampire.
