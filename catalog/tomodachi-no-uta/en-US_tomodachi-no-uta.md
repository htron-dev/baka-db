## Sinopse

Akane is a nice and cheerful girl whose dream has always been to become a singer and reach many people with her voice. To achieve it, she even gave up on having a normal and fun time with her high school friends... until one day she meets a girl, Fumi, and by befriending her undergoes major changes in her life. This is the story of their friendship and their shared love for music.
