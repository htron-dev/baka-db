## Sinopse

Count Diesent Family who're descendants of Cinderella is collecting shoes with history—Edel, who has heard such rumours, to have appraised a memento of her mother, the "red shoes" that move by themselves, she goes to visit the next family head—Alan. However, what he told her...was a surprising truth about Cinderella!! To learn the truth of her mother's death, will Edel, who took up the shoes, in spite of her extreme shyness work in Alan's shoe store "Glass Dome"!? A love story about shoes presented by a novice shoemaker & overbearing young master.
