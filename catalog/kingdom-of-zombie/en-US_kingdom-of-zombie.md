## Sinopse

Twenty years after a zombie outbreak Van, a mischievous child, escapes from the house of his grandfather to watch a gladiatorial tournament. In his way, Van is asked to deliver a letter to his hero at the gates where the zombies are being held, but a mysterious man kills the guards and lets the zombies escape. Being attacked by the zombies Van shows to have powers and starts to counterattack them.

Note: This series was cancelled.
