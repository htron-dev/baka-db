## Sinopse

Next to the home of a girl named Mutsumi who loves Kyoto Fushimi's sweets, comes a guy from Tokyo with a same surname, Kurasawa Kojirou. Even though he looks very handsome, he looks kind of scary as she don't know what he is thinking. Mutsumi was swayed around as they are in the same class even in high school. What kind of romance will spark between them?
