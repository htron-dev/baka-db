## Sinopse

"From now on you will be my Goshujin-sama" A mysterious man came out of nowhere and proclaimed to Akiyuki--Young business man Souichirou-- Not only did he take advantage of Akiyuki's body against his will, but he turned him into a 'maid' exclusively to attend all of his needs. How will Akiyuki deal with Souichirou's unsolicited advances, how will he get out of this predicament?
