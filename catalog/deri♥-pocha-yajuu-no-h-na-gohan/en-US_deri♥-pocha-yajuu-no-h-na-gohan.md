## Sinopse

Kouta shares his hand-made lunch with the popular and handsome boy Shibusawa senpai, who had fainted at school due to hunger. Shibusawa has a small appetite and is rumored not to eat hand-made obentos, but eats happily as if he likes it.They spend the day together with Kouta's handmade lunch.

But one day, Shibusawa senpai suddenly changes, saying "Kouta is delicious!" And the way he's pushed down...?!
