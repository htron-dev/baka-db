## Sinopse

The Japan Thanatos Society provides illicit euthanasia services. Akira Kurushima is the man who judges whether you should live or die. He is the Thanatos Society's investigator-doctor who brings a tranquil death to those who call. Today, he will again bring peace to those who desire death.
