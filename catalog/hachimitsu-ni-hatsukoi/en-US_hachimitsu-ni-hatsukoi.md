## Sinopse

Love... Friendship... Where does one begin? Where does the other end? Neighbors and childhood friends Koharu and Natsuki are inseparable to the point that all their friends would like to see them as a couple. Our heroes have no intention to satisfy them, but their entrance to the high school will put their feelings upside down and put them to the test.
