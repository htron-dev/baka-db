## Sinopse

Hong Kong, the world of Yakuza, is the city of the night. In Cheemun, two forces are contending to appropriate the right to the regions. Controlling the reign, San Fei Fuu is a young leader who has both skill and popularity. On the opposing side is Fei. In order to crush Fei and increase their territory, the newcomers Wen Li engage a strategist from America named Mei. However, Mei, with his cold good looks and intellect, seems to exhibit an attachment towards Fei that borders on bizarre...?!
