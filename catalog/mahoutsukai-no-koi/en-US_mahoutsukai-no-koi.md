## Sinopse

Without worries about girls or money, half-heartedly living Nishizaki meets the love of his life on campus. A lover who bites his lips when happy... Araya is always troublesome, gloomy, and without desire. Thinking of him, Nishizaki’s teeth always hurt...

Other than the title series, the “Your Everything” series, “Aurora Railway” and “The Owner Up Above” is also included.
