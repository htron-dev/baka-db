## Sinopse

A collection of short stories. One of them is called From the Right.

• From the Right -
Keigo and Keisuke are brothers and are both in love with Takafumi. Keigo is blind in his right eye and Takafumi and Keisuke take advantage of it by doing things like mutual masturbation in the movie theatre to Keigo's right side. When Keisuke has an accident, Keigo goes to see Takafumi in New York where he's working to tell him Keisuke is dead and lives on in Keigo's new right cornea. When Takafumi reacts to the knowledge, Keigo takes advantage, but will be ever be able to be Keigo with Takafumi, or will he always be Keisuke?
