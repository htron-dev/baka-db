## Sinopse

Tokyo is being ruled by several tribes, or gangs. They respect eachother and live peaceful. But then some stupid members of the Musashinokuni Saru decide to go to club Saga which is in the Wu Ronz Territory. The peace between all tribes are being tested now as the Wu Ronz and the Saru's prepare for a power clash.
