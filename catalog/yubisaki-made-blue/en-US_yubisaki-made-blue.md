## Sinopse

Motohashi Kikuno is on the train to her first day of high school when she meets a boy who helps her out of a small jam. She meets her best friend Yuiko at school and discovers that she now has a boyfriend. Then the next day Yuiko’s boyfriend dumps her – and it turns out to be the boy from the train!
