## Sinopse

The prisoner Akeboshi Kurou who was being transferred to a prison in Tokyo is involved in a plane hijacking, in the process he tries to save a girl who was taken as a hostage. But the plane is attacked by a herd of crows and a monster raven that make the plane crash.
