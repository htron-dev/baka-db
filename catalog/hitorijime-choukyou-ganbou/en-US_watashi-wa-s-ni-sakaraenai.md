To pay for her father’s debts. Poor, virgin Miku ended up applying to be the lover of a distantly related CEO, and takes "lover lessons" from a handsome butler. On the sofa of a mansion... in a suite of a high class hotel... undergoing H lectures from the butler causes Miku who is a virgin to weaken...!?
