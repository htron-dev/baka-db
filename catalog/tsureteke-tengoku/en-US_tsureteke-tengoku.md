## Sinopse

Ever since Fumiya and his family moved next to a graveyard, Fumiya constantly sees spirits and ghosts. Luckily, His childhood friend Shogo, the son of a Buddhist priest, is always ready to help chase away the problem -- for a price. Somehow, though, it seems that the more this happens, the closer they become...
