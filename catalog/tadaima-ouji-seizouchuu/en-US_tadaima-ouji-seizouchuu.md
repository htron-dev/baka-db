## Sinopse

Do you believe in the "Prince" that exists in fairy tales?

Murata Ouji-kun is handsome, has excellent grades and excels in sports. Nobody at school can resist his charm except one, Tsukamoto Saki, and that's because she produced this "Prince"?!

Included one-shots: Hyoutenka Ouji-sama!!, Tsumetai Kareshi no Tokashikata, Uzai Joshi demo Koishite Ii desu ka?
