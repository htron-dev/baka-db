## Sinopse

Miri (16) is surprised one day to find out that she is actually a twin separated a birth! Raised by her Yakuza father, she's been brought up to be boyish and manly. On the other hand, her twin, Momo, has been brought up to be girly and cute! The catch is that Momo is actually a boy! Then, Miri gets scouted by a talent agent....as a male model! Join the two twins, with the model Hikaru and his roommates Hideki and Katsuya for some crazy fun!
