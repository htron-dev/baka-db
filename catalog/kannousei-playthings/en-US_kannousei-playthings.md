## Sinopse

Bartender Yura, who works at a BDSM club, likes to tempt arrogant men that looks down on him. He met Chihiro that just happens to be his type. Curious sexual questions between the two led them to spend the night together, and it seems both are after something and making use of one another. Chihiro, who lasts long in bed, has found Yura to be an enthralling toy, while Yura has found the perfect arrogant guy that he'd enjoy playing with.
