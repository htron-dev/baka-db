## Sinopse

Kawasaki and Fujita have been best friends since early childhood. Kawasaki has secretly been carrying a torch for his cheerful buddy, but Fujita only has eyes for the girls around him. The delicate balance of their relationship gets shaken when Fujita gets himself a girlfriend in high school. What is Kawasaki supposed to do with his feelings now?
