## Sinopse

Watase Marin has a special ability―she is able to see ghosts. However, there's a catch―ghosts are able to take over her body if she makes eye contact with them! What happens when a cute girl ghost decides to stir up some trouble and make her confess, then kiss Hori-kun, a guy in her class that she likes?
