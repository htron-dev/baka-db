## Sinopse

Ichinose Kaname, 22 years old and graduating Psychology, makes her brother’s detective agency her preferred career choice. But that’s a world of sly dogs and old troopers. Will the newbie female detective Kaname manage to get her footing in it? And what part will her new supervisor, Uesugi Shinya play?
