## Sinopse

A promotional anime for Nissan Soap's Fafa laundry mascot. These two ONA films were promoting the AiR Fafa project. The project itself was a type of augmented reality app that people could play with.

North American viewers may recognize Fafa under a different name "Snuggle". The mascot for the fabric softener brand owned by Sun Products.
