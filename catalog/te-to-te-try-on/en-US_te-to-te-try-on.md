## Sinopse

Washizuka Shinichiro has just woken up after returning to Japan with his father the hard way, with a collar on his neck and his father gone, leaving a note that he’ll be back in 2-3 years and saying that he’s been left on the grounds of a prestigious school and he’s been enrolled. But before he can get there he has to find the school and use all of his survival skills to get through a high-tech environment gone awry, along with a female student he found sunbathing…
