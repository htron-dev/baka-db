## Sinopse

A spin-off of Senyuu. bundled with the first press release of the second Blu-ray/DVD volumes of the anime.

Spin-off focusing on Hero #14, Foyfoy Dran, and his royal soldier, Rudolf I. Athena. This story takes place during pre-series. Foyfoy and Rudolf are on a journey to subdue the Demon Lord. Foyfoy simply cares about the reward... but is that really the only thing he cares about?
