## Sinopse

In this special edition of the teenage basketball manga classic, "Dear Boys," Takumi Fujiwara and Ranmaru Miura of Mizuho Junior High meet Tsutomu Ishii and Kenji Dobashi from Takakura Junior High at a Mizuho High basketball game. The four boys aim to be the number one basketball player.
