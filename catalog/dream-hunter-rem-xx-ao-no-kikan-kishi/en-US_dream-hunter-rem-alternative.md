## Sinopse

A sequel to a classic (yet little known outside Japan) OVA from the 1980's featuring a private detective investigating and exterminating demons inside dreams. Armed with mahou shoujo powers and bikini armor transformation, Rem is back and investigating a multiple missing persons case.

In her investigation, Rem meets another dream hunter! Who is she and could she also be a dream guardian like Rem?
