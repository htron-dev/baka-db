## Sinopse

After a long platonic relation, Makoto Totsugawa and his girlfriend are finally going to make love, but it is without counting on the arrival of Shihori, the big sister of Mokoto. This last one, by understanding the situation, loses her composure. But why?

Between jealousy and sensualism follow the sensual adventures of little wild young ladies...
