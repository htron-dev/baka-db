## Sinopse

Manga version of ‘Les enfants Terribles’ by Jean Cocteau.
Paul is obliged to stay in his room while recovering from an injury he received when he was hit by a rock hidden in a snowball. But for some reason he doesn’t seem to be able to leave the room anymore...
