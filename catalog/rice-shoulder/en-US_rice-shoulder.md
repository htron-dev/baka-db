## Sinopse

The story revolves around a 202-pound, 18-year old girl named Okome Akino (whose name is a pun of "autumn rice"). After Okome enters a sumo match and wins against a male amateur fighter by a landslide, a trainer named Akane scouts her, and she enters the world of women's boxing.

(Source: ANN)
