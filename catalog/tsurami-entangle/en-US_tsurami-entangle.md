## Sinopse

Super negative girl Akira is a self-proclaimed internet idol inside of her blog and she refuses to go to school. After some convincing and persuasion from her friends, she finds herself a goal: a goal to become a professional voice actress. Now she must attempt to enter the real world and lead a 'normal' school girl life. Will she make it?
