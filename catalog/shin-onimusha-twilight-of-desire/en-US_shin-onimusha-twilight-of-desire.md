## Sinopse

Based on the popular Capcom videogame series Onimusha, Twilight of Desire fills in more of backstory of this rich world and its characters. In this all-new chapter, Samanosuke clashes blades with Toyotomi Hidetsugu, nephew of the villainous Toyotomi Hideyoshi. Plus, learn more about how Hideyoshi came to utilize the dangerous Genma Power.
