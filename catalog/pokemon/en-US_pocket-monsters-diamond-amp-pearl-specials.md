## Sinopse

One-hour special split into 2 episodes during the airing of Pokemon Best Wishes! featuring the old characters from the Diamond & Pearl series Hikari and Takeshi with their Pokemon.

Episode 1 - ヒカリ・新たなる旅立ち! (Hikari, Arata Naru Tabidachi!):
Hikari - Setting off on a New Journey!

Episode 2 - ニビジム・史上最大の危機! (Nibi Gym, Shijou Saidai no Kiki!):
Nibi Gym - The Greatest Crisis Ever!
