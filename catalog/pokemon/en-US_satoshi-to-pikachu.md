## Sinopse

A tumultuous gale storm, a gym battle between Pokemon, encounters with various animals and creatures, an exciting quest down into the depths of a sealed maze, thus heightening the thrills Ash and his friends experience during their trip into the unexpected! Will Ash and his buddies brave all danger and make it out alive? Read all about it in this comic book adaptation of the popular anime series. It’s red-hot!!
