## Sinopse

After defeating Mask of Ice, Red and Gold adventure towards Mt. Silver to train together. For three days, Pika, Red's Pikachu, helps train Pibu, Gold's Pichu. While in the midst of their training, Pibu is suddenly kidnapped by an unknown attacker! With Gold receiving an injury from the anonymous kidnapper, Red and Pika run off to rescue Pibu using the experience they have built over the past years to help defeat their foe.
