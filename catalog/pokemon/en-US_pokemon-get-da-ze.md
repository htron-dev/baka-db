## Sinopse

Pokemon Get da ze! is a comedy-adventure series about a Pokémon trainer named Shuu, who can communicate with his Pokémon, such as his Pikachu, through a headset.

The series is mostly composed of short stories about his attempts to make up for his lackluster skills in capturing Pokémon.
