## Sinopse

Pokémon Chamo-Chamo☆Pretty♪ is a manga series by the shōjo author Yumi Tsukirino and is a spin-off of Magical Pokémon Journey.

The series is a comedy-styled manga which follows the continuing adventures of Pikachu and Clefairy who are joined by new friends Torchic and Mightyena.
