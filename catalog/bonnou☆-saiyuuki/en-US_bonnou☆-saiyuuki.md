## Sinopse

The manga is a reimagining of the famous Journey to the West story, and centers on the Buddhist priest Hoshi Sanzo and his journey to India as he is accompanied by three beautiful and charming women: Son Gokuu, Cho Hakkai, and Sha Gojyou.

(Source: ANN, edited)
