## Sinopse

He's always liked tall men, even better if they have big hands and mouths. And clumsy ones are easy to handle... This is a pure love story about a capable and stoic man. This anthology contains six stories, including the title story and other works about an elementary school teacher, police officer, nursery school teacher, and office worker!
