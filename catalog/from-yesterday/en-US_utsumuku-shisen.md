Two lonely people who suffered the loss of their loved ones...The love story of our boys, Kaoru and Yoshiyuki, from strangers to lovers, how strong will their love be? Is it just for the sake of comforting? Share their love and feel the bond between them.

Hasumi and Ryuichi from From Yesterday make a cameo appearance.
