## Sinopse

The ever cool and manly Uito has a sweet, cutesy side he shows only to Sei...! They don't miss an opportunity to smooch whenever their parents are out, and work hard on their finals and part-time jobs so they can live together one day. It's no surprise they go wild on their graduation trip...! Who fell in love first? This story follows two childhood friends who spend their school days totally besotted with each other!
