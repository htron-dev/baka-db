## Sinopse

When 16-year-old Haruna runs away from an arranged marriage, her parents forced her younger twin sister Wakaba into the marriage instead. But while Haruna ran off with a boyfriend, Wakaba has never even been kissed. How will a high school girl survive going from nothing to a full-on marriage with no steps in between?
