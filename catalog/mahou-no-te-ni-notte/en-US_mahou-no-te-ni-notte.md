## Sinopse

Hachimoto Satsuki is an aspiring hairstylist who meets with legendary "Magical Hairstylist" Hayama Ryuichi, who allows her to work for him. Fairly entertaining situations grow from this relationship as Satsuki meets others and learns more about being a hairstylist.
