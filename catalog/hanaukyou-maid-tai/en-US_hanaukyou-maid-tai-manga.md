## Sinopse

After his mother died, a 15-year-old boy named Taro is invited by his grandfather to the Hanaukyou mansion. Soon after he arrives, his grandfather disappears, and Taro learns that not only will he inherit the unbelievably huge estate, he will also have access to the countless beautiful young maids working there.
