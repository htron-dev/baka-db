## Sinopse

Ayumu gets dumped by her boyfriend because he claims that he hates women who are better at his job than he is. Although she decides to have a change in attitude, another attractive coworker who entered the company at the same time as her, Shunsuke, tries to cheer her up, which makes her mad, but ends up being the start of something more between the two...
