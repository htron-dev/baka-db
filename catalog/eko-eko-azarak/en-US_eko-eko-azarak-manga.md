## Sinopse

When the chant "Eko Eko Azarak" sounds, something is bound to occur, be it within the town or the school!

A classic horror manga, Eko Eko Azarak follows the witch Misa Kuroi, and the various "horrors" she encounters during her travels.

(Source: Batoto)
