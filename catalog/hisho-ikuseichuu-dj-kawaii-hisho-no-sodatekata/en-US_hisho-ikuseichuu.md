## Sinopse

Kazuya Aki's not very bright, so when the company he works for takes off in the middle of the night, he's left to deal with the creditors. Munekata Kashi is the president of the company that is owed the most money and he has some creative ways for Aki to pay it back. Souichirou Takaba is Kashi's long suffering secretary, but are his intentions toward Aki completely innocent? (B-U)
