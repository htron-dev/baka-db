## Sinopse

Set in 19th Century California, the story follows Brad, a handsome young bounty-hunting playboy who's one day saved from certain death at the hands of supernatural forces by The Dark Hunter: a seemingly immortal man who works for the Viper Detective Agency's Special Investigation Department.
