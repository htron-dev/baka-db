## Sinopse

In the distant future, there exists a man named Ragnarok Guy, aka the Godkiller, the sole survivor of an elite band of superhuman soldiers. But now, he lives his life as a fugitive, pursued by a massive federation who wants nothing but to wipe him out. What are his secrets? And what is his goal? Find out in this action-packed sci-fi epic!
