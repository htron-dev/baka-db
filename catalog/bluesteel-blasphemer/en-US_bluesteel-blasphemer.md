## Sinopse

Amano Yukinari, a young gunmaniac, gets reincarnated into another world by an alchemist and starts traveling the land with said alchemist's younger sister Daša Urban. After he kills a local deity by using his newfound powers and gun knowledge, the locals revere him as the new deity.
