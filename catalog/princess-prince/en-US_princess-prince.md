## Sinopse

A king in mourning of the loss of his precious wife is warned by a fairy to raise one of his twin sons as a girl and the other as a boy in order to protect his kingdom's secret. Now as they reach adulthood, they find the very secret that they were raised to keep and must protect it from the evil that seeks to abuse it.

(Source: ANN)
