Welcome to the 82nd Precinct, also known as the Crocodile Precinct, which is filled with cops that never let go once they sink their teeth into something. Leading them is Hino Mika, a female cop who's as dangerous as she is beautiful. Follow her on her adventures in this sexy action suspense series.
