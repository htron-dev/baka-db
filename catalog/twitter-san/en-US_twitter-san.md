## Sinopse

"Twitter" is a web service that is extremely popular all over the world now posting and sharing short sentences with 140 characters or less. And this is a rumor...there seems to be a humanoid interface to observe users and develop functions in order to improve the contents of the service more. Yes, this girl is "Twitter-san."
