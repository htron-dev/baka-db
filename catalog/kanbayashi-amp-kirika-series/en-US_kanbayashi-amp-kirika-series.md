## Sinopse

In the middle of searching for his ideal Lady X, Kanbayashi Toshihiko, a young popular writer meets a popular teenage artist, Kawamoto Kirika. Together they solve a lot of cases and go through many things.
With Kirika's help, will Kanbayashi find his Lady X?
