## Sinopse

Long after the zombie apocalypse ends civilization, the survivors do their best to go about their daily lives. On one such day, the survivor Jin happens to save the life of a white cat, who then starts to keep him company. This is their day to day life together as they survive and look for an "island" where Jin's wife may yet still live.
