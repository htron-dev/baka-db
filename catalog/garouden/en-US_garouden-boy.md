## Sinopse

Based on the Garouden manga. A short 2 volume manga about two males whose goals are to become number one in life (and their success). The plot outlines the protagonist, Tanba and a former sumo wrestller turned high school teacher, Kiriu and their head to head combat, from the eyes and views of the main character, Shinichi Kido.
