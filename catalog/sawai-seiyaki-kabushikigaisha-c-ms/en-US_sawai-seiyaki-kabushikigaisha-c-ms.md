## Sinopse

Sawai Pharmaceutical Co., Ltd commissioned commercials. This entry includes:

"Jouhouteikyou ni Tsuite" an informative piece about how the company information provision.

"Okusuri Nomeru ka na?" a musical video for mothers and children. It tries to help facilitate children to taking their medicine through a happy song with other animals. It was created in conjunction with tricks and psychology from the Osaka Maternal and Childcare Center as well as the Japan Acoustic Laboratory.
