## Sinopse

The company Nagase is famous for its high productivity and efficiency levels of its employees, all they owe to their rule "not allowed romances between co-workers." When Shinya Izumino gains entry to the company intends to break that rule, however Andou manager will be responsible for their training. Andou will show you how to work efficiently and will teach Andou Izumino enamored of truth. This is your love training.
