## Sinopse

Rururuko is a human orphan lost in space. She is raised on board a space ship operated by the spacefaring Saurus race, but she dreams of someday returning home to earth. Before she gets there, she and her tiger-striped kitten Fuuna will have many adventures with talking dogs, space pirates, and all sorts of other interesting people who inhabit the vastnass of the universe.
