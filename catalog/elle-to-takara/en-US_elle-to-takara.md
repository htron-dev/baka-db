## Sinopse

Elle is a wolf cub, on his way to being an adult in his tribe. To do this he must hunt and capture his prey! His prey, however, is Takara a rabbit who is much too big, and often is able to dodge the traps Elle sets for him. Though unbeknown to Takara, Elle has one final trick up his sleeve. Will Elle finally be able to capture his elusive prey?
