## Sinopse

Mei has always loved the older Shino, even when he became the school doctor. When Shino seemingly tries to end any relationship they had, Mei attempts to give up her own feelings. But does Shino really feel nothing at all for Mei?

The extra is a prologue to Watashi no Megane-kun.
