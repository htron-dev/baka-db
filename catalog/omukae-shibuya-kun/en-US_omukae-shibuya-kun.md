## Sinopse

Aika Aota is a 28-year-old teacher who lives her day-to-day full of harmony in the nursery. She tries so hard in her work that she does not have the time to go out and find a boyfriend. But this is until Taikai Shibuya comes into her life, a mysterious boy who will make her routine change completely.
