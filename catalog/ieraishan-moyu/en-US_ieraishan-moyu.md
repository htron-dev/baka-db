## Sinopse

The heir of a rich viscount falls in love with a magazine reporter. The “heir boy” dies in a house fire. Seven years later, the reporter finds his “dead” lover, now a high profile male prostitute. Together, they fall into the darkness of political scandal and human smuggling.
