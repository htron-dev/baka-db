Hina's giant secret is in jeopardy! He's the head of a family who are descendants of the wolf, and not even his friends on the student council know his strange legacy. But when Hina starts sneaking out during the full moon, it's only a matter of time before things get deliciously complicated! And what will happen if animal instinct finally overrides human control?
