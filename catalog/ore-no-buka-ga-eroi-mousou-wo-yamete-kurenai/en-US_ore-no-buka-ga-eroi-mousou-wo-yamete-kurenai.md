## Sinopse

Sayama, who possesses the power to read people's minds, accidentally sneaks into the mind of his subordinate, Kaneko, during a meeting one day. Inside, there are all kinds of erotic play, such as blindfolds, restraints, and verbal abuse that can't be imagined from the seriousness of his face as he takes notes... And the most shocking thing is the subject of these fantasies is him?! Despite his confusion regarding the reason why the other party is himself, Sayama can't help but to be curious. Even when he tries to avoid running into him, this time, Kaneko appears in his dream...!? A person who likes to have erotic fantasies with a psychic superior. This is a psychic love with unstoppable lewd fantasies involving bondage, blindfolds, verbal abuse, toys, tentacles, etc.!
