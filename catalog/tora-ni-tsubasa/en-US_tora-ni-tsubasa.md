## Sinopse

In this world many people have "gifts." Not the kind of gift that can be given to someone else, but rather a natural born ability. Artistic talent or figure like a gorgeous model, there are many kinds of gifts. Among all of them Yamashita poses a rather unusual gift that should be kept hidden.
