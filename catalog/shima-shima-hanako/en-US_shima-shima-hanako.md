## Sinopse

Fu Yamanaka is a high school girl. She lives with "Uncle,' who runs the "Shima Shima" coffee shop. One day, an adorable girl falls from the sky right in front of their shop! The girl, whose name is Hana, is a fox who was saved in the mountains, and she's here to repay that act of kindness. Along with her watchdog Tsugo, the two girls begin their adorable and carefree days together! This sweet comedy about repaying kindness will warm your heart!!

(Source: Manga Box)
