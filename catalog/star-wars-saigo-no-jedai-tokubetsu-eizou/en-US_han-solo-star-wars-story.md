## Sinopse

Two shorts created by Disney Japan in honor of Solo: A Star Wars Story being released on MovieNEX, which is a set item of Blu-ray disc, DVD, and digital copy (cloud compatible) release of films (essentially every home-media release available as a single purchase). MovieNEX is unique to the Japanese market.
