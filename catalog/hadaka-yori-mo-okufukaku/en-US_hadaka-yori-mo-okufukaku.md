## Sinopse

Hagiwara, who works a desk job at a college, picks Yuuto up at a club. Yuuto states his inexperience, and Hagiwara, attracted to his good looks and hot body, decides to show him the ropes. The two part ways, albeit reluctantly, after an enjoyable night together... only to cross paths again...! Despite Hagiwara's shock, Yuuto coolly suggests they become friends with benefits!
