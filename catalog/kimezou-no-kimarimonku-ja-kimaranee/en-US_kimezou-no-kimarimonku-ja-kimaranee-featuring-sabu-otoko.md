## Sinopse

Follows the story of young employee of a company that produces star-shaped confections. The unusual man views society cynically and does not fit typical Japanese competitive male stereotypes.

He has to contend with a variety of coworkers including the flamboyant Kimezou, the company president, and the president's daughter.

(Source: ANN)
