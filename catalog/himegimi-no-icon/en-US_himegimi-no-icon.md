## Sinopse

Asakura Aki is her middle school's track star. She's in love with her neighbor and childhood friend, Shiiba Chihaya. There's news that a transfer student is coming with a track scholarship. Aki doesn't even lose to boys, so they decide to square off in a 100 meter race!
