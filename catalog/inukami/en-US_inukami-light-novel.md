## Sinopse

About the dog goddess Youko and her master Kawahira Keita and their fighting against various troublesome spirits. It has been adapted into a manga by artist Matsuzawa Mari and into an anime by Seven Arcs. Several more inukami besides Youko also play an important role in the novel, most notably the ten inukami of Kawahira Kaoru, Keita's brother.

(Source: MU)
