## Sinopse

Possessed of aristocratic blood but bereft of money, the impoverished Baron Miharco travels the galaxy in search of his ideal woman, attended by his robot valet Lampachika. This is the ballad of his itinerant travels, and of the adventures that await him!

(Source: Shinchosha, translated)
