## Sinopse

What will happen to this Ossan after being hurled into another world? Together with his wolf buddy, he lives a relaxing life. He hasn’t battled much, but this Ossan was strong originally. He hates self-important people. And since his family has increased, he’s surrounded in fluff.

Now, as a result of living such a relaxing life, trouble loves him.
