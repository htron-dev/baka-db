## Sinopse

What will happen to an old man after being hurled into another world?

Together with his wolf buddy, he lives a relaxing life. He originally was strong, but hasn't battled much. He hates self-important people. And since his family has increased, he's surrounded in fluff.

Now, as a result of living such a relaxing life, trouble loves him...
