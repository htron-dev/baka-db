## Sinopse

A man lost his lover years ago, yet he is still unable to move on. Now supporting his lover's child he works as a host. A young master of a yakuza clan meets this man by coincidence and instantly becomes intoxicated by his passion fruit scent....but will this man be able to break him free from the past that still binds him?
