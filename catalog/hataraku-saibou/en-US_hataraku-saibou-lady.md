## Sinopse

A beloved internal story dedicated to all living ladies. Menstruation, pregnancy, childbirth, female-specific upsets and illnesses. The body of a woman who is exposed to rapid changes every day. "We (immune cells) protect the young lady (inside the body)!"

A spin-off specializing in "women" from the blockbuster cell anthropomorphic manga "Cells at Work!"

(Source: Kodansha, translated)
