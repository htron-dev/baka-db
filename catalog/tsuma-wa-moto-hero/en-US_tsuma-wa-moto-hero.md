## Sinopse

In a world where heroes protect peace at all costs, the retired hero Telephone has a shocking revelation... she was a woman! Unbeknownst to her partner Teddy, he says some final words of goodbye... which she interprets as a proposal! They get married, and a life more exciting than the hero industry begins!
