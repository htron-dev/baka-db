## Sinopse

Silver Arrow ・ Night Eyes War is the 4th book of the Silver Arrow series. Arrow is a high profile Jewelry thief, he is sometimes can be seen with Alex, his childhood friends and Shallon Hyden, the daughter of the rich Hyden family. Even though he likes to steal, Arrow hates the idea of killing, he often get involves in helping someone and stealing something at the same time.
