## Sinopse

After her dad remarries, Ema moves into the Asahina Family House and acquires 13 brothers. Ema intended to live peacefully with them as a family, but the sudden appearance of a girl in an all male household causes discord. Going on with their lives, the brother’s feelings for Ema change and the start of a same roof forbidden romance begins.

(Source: Coyomoose)
