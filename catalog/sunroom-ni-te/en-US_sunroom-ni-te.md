## Sinopse

In December of 1970, Takemiya published a short story, "In the Sunroom" (in Bessatsu Shojo Comic) which is possibly the first shonen-ai manga published and contains the earliest known male-male kiss in shoujo manga.

The manga volume consists of five shorter stories:

-   Sunroom ni te (In the Sunroom, 1970)
-   Hohoemu Shounen (The Laughing Boy, 1972)
-   20 no Hiru to Yoru (20 Days and Nights, 1973)
-   Star! (1974)
-   Mister no Kotori (The Mr.'s Baby Bird, 1975)
