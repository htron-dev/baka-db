Chie Souma is a college student who is depressed because she can't find work. She wanted to enter one of her beloved fantasy novels, and while she was avoiding reality, the ground suddenly started shaking, and she lost consciousness. When she woke up, she didn't recognize where she was. It seemed that she had tripped into another world. In another world trip novels, looking like a woman was supposed to be dangerous...
