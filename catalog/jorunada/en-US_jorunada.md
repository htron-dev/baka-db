## Sinopse

« In fact, heroes do not exist. Some men are good, others less »

Between dream and reality, is it possible to assume our own sexual fantasies? That's the question that arises in Kahori Onozucca Jornada, by confronting its heroines to particularly harsh situations. Yet this is often in excess of their false guilt and accepting their own choices that young women in her stories will grow up. A borderline book with a troubling psychological precision...
