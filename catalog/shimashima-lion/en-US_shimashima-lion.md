## Sinopse

Shimazaki Mako used to be a zebra, but she hated her life of being chased by lions, so now she's turned into a human. But in the apartment next door is a former lion...!?

It's wildlife survival of the fittest for these former animals turned high school girls on Kami-sama's whim.
