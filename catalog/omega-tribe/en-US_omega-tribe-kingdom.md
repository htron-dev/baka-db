## Sinopse

The super humans start their move to create a coup d'etat in Japan. The collective goup of these super humans has a group name "Omega Tribe". The plan for the coup d'etat brings multiple problems at different levels. What will happen to the politics of Japan and the coutry itself? What are the goals of the Omega Tribe? Who's the enemy? Confusion continues to arise as this story goes on...
