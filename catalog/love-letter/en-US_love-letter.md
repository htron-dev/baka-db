## Sinopse

After just moving to a new town, Akira meets a girl he just can't forget.

Three years later he sees her again just outside the train. With his friend's help, he learns the girl's name and decides to write her a love letter. Planning to drop of the letter at her house, Akira goes to the girl's neighborhood and finds her standing alone. Just as he tries to hand her the letter directly he sees her leave with another man. Just who is that man? Will Akira get a chance to tell the girl's his feelings? Will they ever have a relationship?

Besides the story from the book cover, there are 4 more short stories inside:

Summer Night
Yume Iro orgel
Aru Natsu no Hi no Dekigoto
