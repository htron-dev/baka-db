## Sinopse

Nitoro Hanako is a camerawoman who still has not got any decent work. With the help of her mentor, she meets Super Sonico, a new girl who wants to become a gravure idol. After being pressured by her mentor, Hanako decides to take the position of filming Sonico's new career. With no real experience, can she bring Sonico to the top of the industry?
