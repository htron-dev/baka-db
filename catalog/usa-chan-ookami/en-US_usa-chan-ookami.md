## Sinopse

Wolf-kun and Usagi-chan are a couple who belong to very different races. A problem arose within Wolf-kun & Usagi-chan's relationship... Wolf-kun couldn't make Usagi-chan cum. The only answer that Wolf-kun could think of was... "What if you embraced me instead?"

(Source: Fusion Product, translated)
