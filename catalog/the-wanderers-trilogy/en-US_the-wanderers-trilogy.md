## Sinopse

A collection of three one-shots about martial artists.

1. The Birth of a Brand New Technique - An extraordinary martial artist helps a young woman in the mountains.
2. The 1% of a Man - An ordinary guy fights a giant protect his precious gravure idol magazine.
3. The Fortress of Absolute Despair - An outlaw defies heaven by using force to take the emperors throne.
