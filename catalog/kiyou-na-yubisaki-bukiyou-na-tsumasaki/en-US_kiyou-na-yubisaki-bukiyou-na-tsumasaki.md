## Sinopse

Ichigaya and Tomoki are high school students, they don't really know each other. One day Tomoki falls asleep during class and wakes up to find that the "cool" Ichigaya has painted his nails with classmate's polish. The only way to get it off it to go to the home of Ichigaya. There Tomoki steps into the lonely life of Ichigaya.
