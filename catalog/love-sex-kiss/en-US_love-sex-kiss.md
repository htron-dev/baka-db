## Sinopse

A reprint collection of the influential cult mangaka's previously published magazine works from a period between 1995 and 2002.

1. Dynamite Glamour (1995) ~ an edgy, brash comedy about two high school boys and their lover's squabble (sequel of 'Water Breath')

2. My Sweet Lover Boy (1995) ~ a little oneshot featuring the couple from DG

3. LOVE SEX, KISS (1997) ~ an angsty tale of two high school boys face a major turning point of their friendship and relationship

4. Smoking Call (1998) ~ a little oneshot featuring the couple from LSK.

5. Red-Pizza Blues (1998) ~ revolves around members of a rock music band

6. Pension (1999) ~ revolves around members of a rock music band

7. Sugar Fixx (vol. 1, 2002) ~ too much sugar spells an overdose and sex

8. Author's Notes

9. Afterword
