## Sinopse

An animated segment from the Kodomo wa Wakatte Agenai live action film's in-story anime "Mahou Sakan Shoujo Baffalo Koteko". The video also features an ending credits sequence and song that will do appear in the film. Miyu Tomita performs the song "Sakan no Kokoro de" (With a Plasterer's Heart).

(Source: ANN)
