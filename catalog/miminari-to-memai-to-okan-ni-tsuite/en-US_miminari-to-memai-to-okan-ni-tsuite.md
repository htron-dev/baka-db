## Sinopse

Mana, the shopkeeper of a secondhand bookshop, has the special ability of knowing where the lost stuff is once touching the owner's hand. Akabane, a debt collector, began to get interested in this special ability of Mana. Both will be busily engaged in trouble... and love.
