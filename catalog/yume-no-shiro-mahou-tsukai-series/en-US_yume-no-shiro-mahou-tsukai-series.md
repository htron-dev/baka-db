## Sinopse

Yume no Shiro is a one volume work by Masami Tsuda and consists of 3 short stories. Masami Tsuda is famous for Kareshi Kanojo no Jijou. These stories were drawn while KareKano was still running. It's all about love couples. Also, there is a thread running through all three stories. That's a magician who helps the people. Who are the people he helps?

1. Mori no Naka (Inside the Forest)
2. Watashi wa Ningyo (I'm a Mermaid)
3. Yume no Shiro (The Castle of Dreams)
