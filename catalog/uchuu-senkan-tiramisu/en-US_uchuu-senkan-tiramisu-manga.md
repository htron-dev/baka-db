## Sinopse

The human race extended its life field and greed to the vast reaches of space... In Space Era 0156, a war breaks out amidst all of the colonies in space. Earth begins to construct a new space battleship called Tiramisù in secret. Just as the war begins, one genius pilot steers the Tiramisù as a beacon of hope for humanity.
