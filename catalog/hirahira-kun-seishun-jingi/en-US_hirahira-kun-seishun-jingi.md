## Sinopse

Hirahira-kun is a boy that likes helping the most weak, and for that he uses his creativity to think up plans to defeat his corrupted upperclassmen and so to maintain the entirety of his colleagues of class.

Included one-shots:
Volume 1: Isourou wa Tsurai yo
