## Sinopse

Four oneshots that serve as a prequel to the series Usotsuki Lily, including:

1. Usotsuki Lily
   The original one-shot that the series is based upon.

2. CLO CLO
   Stands for Clone Clone and is a story about two sets of twins dating.

3. Summer Storm
   A story into the backgrounds of Komachi and Kojirou.

4. Honest
   How En and Naota became friends, told from Naota's point of view.
