## Sinopse

After two years spent abroad and abstinence of thievery, Gou and Taki return to Japan and are instantly given a new task from the police: they must find a stimulant drug worth 500 million yen. Depending on the success of this mission, they will be able to return to their old life. Will the combinated skills of Taki and Gou be enough to solve this difficult case?
