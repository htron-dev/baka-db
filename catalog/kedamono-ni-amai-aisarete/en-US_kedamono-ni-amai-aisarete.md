## Sinopse

Beautiful OL (office lady) Kanako's newest hobby is to tease the newest employee about sex. But this year she can't rely on making the cool Ogura-kun her trophy--he's the one in control! However, his bold and unexpected technique surprises Kanako!! He plans on toying with her, and these intentions bring her great happiness...

Contains the stories: Kedamono ni Amai Aisarete, Kedamono wa Amaku Sasou, Kedamono wa Amayaka ni Shitto suru, Koibito wa Hakui no King, and Yasashiku Ijimete (Source: M-U)
