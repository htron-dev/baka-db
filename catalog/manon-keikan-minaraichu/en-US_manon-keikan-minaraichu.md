## Sinopse

Paris at the end of the 19th century. Because of some incidents happened in her childhood, the school girl Manon decided to become a police officer. While involved in a mysterious case, she meets Inspector Claudel, "The Orfèvre's Dog" of the Paris' Police Special Investigation team. What will happen if Manon continues to involve herself with the case more and more...! ?

A refreshing romantic and mysterious story is coming up!
