## Sinopse

The Chidori Art School, where boys studying drama gathered in 1935. Keitaro Chidori, a boy who makes the hardest efforts than anyone, toward the dream of wanting to stand in the middle of the stage and to become a star, and a freshman who holds a secret, Mugizushi, who appeared before him. The story of fighting over "talent" now rings the opening bell.

(Source: Kodansha, translated)
