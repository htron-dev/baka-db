## Sinopse

Murial always fall asleep out of the blue regularly. One time, she dreamed about a boy and a talking rabbit in a mystical land, Toys Hill. Since then, whenever she falls asleep, she keeps dreaming about him and his friends in the place. Who is this boy? Why does he keeps appearing in her dream and what is Toys Hill?
