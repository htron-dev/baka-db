## Sinopse

Specials of Shima Kousaku no Asia Risshi-den not considered a part of the normal episode count. The first two are a part of phase 1 and sandwich the arc. The 3rd episode is a New Years special in the middle of phase 2. And the 4th one is a recap of the ASEAN market after Phase 2 finished.
