## Sinopse

A collection of three stories:

1. Hiroki and Yuu are two friends who begin to develop feelings for each other.
2. Step-brothers hide their love for each other by sleeping with others (Jun and Seiji)
3. Keito has been in love with his childhood friend Aki for a long time, even though Aki has been bullying him since they were little.
