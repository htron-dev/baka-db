## Sinopse

Shougyokuden tells the story and the intrigue of the Inner Palace of the Daishin Kingdom. Shougyoku Kan is the former Daishin Kingdom Military General, and is asked to become the Empress by Bunrin, the Emperor of Daishin Kingdom who was formerly her subordinate.
