## Sinopse

Joutarou's father is a photographer who travels abroad a lot, which is why Joutarou has to live alone and take care of the 2 friends of his father who live with him. One day, his father returns with a nine-year-old girl who was abandoned by her parents, causing Jou to remove memories within him.
