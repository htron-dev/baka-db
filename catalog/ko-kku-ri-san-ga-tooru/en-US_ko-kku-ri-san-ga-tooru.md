## Sinopse

The KoKkuRi who draw the blood of the "Clan of Deception" consist of three women—Ko (fox), Ku (Dog), and Ri (Badger)—with Rikako Oomori at the center. They go to the windy town of Shinjuku for a showdown against a motley crowd of apparitions that eat nests.
