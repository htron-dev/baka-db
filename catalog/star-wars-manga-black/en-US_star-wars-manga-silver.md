## Sinopse

Contains original stories set in the Star Wars universe featuring characters from the six films. This work shows you many different sides of the familiar franchise, featuring everything from comedy to tragedy to despair to redemption. (Source: Waterstones)

Star Wars Silver contains the following stories;
_ Perfect Evil Part 2 by Hiromoto-SIN-ichi
_ Oh!! Jawajawa by Hitoshi Ariga
_ Star Wars -Phantasmagoria- by Baron Ueda
_ Vader vs. R2 & 3PO by Makomo Nakatsuka \* Dark Jedi by Yasushi Nirasawa

Also contains two fold-out illustrations by Yuji Kaida and Kia Asamiya.
