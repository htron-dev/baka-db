## Sinopse

Contains original stories set in the Star Wars universe featuring characters from the six films. This work shows you many different sides of the familiar franchise, featuring everything from comedy to tragedy to despair to redemption. (Source: Waterstones)

The following stories are in Star Wars Black;
_ Perfect Evil Part 1 by Hiromoto-SIN-Ichi
_ Tantive IV by Formalin
_ Evil Eyes by Nao
_ Dear Anakin by Furi Furi Company

Also contains three illustrations by Kia Asamiya, Yuji Kaida, and Tsutomu Takahashi.
