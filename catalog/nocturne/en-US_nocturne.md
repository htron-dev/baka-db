## Sinopse

Yuri is a doll-like child, orphaned at a young age when her mother dies in a car accident. In her will, she requests that Yuri be placed under the guardianship of a close friend of her, Kim Dowook. Taken in, Yuri begins a new life, different from her life before. Where will this take her, and what does she think of her guardian...?
