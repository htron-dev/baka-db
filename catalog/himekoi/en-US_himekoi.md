## Sinopse

Yoshikuni and Okada were classmates, but Okada changed schools and they parted. Years later they meet again at school, but Okada does not recognize Yoshikuni, but realizes that he treats him differently. Since then, Okada begins to realize his true feelings for Yoshikuni. Secret love and delicate as cherry blossoms.
