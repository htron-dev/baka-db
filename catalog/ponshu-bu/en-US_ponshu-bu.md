## Sinopse

Sake-lover Motoki Akiyama (better known as Pon-chan) is taking applications for like-minded individuals to enjoy ponshu (aka nihonshu, or sake) with her! It’s another delicious day spend alongside her younger brother Matsuya (in charge of food), long-time friend and liquor store owner Dai-chan (in charge of sake), and Chief Pon (in charge of drinking)!

(Source: Tokyo Otaku Mode)
