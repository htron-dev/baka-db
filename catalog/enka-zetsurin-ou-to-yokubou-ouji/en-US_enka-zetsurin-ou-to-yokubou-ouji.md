## Sinopse

As the Emperor's favorite eunuch, Yan is called to attend the harem every night to receive the emperor's attention. However, Yan is actually the prince of a country which the emperor had eradicated. Now that Yan has gained entry into the imperial palace, he intends to exact his revenge the person responsible for his fathers death.
