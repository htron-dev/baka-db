## Sinopse

My prince, do my feelings reach you-?

This is a world where magic exists. People with the ability to use magic are gathered together at a magic academy city where there are 12 special boys called "flower knights." All of the girls admire them and have feelings for them.

This is a mysterious story of 12 months of language of flowers. Now love is beginning.
