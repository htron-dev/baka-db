## Sinopse

The beautiful, strong Agatsuma Tamaki has a weakness~ his kohai* (*junior in age or rank), Sawamura Kei, who simply just drives Tamaki up the wall by his mere existence. On one fateful night, Tamaki’s guilty secret was discovered by no other than…! Will romance blossom between this wild but emotionally inept couple?♥ This book also collects a 70-page extra story.
