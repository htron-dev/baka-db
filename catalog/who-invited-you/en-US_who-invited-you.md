## Sinopse

Hands already full with work, Jade suddenly finds an old friend from China ready to squat at her place. But Willow isn't the sweet boy she once knew - she can't stand him now, let alone live together. Can Jade adjust to this unwelcome roommate as her childhood crush, or will she simply kick him to the curb?
