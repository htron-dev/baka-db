## Sinopse

A pure-hearted kuro-gal and a simple diamond-in-the-rough... With a love as odd as theirs, who's to say where it will lead? Dreaming of finally becoming a Tokyo girl, Tamao moves to the capital and falls in love with Yamada, a guy working at a supermarket. At first glance he may seem unappealing, but a love as pure as theirs is second to none!

(Source: Houbunsha, translated)
