## Sinopse

The storyline continues several months after Weiß Kreuz Glühen. It follows Aya and Ken as members of an assassination team named Krypton Brand Side B in Great Britain. Weiß is no more. Of the original Weiß, only Ken and Aya have found their way into Side B.
