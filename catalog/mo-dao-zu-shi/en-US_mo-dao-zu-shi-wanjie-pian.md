## Sinopse

The third season of Mo Dao Zu Shi that covers the Yi city arc. With the appearances of new characters; A-Qing, Xiao Xingchen, and Xue Yang. The story will follow the two main charters Wei Wuxian and Lan Wangji as they try to solve a mystery involving the haunted arm of an un-none corps.
