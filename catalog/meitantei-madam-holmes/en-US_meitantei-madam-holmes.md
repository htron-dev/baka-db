## Sinopse

Detective Wato has been assigned to the security team for a cruise ship party for celebrities where he meets Madam Holmes, a beautiful and intelligent young lady. But soon after the ship leaves the harbor, a group of terrorists take over the ship. However, Detective Wato soon learns that the entire terrorist attack was a setup masterminded by Madam Holmes to smoke out the real terrorist hiding amongst the guests. And to surprise him even more, he learns that Madam Holmes is actually the great granddaughter of the famous Sherlock Holmes. Now the unlikely pair solves crimes and mysteries using Holmes’ amazing deductive skills and Wato’s experience as a cop.
