## Sinopse

Spin-off of Kakumeiki Valvrave.

The protagonist is Hinomoto Jin, jealous towards his classmate Haruto for his ability to control the Valvrave. He and Ota Nao, a beautiful and popular second year student who lacks confidence in herself, become the pilots of a Kagerou, a unit based on Valvrave II, under L-Elf's commands.
