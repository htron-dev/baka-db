## Sinopse

Classmates from middle school, Oogoshi and Saotome reunite at their job. Oogashi is as popular and talented as he was in middle school. For long, Saotome has held on tight to his admiration but now that they're both adults he can't really tell him that easily. What will Oogashi say when he it seems like he's seen right through his feelings!? A youthful love story of two awkward salary men!
