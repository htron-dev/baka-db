## Sinopse

In Korea, the deer is considered as the symbol of purity and innocence. Ria wishes she were a deer... Torn between her love for her protective brother and her desire to fend for herself, she'll need to learn how to make her own choices, as well as her brother will. Not only does Rin meets a girl who falls head over heels for him, but he also starts to feel growing an odd interest in guys...(Source: Shoujo Manga Maniac)
