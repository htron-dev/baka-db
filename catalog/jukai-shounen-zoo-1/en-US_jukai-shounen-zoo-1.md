## Sinopse

The (rather surreal) adventures of a high-school kid who’s a total loser and throws up a lot. That’s about it. 25% of the manga is Juichi (the title is a pun on his name) throwing up or sneezing in full page spreads. It’s total madness.

(Source: MU)
