## Sinopse

From Divine Sanctuary:

The story begins with the death of a young girl. To find the reason why she needed to die, 3 good looking girls and a detective try to find a killer who seems to know something about it. But did she really die? And since one of the girls looks exactly like the dead girl...will they succeed to find that man...? And can the detective protect the double?
