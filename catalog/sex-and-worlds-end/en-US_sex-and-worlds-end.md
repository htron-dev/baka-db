## Sinopse

Izumi Hasegawa's bike cuts through the silence of the night. A man in a car is chasing him—Masao, a loan shark. When Izumi agreed to be his friend's guarantor, he didn't think about it too much. He didn't expect to get into trouble. So when Masao catches up to him at the entrance of a sleazy motel, Izumi still thinks the debt isn't his problem. Masao seems to enjoy demanding Izumi have sex with him...
