## Sinopse

St. Dorothea Academy is a prestigious girls school with rich history. It values friendship of its students and there's a tradition to appoint 2 students who showed the most beautiful friendship to be "La Raine" every year. It's said that La Raine, with their eternal friendship guiding the other students, is the one that puts the academy to shine...
