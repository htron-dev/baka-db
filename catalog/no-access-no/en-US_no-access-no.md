## Sinopse

A hot and handsome young man that can capture the heart of any woman caught the innocent love of a fellow worker. On the other hand, a mysterious woman that claims she would never fall for a man comforts her best friend. What will happen when these two lives clash into one another at a fateful meeting? Will this mysterious woman change her ways?
