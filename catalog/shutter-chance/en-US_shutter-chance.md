Ryuusei is a photographer specialized in erotic themes who has lost his inspiration. Until, one day, he comes across Yoshiuki, a middle aged insurance agent, and strikes a weird deal with him. With such an unsual subject, will Ryuusei be able to feel again the thrill of capturing the moment through his lens? [Baka-Updates]
