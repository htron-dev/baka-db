## Sinopse

Dust Box 2.5 is a four-panel comedy manga centering around a man who just got accepted into a voice acting agency. The main character is a shut-in, and hates to cause trouble for anyone, but after getting his audition accepted, he enters an environment where he encounters many voice actors with their own distinct personalities.

(Source: ANN)
