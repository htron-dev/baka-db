## Sinopse

Beauty school student and amateur model Nanami Yuuta is a huge fan of the mysterious top male model, COU. When Nanami, who thinks nothing of other girls while in his daze, was going to a photo shoot one day, he was shocked to see COU there. Moreover, he was selected as his shoot partner for the risqué theme of "I want to sleep with COU," and the two wind up entangled in bed...?! "Do you want to keep a secret with me?" A pure love story of two beautiful people, unfolded in the brilliant world of modeling.
