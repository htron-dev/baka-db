## Sinopse

Kanna, a young girl from a good family. Matilda, a witch. Marion, an orphan. All of these girls have special powers as shamans are marked and feared as "monsters" and are fighting loneliness in their small worlds. That is until they meet the overwhelmingly powerful shaman named Hao...
