## Sinopse

A dog named Weed happens to hear he is the son of Gin, the great leader of Ohu, Who lead the dogs of ohu to victory in a fight against monstrous bear Akakabuto. Weed wants to find his father no matter of what. On a way to his father Weed meets many friends and enemies.
