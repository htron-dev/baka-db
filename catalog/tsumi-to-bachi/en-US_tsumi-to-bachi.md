## Sinopse

When the Isshiki family runs into monetary trouble, their last option is to turn to the god of fortune that resides in their home. However, instead of helping them, he rather see their entire family destroyed! It's up to Tarou to find out Kami-san's past and why their God of fortune has turned into one of misfortune!
