## Sinopse

He's from an impressive clan. His bloodline is impressive but he's not. Having been thrown out of his home, Ryosonuke lives as a sort of tradesman. After receiving a vampire doll from his sempai his split blood wakes it up and with the death of his sempai he also acquires a meat eating girl. The two girls help Ryosonuke to control the monster that lurks in his blood as different individuals come after him for various reasons.

Since December 24, 2004 this series is on hiatus.
