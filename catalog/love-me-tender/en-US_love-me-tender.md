## Sinopse

Sae and Kazuki, friends and models, end up living in the apartment of the beautiful Naoyuki.

But despite the appearances, Naoyuki is not a girl but a guy, and even Kazuki's childhood friend! This weird trio will meet many characters, all more interesting and complicated than the others.
