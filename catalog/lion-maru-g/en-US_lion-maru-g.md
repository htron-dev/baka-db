## Sinopse

Based on a popular tokusatsu series. The pervert and coward Ushio Shishimaru received a mysterious sword from an old man he met in his journey. The power of the sword can transform him into the powerful Lion Maru. Enjoy his adventure along with beautiful Saori and her boyish sister Kaori (aka Kosu-K) in the new kabuki district. (Source: Fateful Encounters)
