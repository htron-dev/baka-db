## Sinopse

Marei Misaki is a second year in high school, and she sucks at studying. She has no real goals in life, and has zero interest in taking the college entrance exam... until her childhood best friend, Micchan, says she's going to college. Now Marei has found her goal: pass the entrance exam for Akamon University, an elite college, and go to school with Micchan.
