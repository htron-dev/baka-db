## Sinopse

Inoue is in school manga club which don't do club activities at all. One day he gets some scratch and school infirmary, where he meets Aoki. He talks with her about the club, and suggested to make club magazine. Inoue decides to do so. Later, Aoki joins the club, plotting to break it.
