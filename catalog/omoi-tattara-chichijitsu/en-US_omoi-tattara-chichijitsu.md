## Sinopse

Shigeru Yakumo, talent scout and model manager of cactus productions works with lovely, big-breasted ladies on a daily basis. As if that wasn't lucky enough, he comes home to his loving, sex-crazed wife, Nanaho, who used to be the top model of cactus productions! Working with beauties starts to take a toll on Shigeru when temptation strikes and the models come after him! Can shigeru resist these alluring vixens and stay true to his wife? Or will he fall into breast temptation?
