## Sinopse

Asami Ike made a Christmas Advent animation, each day was a new short animation counting down to Christmas. She posted each ep on her official Twitter page. 17 total pieces were made but advent day 12-15 used entirely live-action footage and as such are not included in the episode count. A compilation of the whole advent was posted to her official YouTube channel.
