## Sinopse

After accidentally kissing a male classmate in school, Ryou suffers as the students whisper and spread rumors about him. Feeling ostracized Ryou keeps to himself - only to wind up in the clutches of the schools counselor. Will this relationship blossom? Can it last? And what would happen if someone found out?!
