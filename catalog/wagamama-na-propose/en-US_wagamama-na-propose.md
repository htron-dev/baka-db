## Sinopse

Paige is recovering from heartbreak after being abandoned by her fiancé. She'd moved to Chicago to get a fresh start. At the library where she works, she's recently been visited by a man named Shane Huntington. He's a skilled detective and handsome playboy, too much like the fiancé who betrayed her. She knows she should have nothing to do with him. And yet, even as she tries to remain distant and cold, Shane comes to her with an offer she can't refuse...
