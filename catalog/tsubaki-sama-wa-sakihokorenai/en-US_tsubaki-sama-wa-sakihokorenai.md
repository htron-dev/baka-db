## Sinopse

"Be pure! Be just! Be beautiful!" That is Tsubaki's motto! She's cute and tries very hard, but sometimes she's just wide open. Aiming to be a graceful woman in the future, her ideas clash with reality!

Let's explore Tsubaki's daily life in this comedy!
