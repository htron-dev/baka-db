## Sinopse

A merman prince saves a boy from drowning in the ocean and falls in love with him. Because the prince cannot forget about the boy, he begs his father for a pair of legs, but he has to trade it with a prize. That prize is if he and the boy do not share mutual love within a month, there will be a difficult challenge waiting for the prince.
