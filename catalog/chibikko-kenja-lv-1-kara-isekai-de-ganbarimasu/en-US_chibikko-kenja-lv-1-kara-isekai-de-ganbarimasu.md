## Sinopse

Yuri, an avid player of the MMO game Elyushia Online, successfully switches to her desired sage job. However, apart from starting at level 1, she suddenly gets transported to the real Elyushia! She must now brave a lifelike land of a video game in hopes that she can return to her own world.
