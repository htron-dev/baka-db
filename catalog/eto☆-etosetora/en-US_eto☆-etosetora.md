## Sinopse

In main story a girl named Mifuyu wishes from zodiac that they would help her to tell her crush how she feels. Out of blue all animal gods appear to her room and start to make her dream come true. In other stories glasses that show peoples true form are found, a ram works as students councils chair and two frinds come over their bad luck.
