From Vampire Knights:
Touko is a 15 year-old aspiring actress who can be a bit of a liar. But thanks to one lie, she lands herself in the middle of an audition for a TV drama! A producer she meets there, Kyouichi, sees potential talent in her as an actress! She's found what she wants to do in life, but what kind of future awaits her?!
