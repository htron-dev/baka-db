## Sinopse

The main character, Tooru Kokonose, is a school girl who cannot find her real self at home or at school. It is only when she is touched by a man that she can feel "alive." A dangerous relationship with a strange man who is lost in the night city begins but how will it end?

(Source: Nihon Bungeisha, translated)
