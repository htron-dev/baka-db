## Sinopse

A collection of short stories by Toda Seiji published in magazines like Nextcomic Faust and Hi Mystery from 2004 to 2009. All of the stories deal with the struggles of women coming to terms with their families, their careers, their love lives, and themselves. The stories are:

(1) Life
(2) 1900
(3) Neko
(4) Meme
(5) Mishiranu onna
(6) Katazukero
(7) Sappho no matsuei
(8) Kaeru
