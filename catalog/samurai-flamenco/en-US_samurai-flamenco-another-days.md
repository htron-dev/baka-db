## Sinopse

Samurai Flamenco is a comedy and drama about Masayoshi Hazama, a man who becomes a superhero by himself without superpowers or technology, and Hidenori Gotou, a cop who finds out Hazama's true identity and thus constantly gets in trouble thanks to the superhero.

(Source: ANN)
