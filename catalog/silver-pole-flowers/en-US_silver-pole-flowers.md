## Sinopse

Hana's mother was a pole dancer. Hiding her face, she now carries on her mother's love for dance, even if she has to hide her identity, pole dancing being subject to prejudice due to its connection to the indecent. When Karin, a pole sports athlete sees her dance for the first time, she begins to grasp what her dance lacks in comparison. A heated rivalry between girls dancing on different paths and poles is born.
