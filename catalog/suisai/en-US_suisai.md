## Sinopse

"I want to play the melody that'll make my heart dance..."

Urara Sano was the ace of the track and field club in junior high. Now she's a first-year at Hoshigaoka High School and wants to try new things. After meeting with Minato, a first-year saxophonist, she decides to join the wind band club to play the flute... But everything isn't going to be easy, especially for a beginner like Urara!
