## Sinopse

The suspicious white rabbit Peter White kidnapped Alice and took her to the Country of Hearts. And now for the second time, she encounters a change in the landscape called the "Move" -- which sends her from Country of Clovers to Country of Diamonds. For some reason the role-holders don't remember Alice! She stays in the Castle of Diamond and meets the Black Rabbit prime minister Sidney Black. Will she be captivated by his wicked tongue...!?
