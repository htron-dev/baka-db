## Sinopse

During the change from the Meiji Era into the Shouwa Era, there was a strange medicine seller. This man called Kouen, is a lazy and messy person but as a medicine seller, he is of top class. Together with his assistant, Tsuruhiko, they help out the common folk but they somehow got entangled in a mysterious incident.

(Source: Kodansha, translated)
