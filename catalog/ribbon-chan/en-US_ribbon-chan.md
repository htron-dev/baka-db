## Sinopse

An anime short series inspired by soft drink company Sapporo Beverage's mascot character Ribbon-chan. The story center on the soda-loving young girl named Ribbon-chan and her pet dog Citron.

Note: The series aired on Wednesdays during episodes of the Nori Suta Max television program.

(Source: ANN)
