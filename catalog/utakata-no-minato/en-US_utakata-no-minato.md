## Sinopse

Minato Wadatsumi is a boy who was born and raised in a port town, and has an extreme phobia of fish. One day while walking to school, he reluctantly helps a fish back into the water; not knowing how it will change his destiny.

A romantic comedy set 3000 meters under the sea!
