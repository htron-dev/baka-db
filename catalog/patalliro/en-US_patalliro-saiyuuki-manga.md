## Sinopse

Spin off of Patalliro! and a parody of Saiyuuki (Journey to the West):

A really weird monkey called Son Goku & his master, Genjou Sanzou-Houshi, look for Buddha's Scriptures. Along the way, they encounter various people and have interesting adventures.
