## Sinopse

Momoka finds wonder in the trivia of everyday life, often spending her days in bookstores advancing her knowledge. Despite being selective towards who she shares this side of herself with, Hashima—the tardy boy who sits next to her in class—tempts her to open herself up. Momoka's curiosity sparks an exchange between the two that broadens her world in a way she would never expect...
