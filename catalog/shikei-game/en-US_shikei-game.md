## Sinopse

Seven former fellow middle school students are forced to participate in a "death game" by an eerie rabbit. They have never met since graduating, but now they must complete 6 death games within one hour to escape their entrapment! A game of despair begins as their dark past is revealed and their fragile relationships are put to the test!
