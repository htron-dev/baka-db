## Sinopse

I, Takahiro, am a normal high school student, until my life took an interesting spin when my dad had a shotgun marriage with a rich Hollywood actress! To capitalize on my new status, I now live alone in a high-class hotel… and indulge in a carnival of pleasures… until 2 bodyguards showed up, hired to protect me from my supposed kidnappers!
