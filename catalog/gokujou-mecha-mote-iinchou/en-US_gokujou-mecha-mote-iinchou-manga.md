## Sinopse

Read about an outstanding freshman who is kind and well-known chairman. Everything goes easy for her except one for one problem and that's the class troubled boy trio who can't seem to follow the rules. How will she handle this rebellious group who can't seem to stay out of trouble. Watch as things slowly change from her trying to keep them in line to her seeing there is good in each one of them.
