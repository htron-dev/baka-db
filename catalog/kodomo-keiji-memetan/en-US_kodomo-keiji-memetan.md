## Sinopse

Memetan, a detective who sports a yellow romper better than nobody else. This comic will leave you laughing in disbelief as it follows the bizarre daily life of the ever capable detective, Memetan, who despite being a baby, is unpredictable and is always chasing down criminals. Anybody who's anybody in Tottori-ken knows Memetan!
