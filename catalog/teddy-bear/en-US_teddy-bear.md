## Sinopse

Tragic love story revolving round a girl who got to know a really cute guy and they started to contact each other through phone mail. She eventually agreed to date him.

However, he started to restrict her dressing and movement. She had no choice but to break up with him one day. He agreed and walked away from the relationship that day with a smile on his face but there is a reason behind it....
