## Sinopse

To pay the bills, Takumi works for a company specialising in breaking up relationships. Today, his newest target is a quiet medical student who is supposedly having an affair with a married professor. Can Takumi keep his spotless work record he's so proud of, when his feelings unexpectedly become involved?
