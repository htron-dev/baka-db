## Sinopse

They say that every great man in history had some kind of fetish. Some establishment seriously thought that; "If we gather every person with an extreme fetish, we can breed geniuses, right?" Itou Manami is a new teacher assigned to be the homeroom teacher of such class. How will he handle such problematic students?
