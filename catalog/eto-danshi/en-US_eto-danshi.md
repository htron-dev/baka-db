## Sinopse

A collection of short stories themed around the Chinese Zodiac.

1. Eto-sou 2012 (Zodiac Signs Manor) [Honma Akira]
2. Shuu-chan no Nengajou (Shuu-chan's New Years Greeting Card) [Takao Hiroi]
3. Ichizu na Puppy (Earnest Puppy) [Umiyuki Lily]
4. Hitotose Futari (A Couple Once Upon a Year) [Suzukura Hal]
5. Okaeri, Itterasshai (Welcome Home, Have a Safe Trip) [Hanamori Mito]
6. Sarainen Date shimashou ne? (Let's Have a Date the Year After Next) [Aikawa Fuu]
