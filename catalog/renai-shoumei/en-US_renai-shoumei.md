## Sinopse

Collection of short stories:

1. Renai Shoumei
   Japanese translator, Atsushi, is able to convey other people's words, but he can never seem to express himself. Now American businessman Chris wants more of a relationship, but can Atsushi say the right things to save this relationship? Atsushi has already gone through one heart breaking relationship...and why does Chris seem to know the previous lover?

2. Kiss and Cry
   Otsuka is the manager for handsome young actor Kanade, and also his secret lover.

3. Gokujou no Kiss

4. Escape (FUWA Shinri)
   Osanai's new job at all boy's school means "look but don't touch" for the newly gay single teacher. Unfortunately student council head Matsuyama Yutaka has no intention of leaving his cute sensei alone, no matter how much Osanai tries to resist...
