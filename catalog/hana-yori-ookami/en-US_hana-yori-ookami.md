## Sinopse

Riku Kirishima, a rough-looking high school student, is a lover of romantic comic books who takes care of his family in place of his late mother. He has a younger brother, who he cares for deeply. One evening, he is suddenly embraced by a man in the park who offers him $300 to spend the night. Riku runs away, but soon learns that the handsome pervert who approached him is actually the guardian of his brother's friend! Enjoy this love story between a handsome florist and a romantic high school tough guy!
