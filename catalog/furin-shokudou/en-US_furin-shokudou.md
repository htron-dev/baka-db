## Sinopse

The manga centers on 35-year-old businessman Ryuuichi Yamadera. Ryuuichi travels around the country on business trips, and his sole entertainment is trying different foods at local restaurants. He also ends up sleeping with the various married women he meets at the restaurants, while also being married himself.

(Source: ANN)
