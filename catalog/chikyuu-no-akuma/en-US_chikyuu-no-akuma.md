## Sinopse

Chikyuu no Akuma features two stories, the title story is about a man building an underground shelter in a small town whose brain is being controlled pert-time by aliens intent on invading the Earth. The other story, written in 1956, is about Japan after the polar ice caps melt and how a few people are trying to rescue animals.
