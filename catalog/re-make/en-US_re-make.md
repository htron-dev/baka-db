## Sinopse

Kanoko Okumura is 25 years old entering into her third consecutive year boyfriend-less and working as a temporary worker for a company. One after another her friends are getting married and after seeing her coworkers enjoying be fashionable, she can't help but want to change herself. And so she decides to quit her job and interview to become a beauty assistant even though she has no experience in the field at all...
