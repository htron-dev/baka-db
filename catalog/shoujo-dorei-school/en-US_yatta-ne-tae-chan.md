## Sinopse

After being abandoned by her mother, Tae-chan is left alone with Koro-chan, a teddy bear that she treats as if it was a family member as it's the last thing still connecting her to her mom.

Tae-chan is usually quiet but she won't have any pity for those who dare hurt Koro-chan...
