## Sinopse

During a particularly hot day, Seth casually helps Minato, who got into trouble at the park. Seth, a local foreigner, is famous for walking around with a woman's parasol, and he is dubbed as The Parasol King. From then on, Seth calls Minato "Parasol," and the two gradually drift closer together...

(Source: Gentosha Comics, translated)
