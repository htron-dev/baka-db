## Sinopse

Makoto, a construction worker, is dragged along to a gay bar after work, where he meets Shinobu. Shinobu, an effeminate young man whose interests lie in foreigners, is immediately drawn to Makoto's looks, and they begin a relationship. Until one day, Makoto comes to the bar dripping with blood...
