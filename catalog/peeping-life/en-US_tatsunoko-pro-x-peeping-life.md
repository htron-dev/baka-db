## Sinopse

Collaboration between Peeping Life, Tatsunoko Productions, Circle K Sunkus, and TV Asahi. The first episode Boyakki helps Yatterman No. 2 fix Omocchama who broken mid-battle. The second episode has Otaku-kun talk about Tatsunoko's 50th Anniversary. The third episode has Speed Racer block the front entrance of a 7/11 with his car and has a traffic cop come up. And the fourth episode has Gold Lightan bother Hiroshi working on a PowerPoint presentation due tomorrow.
