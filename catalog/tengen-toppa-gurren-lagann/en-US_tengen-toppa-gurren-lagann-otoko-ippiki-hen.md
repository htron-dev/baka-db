## Sinopse

Otoko series is a "drama CD" based on Tengen Toppa Guren Lagann, an immensely popular anime series. Now this CD is being turned into a comic! The setting has been changed to modern Japan, in which familiar characters such as Kamina, Simon, Yoko, and Nia attend Guren Academy! Don't miss the spirit of these men, which burns hot and red just like in the manga series!
