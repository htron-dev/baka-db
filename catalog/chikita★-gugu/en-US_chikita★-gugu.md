## Sinopse

After his whole family was eaten as a child, Chikita Gugu is the sole survivor of the legendary Gugu clan. Now he's a teenager and people are coming to him for help, even though he has no training or ability! Luckily he's not alone. Laa Lam Delal, the youkai who killed his family, is watching over him, waiting for him to ripen...
