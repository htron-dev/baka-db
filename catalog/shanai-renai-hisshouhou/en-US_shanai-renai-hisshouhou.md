## Sinopse

Hiromi, an office worker, comes to know a secret of manager Hojo, who is cool and dandy. Sakura, an energetic career woman has defeated Ozaki, who entered the company in the same year. Kaede is worried about her future, because she becomes a subordinate of Mogami with the credit of top sales. This series is to support those women who are working hard for both love and career. Besides three features of this series, another story is included in the collection of short stories.
