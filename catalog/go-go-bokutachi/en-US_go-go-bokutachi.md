## Sinopse

Atsu does not join any clubs in his school because he wants to find a part-time job. However, a club name "Lounge Lizard Club", which is famous for having pretty boys as members, comes to invite him to join. More astonishingly, the leader of the club expresses his affection towards Atsu openly in the public...
