## Sinopse

The Magic World of Oz is in danger! Marion is an evil witch that's dominating the 109 kingdom. North Ypsilon is a white mage who goes down to Earth looking for the hero that will be able to defeat her. When he arrives to Japan, he stand in a Tokyo highschool where he meet Sorata, who somehow becomes the hero he's looking for...
