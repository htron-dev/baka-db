## Sinopse

Kanou Osamu is a failed writer who has just been evicted from his apartment. As he's trying to figure out what to do, he bumps into his high school friend and roommate Satomi Tatsuo who offers him a place to live on 2 conditions. The first is that he can only stay for one month and the second is that he must listen to everything Satomi tells him.
