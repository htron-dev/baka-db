## Sinopse

The former chef of Hakodate's Goryokakutei, Kitagata Toshizou! The man who has been contracted to completely turn around a restaurant on the verge of failing! When it comes to cooking techniques, ideas, and spirit he is unrivaled! Only those that can make it through Toshizou's earth shattering training will be able to gain footing on the road to revival. Then comes in another shop...!!
