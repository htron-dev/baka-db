## Sinopse

Marionette was a type of doll which moves by pulling its strings. Anthony wanted to build his dream from these dolls. He wanted the dolls to be popular and like by the people whole around the world. But, to make a dream comes true was not easy. Anthony almost lost his hope and made Bell, the woman that he truly loved, disappointed...
