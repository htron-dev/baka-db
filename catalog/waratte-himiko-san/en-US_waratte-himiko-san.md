## Sinopse

Shy Himiko, who has a serious and gloomy exterior, but is a romantic daydreamer on the inside, has a crush on her classmate, Haruka-kun. He is cheerful and popular, but quite clueless about love. Can shy Himiko make him understand her feelings for him?
