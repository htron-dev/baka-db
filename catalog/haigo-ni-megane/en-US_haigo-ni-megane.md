## Sinopse

It suddenly becomes apparent to Hazuki that his friend Misono absolutely dotes on him. Naturally they're classmates and attend school together. But now he's all grown up. Calling his name Misono says "Oops my bad…I came by mistake." Despite that it seems that Misono suffers from ED and responds only to Hazuki. Hazuki is convinced that what he feels for Misono is "true love" and realizes that Misono is always by his side…
