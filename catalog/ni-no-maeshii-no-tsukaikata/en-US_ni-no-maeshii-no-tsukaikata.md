## Sinopse

After being awarded the Nobel Prize, child prodigy Alice T.P. Riida runs away from home and creates the first Artificial Human, Ninomae Shii. Along with Shiratori Jiyun and Kurosaki Yaoki, Shii now struggles to find out for what reason he was made for.
