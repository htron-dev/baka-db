## Sinopse

Educational video that teaches teenagers about value of money, basic banking principles, use of credit cards and other ways of financial interaction in the society.

\*Part of the series of films produced with cooperation of: Japan's Central Council for Financial Services Information and Bank of Japan Public Relations Department.
