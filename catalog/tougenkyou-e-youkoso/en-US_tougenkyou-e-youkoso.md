## Sinopse

"Welcome to the old hot spring inn where witches are waitresses!"

Kisara, a magic academy high school student, who is working as a waitress at "Wind and Cloud" inn as part of her witch training. At first glance, it is usual lodging, but it is actually the strongest magic hot spot in the eastern region and the owner is actually a former big notorious witch, Akeno Nanami. Initially, Kisara's first step in her ambition to rule the world was to beat Akeno...but!?

Kisara, a servant-witch, together with Asagi, the heir daughter of an ancient martial arts dojo, meet variety of unique characters and experience the everyday extraordinary life...!

(Source: Shogakukan)
