## Sinopse

Tsukueda Ryuu is a seeker in Neo Kowloon who always accompanied by a rabbit that he calls "father." Both of them solved case after case, until one day a case lead them to someone connected to a mysterious killer who killed a lot of people including Ryuu's mother.
