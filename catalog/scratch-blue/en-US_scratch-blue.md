## Sinopse

Delinquent Akito and bully-magnet Ryou have been friends for a long time, with Akito protecting Ryou, cooking for him, and pretty much acting like his caretaker. But what binds Akito to Ryou so strongly? Why does he have daily erotic dreams about Ryou chaining him up, blaming him, and forcefully taking him? The answers all lie in a certain even that took place during their childhood...
