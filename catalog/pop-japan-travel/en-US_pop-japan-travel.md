## Sinopse

An otaku's essential travel guide in manga form, Pop Japan Travel follows a group of foreign otaku as they Explore the wild streets of Tokyo, encounter pop idols, evade the yakuza and experience culture shock.

This manga also includes maps, travel tips, and shopping guides--essentially everything the otaku needs to experience Tokyo at its best.
