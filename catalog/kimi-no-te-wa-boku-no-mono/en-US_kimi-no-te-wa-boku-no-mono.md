## Sinopse

Anthology of six one-shots:

1. Kimi no Te wa Boku no Mono (Your Hand Belongs to me) - When his student Kurahashi-kun falls from the sky and lands on Sugisaki-sensei, everyone at school thinks they tried to commit suicide together! Sugiaki is determined to find out why Kurahashi would jump off a roof, but this may be too much for the doctor to handle!

2. Renai Sengen (Love Announcement) - When his son gets married and moves out businessman Sakurai Yuusuke is very lonely. When he finds a starving kid in front of his house, he takes him in. But who is his new house-keeper really?
