## Sinopse

Time is Taisho Era. Suou owns a men's kimono store along with his brother, Chouji, and runs the store with his family. One day he is rescued from a group of bandits by an army officer Godou, nicknamed the "red demon". Even though Suou is quite a player himself, he falls in love at first sight with Godou. Godou, however, have no interest in a relationship with another man.
