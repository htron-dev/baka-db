## Sinopse

A four-panel comedy manga that depicts King aspiring to be a manga creator. In the story, King wants to work as an assistant under professional manga creator Meliodas, and knocks on his door, but the one that answers the door is the editor—the pig Hawk.

(Source: ANN)
