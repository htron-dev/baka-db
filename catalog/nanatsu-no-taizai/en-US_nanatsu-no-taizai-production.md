## Sinopse

The manga imagines what would happen if the setting of Nanatsu no Taizai was instead of a live-action TV drama production. In the manga, Meliodas is an actor who plays the role of a child genius. The manga will show the bad habits of the actors during filming and their everyday lives.

(Source: ANN)
