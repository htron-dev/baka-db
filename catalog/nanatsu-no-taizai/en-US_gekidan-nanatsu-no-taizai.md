## Sinopse

Mini anime of Nanatsu no Taizai parodying six fairy tales: "Little Match Girl," "Urashima Tarou," "Tsuru no Ongaeshi," "Snow White's Scary Adventures," "The Honest Woodcutter," and "Momotarou." They were released daily on the official VAP Youtube channel and on the official Twitter account.
