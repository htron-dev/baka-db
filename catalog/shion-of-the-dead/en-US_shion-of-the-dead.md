## Sinopse

Shion Shouji, 15-years-old (?), is a brilliant and gorgeous high school student!! Tsubasa Raimi, the lucky boy who sits beside her, is torn between whether or not to ask her out. Because, I mean...she's totally a zombie!! Get ready for surreal gags and modern, refreshing, and slightly bittersweet school life, because even zombies need love!
