## Sinopse

Mizutani Masayuki is a calm high school boy. While his parents are on a oversea trip, his classmate Shimizu Sayaka works as a housekeeper in his house. Although Masayuki has a sound mind, Sayaka is under a pervert delusion and tries to twist him round her little finger.
