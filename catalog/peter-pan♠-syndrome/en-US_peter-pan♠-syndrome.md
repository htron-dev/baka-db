## Sinopse

Twelve-year-old junior high school girl Hasumi Kohaku moved into town in search of her mother. She is gifted with ESP and the ability to fly when the sun is down. Kohaku soon used her powers in front of her classmates in order to save her friend Uro from falling out a window.
