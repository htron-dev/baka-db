## Sinopse

Shouta no Sushi is about a young boy who dreams of being a sushi chef just like his father. He overcomes many trials including bitter classmates (one of whom is the heir to a large sushi chain store), aggressive rivals, and the Tokyo tournament to get him into the championships.
