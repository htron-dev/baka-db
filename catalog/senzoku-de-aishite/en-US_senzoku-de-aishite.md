## Sinopse

A collection of one-shots.

1. Exclusive Love Contract -
   Salaryman-horny-uke, Aida Tomoki is surprised when well-endowed sexing-his-way-through-college seme, Mochizuki Shougo walks into his hotel room by mistake. After their first unplanned tryst what exactly does Tomoki-san have in mind for Shougo-kun? This hot and funny one shot has lots of sex and a happy ending.

2. Sono Toki ai Wa Ubawareta.
   After an amazing one-night stand with a guy (his first), young businessman runs away. Six months later he just got a new job, and guess who's the president of the company? He's been waiting to get his hands on the poor uke, and he has no intention of letting him go!

3. Sono Toki ai Wa Ubawareta - Sequel.

4. Ai no muchi, ai no mitsu.

5. Seito Kaishitsu he Youkoso!

6. Koi no Legend.

7. Dr. Yagami's Love Examination (Yagami Doctor no Ai no Shinsatsushitsu)

8. Shitasaki no Amai Aji. Akihi has just been dumped when he runs into the hot new student teacher who gives him a lollipop.

9. Exclusive Love Contract - Sequel.
