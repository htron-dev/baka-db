## Sinopse

Fujishiro Tatsuto is roped into working for the relatively unknown, yet talented photographer Araki Yousuke to repay the cost of a broken camera lens. After school, Tatsuto works as a camera assistant and is captivated by Yousuke’s technique and communication with his models. However, one day Yousuke turns the camera on Tatsuto and asks him to be a model.

(Source: shinkeikaku)
