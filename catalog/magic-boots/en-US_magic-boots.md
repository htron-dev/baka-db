## Sinopse

What do you get when you mix together a wacky wizard, a girl who loves fairy tales, and a pair of enchanted footwear? Anything can happen to those who wear these magical boots which have "unlimited power", and things get crazy (crazily funny) when love goes awry in this cute, two-chapter story by Wujian and Wulin.
