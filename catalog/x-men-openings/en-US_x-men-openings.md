## Sinopse

For the TV Tokyo dub of the series, the intro was replaced with a new, Japanese-animated sequence as well as a new theme called "Rising", by the band Ambience. Starting with episode 42, a second new intro was used, featuring the song "Dakishimetai Dare yori mo".
