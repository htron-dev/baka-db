## Sinopse

Mameko Endou is 14 years old, loves fashion and lives in a village dreaming of becoming a model in Tokyo. A classmate from her school who's in love with her, calls to leave together but Mameko, uncertain of her feelings and embarrassed, ends up ruining their first appointment. Mameko's beauty is not good enough for a model, which gives her little hope. However, after being in Tokyo and claiming to know the two beautiful models Miri and Hikaru, she decided to become a stylist! Her passion for fashion grows even more, and all her "inspiration" becomes hostile visions and are often bizarre...
