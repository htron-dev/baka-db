## Sinopse

Kou is the number one actor in the gay adult video industry, and he has a secret longing for his cameraman, Shuuzou, who's like a partner to him. Kou's gay and Shuuzou's straight. That is a huge frustrating barrier for Kou and his feelings for Shuuzou keep welling up inside him. This eventually leads Kou to fantasize about Shuuzou as he's being filmed through Shuuzou's camera...
