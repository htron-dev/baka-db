## Sinopse

Nao is a girl who wants to become a shoujo mangaka and Kento is a boy famous for being a player. Though Nao believes Kento is a bit irritating she needs to experience love and she wants to improve her manga skills as well. However she knows about love and drawing only from the books she read and she has still to experience both.
