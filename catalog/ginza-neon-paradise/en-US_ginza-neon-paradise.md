## Sinopse

In the Showa era, Aoi longs to see his childhood friend, feeling incomplete and wondering if he is still alive. Suddenly, his friend Takahiko appears before him. Aoi's feelings soon turn to rage, questioning why his friend hadn't given him any signs that he was alive. Aoi's pain becomes difficult to bear.
