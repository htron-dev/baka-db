## Sinopse

Hagiwara sensei works as a lawyer in a company which is used to cover the mobs true business nature led by the leader, Takagi. Hagiwara doesn't really like his working environment but yet he is attracted to Takagi who has taken a liking of the lawyer. Takagi loves Hagiwara sensei very much but he is not sure if he could ever return his love ...
