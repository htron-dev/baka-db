## Sinopse

Etou Matori moves into her new apartment and starts calling her neighbor Tatsumi her 'brother' because they share the same last name. She goes on to call Tatsumi's childhood friend: Inui Minami her 'sister.' This series follows the everyday antics of these three 'siblings' and their friends.
