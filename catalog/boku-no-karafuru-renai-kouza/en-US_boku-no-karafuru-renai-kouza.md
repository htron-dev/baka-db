## Sinopse

Not only is Asou Madoka now attending the same art university as Yukari-san, but he also has the chance to work closely with him! Will his hitherto unrequited love finally have the chance to bloom? Or will Madoka's chance for bliss with Yukari-san be snatched away by Hibiki, the man of uncommon good luck?
