## Sinopse

14-year-old Ritsuka Sawada enters a talent competition and is scouted to enter Kyrian D Ballet Company. Ritsuka hasn't a clue about ballet. She refuses, she has to meet her boyfriend. When she fails to meet him, she finds the ballet company's card the scout gave her and decides to take a look. She ends up finding her missing boyfriend at the company and decides to stay because of him. Slowly Ritsuka starts getting more and more interested in ballet and to become good and unique at it, getting the admiration and envy of her colleagues.
