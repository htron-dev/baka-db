## Sinopse

The story takes place after the events in Crows. It focuses on the soon-to-be 6th TFOA Generation leader Tesshou (depicted in Worst) and his conflict with two Suzuran students (in the first chapter) and on Hanaki Guriko's entering Suzuran and becoming 'the strongest'.
