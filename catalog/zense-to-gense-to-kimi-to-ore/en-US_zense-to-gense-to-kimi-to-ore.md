## Sinopse

He stopped in a local coffee shop to get out of the rain. When high-schooler Takashi met the beautiful shopkeeper Isao, it was love at first sight. When one of the regular customers, a fortune teller, comes in, Takashi gets a fortune from his previous life and a time skip back to Ancient Egypt! What awaits him there is a love triangle that transcends time and space. (Source: MangaUpdates)
