## Sinopse

Princess Tsubaki, who loves romantic stories, is told by her father, the king, to choose a guy to date at her 16th birthday party at all costs. Troubled by this, princess Tsubaki asked a favor of Aoi, a boy who's treated like a Cinderella...!? Includes a one-shot called Quartet Cards.
