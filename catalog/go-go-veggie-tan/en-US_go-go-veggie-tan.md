## Sinopse

A fusion of drawn animation, stop-motion animation, and live-action sequences. Go! Go! Veggie-tan tells a quirky tale of a tomato and eggplant that fell to Earth from their spaceship after a meteorite hit them and they became enchanted by local playground equipment.
