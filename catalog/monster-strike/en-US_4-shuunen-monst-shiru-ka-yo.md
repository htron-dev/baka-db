## Sinopse

A 4th Anniversary Monster Strike TV commercial featuring Egashira 2:50 (pronounced Egashira Nijigojuppun), a Japanese comedian. The first commercial in the promotion was originally released with the live actor. It was then fully rotoscoped and released again with some changes as an animated version.
