## Sinopse

An award winning surreal stop motion anime from Korea using noodles, about a little Bologee (a so-called convex boy) who was born on the Concave Planet. He desperately wants to get to a another planet called Convex Star.

When he finally manages to get there he falls in love with a girl from the Convex Planet. The people living there do not approve......
