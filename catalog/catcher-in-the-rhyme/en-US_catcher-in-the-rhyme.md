## Sinopse

Satsuki Takatsuji wants a new start in high school, but she has trouble speaking and doesn't have the talents to join any clubs. Just when it seems like her new life is over, she meets two girls who are starting a rap battle club and is blown away by how easily they're able to express themselves.
