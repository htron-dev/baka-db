## Sinopse

Juuzaburou Kujou, a genius surgeon to hold a nickname of one the oldest Egyptian divinity, "Horus," the falcon god, known for his power of healing. Despite an unfavorable context of work and problems with his hierarchy, Juuzaburou has only one goal: save the life of his patients! In spite of the fact that he is pretentious, arrogant, proud and a seducer, his surgeon's skills are undeniable.
