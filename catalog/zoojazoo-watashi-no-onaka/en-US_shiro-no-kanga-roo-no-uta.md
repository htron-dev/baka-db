## Sinopse

A musical web short by Asami Ike made in honor of Roo-kun, an albino kangaroo born this year at the Kanazawa Zoo. Two versions of the video were posted. One in a normal horizontal video streaming format, and a second in a vertical streaming format meant to accommodate phone users.
