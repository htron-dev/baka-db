## Sinopse

A marvelous actor who has stage fright plays various roles in real life!

The agency Catherine is the company which sends so-called extras in real life. Yaegashi Taiga, a marvelous actor that has stage fright, tries his best to fulfill the requests he is given by this company. One day, he is told to work on a request with the newbie Amae Shizuku... But Shizuku has a secret and...?!

Please come to our office if anything troubles you! The excellent extras will support you throughout your daily life.
