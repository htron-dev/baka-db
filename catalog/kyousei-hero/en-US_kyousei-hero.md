## Sinopse

Mitsuru Aota, an average Japanese worker, finds himself implicated in testing the government's new legal system. Along with five other randomly selected citizens, they have to apprehend and judge criminals caught red-handed, with the help of the police and an high-tech suit designed especially for this task.
