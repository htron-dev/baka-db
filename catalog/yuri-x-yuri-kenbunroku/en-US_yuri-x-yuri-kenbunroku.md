## Authors

## Sinopse

An ongoing report comic.

An English-language version of chapter 1, a report on Yuricon in Tokyo, can be found in ALC Publishing's Yuri Monogatari 3.
Chapter 2 is a report on the First Love Sisters drama CD recording.
Chapter 3 focuses on the indoor yuri life.
In chapter 4, Morishima visits several yuri-related cafes.
Chapter 5 is about cultural festivals at girls' schools.
Chapter 6 is about wrestling.
