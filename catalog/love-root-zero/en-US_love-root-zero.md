## Sinopse

Just when Yaeko has to reject the love confession of Kazuya-kun, a popular boy in her class, there is a strange earthquake, and the pair is swept away to a surreal alternate dimension where they encounter all kinds of mysterious and dangerous things. Meanwhile, Yaeko's boyfriend, Kouichi, is getting desperate because Yaeko is missing, and she means everything to him...
