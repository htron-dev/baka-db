## Sinopse

Tsumugi-chan is excited because she is finally going to attend the beloved high school her mother graduated from. But as soon as she arrives, she encounters the very popular Ibara cousins, Chitose and Hajime. They are also the class co-vice presidents. In a strange turn of events, Tsumugi-chan suddenly finds herself nominated to be class president on her very first day!
