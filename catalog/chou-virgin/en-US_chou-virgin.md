## Sinopse

Ippei has a trouble with women. He usually throws up or becomes sick around them. Against his dad's advice to go to an all boys school, he decides to go to a coed highschool instead. His dad allows him on the conditions that if he fails to pass the test, that he will dress up as a female and work as a waitress. During the test, he was allocated beside a female and subsequently fails. Now Ippei has to dress as an woman and work in his dad's shop as a waitress.

While he's serving people's food, a cute girl runs in crying. Ippei feels sorry for her so he give the girl more food than the other and tries to comfort her. While the girl thanks him, to his surprise, Ippei found out he has no problem being sick talking with the girl now. He likes that girl, bit now she think he's a woman. What Would he do? He does really hate dressing up as a girl, but in order to talk with the girls, dressing up as a girl is his only choice right now.
