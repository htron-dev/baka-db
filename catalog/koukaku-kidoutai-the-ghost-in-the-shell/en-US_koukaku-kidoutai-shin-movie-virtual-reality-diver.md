## Sinopse

Animation studio Production I.G announced on Monday that it will enter the virtual reality (VR) content market with an app titled Koukaku Kidoutai Shin Gekijou-ban Virtual Reality Diver (Ghost in the Shell: The New Movie Virtual Reality Diver).

The app will offer an original story in 360° 3D video, which can be seen with a VR headset. A three-minute promotional video will be available worldwide for the Oculus Rift on Google Play and App Store, followed by the full work in Japanese and English.

(Source: ANN)
