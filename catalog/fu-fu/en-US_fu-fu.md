## Sinopse

In an MMPORG, Furika, a bunny girl, and Fuyuta, a bird man, meet, come to like each other, and get married in-world. It turns out that in real life they are both high school girls, in the same grade, at the same school. They soon become fast friends outside of the game, or maybe something more.
