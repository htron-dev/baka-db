## Sinopse

Minato is a third year middle schooler with a girly face. He came out of the closet in front of his family and told them that he is gay... Since then, his mother started bringing him cute/girly things which disappointed Minato and hurt his feelings.

Living near the beach, his family runs a bread and breakfast where he meets a new guest: Naoki Oisaka, who he started feeling comfortable with...
