## Sinopse

Normal face, normal style, bad at sport, bad grades. The middle of middle (no, lower!?) girl, Kotoha, is a childhood friend of Kanade who is handsome and smart. Secretly Kotoha loves Kanade, but Kanade always act cold towards her... Kotoha wants to be able to be friends again with him by calling him "Kana-chan" like in the past, but he got angry because of that... Then, she got close to the class playboy Haruta...?

(Source: Shogakukan, translated)
