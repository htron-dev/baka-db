## Sinopse

Kosaka is an average girl, but one day she happens to see Tomine being bullied. Afterward, she witnesses a strange creature emerge from Tomine, and Tomine only gives Kosaka a cryptic answer when she asks what the creature is. Despite Tomine saying that Kosaka shouldn't get involved, Kosaka ends up helping Tomine.

(Source: ANN)
