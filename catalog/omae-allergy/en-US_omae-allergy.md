## Sinopse

Kanou Hiroto, a high school student, experienced sexual harassment when he was a child which led him to be allergic to men. Due to this, he isolated himself from guys until he met Oushiro Kei, who approached him without knowing about his allergy to men. Ultimately, Kanou struggles as Oushiro relentlessly pursues after him. Will Kanou accept this twist of fate to his life?
