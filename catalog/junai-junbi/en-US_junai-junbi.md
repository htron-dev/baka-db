## Sinopse

Saichi devotes himself to attending marriage hunting parties every day to please his grandmother. However, for some reason he was targeted by a shady, tall handsome guy named Kazuomi, who Saichi met at the venue! While being forcibly taken on a date, Saichi noticed that he was enjoying himself. Saichi, who has zero romantic experience, will now experience his first kiss and beyond...!?
