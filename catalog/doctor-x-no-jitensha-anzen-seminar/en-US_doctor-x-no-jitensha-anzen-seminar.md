## Sinopse

Satoru, Akira and Ichiro are riding bicycles, weaving between people walking when they nearly collide with a car at an intersection. They are saved by Doctor X who sends them a letter with alternative future photographs. They are pictures of the accident, showing the outcome of their lives had he not been there.
