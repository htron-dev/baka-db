## Sinopse

The end of 1943. Shirakawa, a fighter pilot who fought in various parts of the world, finally returned to Tokyo. He has just been transferred to the Imperial Army Air Test Center. After a long absence, Shirakawa tries to resume his family life with his wife Mariko.

(Source: Éditions Delcourt, translated)
