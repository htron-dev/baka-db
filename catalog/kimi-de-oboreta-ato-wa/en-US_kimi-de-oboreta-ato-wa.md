## Sinopse

A series of oneshots of boys falling in love.

1. After Indulging With You / After I Get Drunk On You (Kimide Oboreta
   Atoha) Quiet student attends drinking parties to see flirty student he has a crush on. But after drinking too much he ends up back at flirty students place and wanders into the shower with him...

2. After I Get Drunk on You, Next Deep Drink
   The couple from the first story return, Quiet student is feeling insecure about his experience and whether or not he's pleasing his partner. Is it time to drink again?

3. The Other Side of the Glass Iori is the adopted son of a powerful family. He is sexually harassed by his adopted brother Yoshiyuki and must present a cold face to the world. Will Ariga be able to see past it?

4. Secret Work Room After Class Star student Hayase invites poor Koharu to his house so he can sketch him. In the nude. Probably totally innocent.

5. The Little Housekeeper's Daily Routine of Serving the Master (Maid-kun no Gohoushi na Hibi). Young butler is trying to serve junior master to the best of his abilities, but the young master only wants to sexually harass him!

6. Pet Grooming Catboys are roommates at a boarding school.
