## Sinopse

In order to be employed by a rich family, Narumi Inoue, hiding the fact that he is a man, attends a maids' school. For his weekend practice, he is sent to serve a mistress who's been hating maids way before they met!
Holding the "hospitable heart" that Japan is proud of in her chest, "Maid in Japan" named Narumi begins her fight!
