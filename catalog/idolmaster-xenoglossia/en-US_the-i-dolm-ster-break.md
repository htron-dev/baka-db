## Sinopse

The iDOLM@STER Break! focuses on Yuutarou Takagi, an average teen and the grandson of Junichirou Takagi, as he works as a temporary president and producer in 765 Production, after his grandfather is unable to work due to work-related stress. It details his daily life as president and producer, antics and comedic mishaps around the office, and his relationship with idols of 765PRO.
