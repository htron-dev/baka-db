1-2) Rainy Love
When Hayama Hikaru hears a mean rumor about his co-worker Nanashima Yukihito's "size", he wonders if it's true. But Nanashima turns out to be a sweet guy and they become friends. But does Hayama want more?

3. More than a Lover, Less than a Loved One
   Fujimi Kyou has only been at the company two weeks, but his boss already loves to bully him. Now that teasing has escalated into sexual harassment!

Second volume features a short story of Enomoto's work: Dangerous Magnum.
