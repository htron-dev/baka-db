## Sinopse

Moyuru Honoo enters a whole new world for him! His comic masterpiece was appointed to the big screen! Mayuru Honoo accepts all this with anticipation! In making this film, Honoo Moyuru even have fight with the director because their visions are different. What happened? Who will prevail? What is the fate of this film? This is the story of the ups and downs with the spirit of hilarious burning comic!
