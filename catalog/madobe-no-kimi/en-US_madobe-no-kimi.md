## Sinopse

A collection of eight oneshots (some related) by Kumota Haruko, all of which ran in Tokyo Mangasha's "Themed Catalogue" series under the Marble Comics line.

Includes:

1. Madobe no Kimi (Shokugyou Catalogue)
2. Good Bye, Honey (Salaryman Catalogue)
3. Lay Down, Sally -Oyasumi, Sally- (Bad End Catalogue)
4. Hajime Hiku Koi no Uta (Hajimete Catalogue)
5. BAD Boy Seventeen (Mibun Chigai Catalogue)
6. Anata ni wa Ienai -Can't Tell You- (Sankaku Kankei Catalogue)
7. Daitai Iro ni Tokeau (Seme x Seme Catalogue)
8. Tecchan to Yasu no Kyuujitsu (short omake)
