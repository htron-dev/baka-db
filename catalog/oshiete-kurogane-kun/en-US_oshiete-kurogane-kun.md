## Sinopse

When it comes to cooking, Haruka cannot do anything right. She develops a crush on Kurogane-kun who appears to be a nice, shy boy. As Haruka strives to get closer to Kurogane-kun she discovers that not only is he secretly a devil, but he is also known as the household prince!
