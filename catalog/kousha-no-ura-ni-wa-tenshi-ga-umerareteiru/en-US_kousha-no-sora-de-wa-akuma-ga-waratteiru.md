## Sinopse

Wataru Konno is the only male student at the prestigious St. Justine Girls High School, a boarding school built on a solitary island in the ocean. The reality of this school where daughters of rich and famous families gather is a cruel sanctuary where the "student council" dominates with pleasure and pain...
