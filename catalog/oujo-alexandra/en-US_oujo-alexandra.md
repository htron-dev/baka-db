## Sinopse

Lovely and sad story by the creator of Garasu no Kamen (Glass Mask).

What will happen to a lovely princess when she gets blind after an accident?

This story was first collected in 1978 in a manga volume with the same title. It contains Oujo Alexandra 1977, Fuyu no Himawari 1975, Manatsu no Yo no Yume 1972. It was afterwards reprinted for Suzue Miuchi's best works, volume 5 1996.
