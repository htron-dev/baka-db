## Sinopse

Story about a bored and apathetic photographer who leaves fame and the glamorous fashion world of tinsel-town Los Angeles for Sarajevo, hoping to find some meaning in his empty existence and pictures. There, he finally discovers love and beauty amongst the ruins of a war-torn country. (MangaUpdates)
