## Sinopse

4-panel gag manga strips based on the classic video game series, "Klonoa." Based on the first video game in the series.

Klonoa lives in Breezegale and suddenly wakes up after hearing a big crash in a distant area. He embarks towards the location to see what the loud noise was all about. Together with his companion, Huepow, which is also a ring spirit and works as a weapon for Klonoa, he gets ready to save the world from evil.
