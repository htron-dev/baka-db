## Sinopse

One day, Tanaka stumbles upon a brand new ramen store, which he enters to find out that it is run by a cat named Taishou who dreams of running his own line of ramen. Forced to come back everyday in a strange way, Tanaka and Taishou have some weird adventures in a ramen store.
